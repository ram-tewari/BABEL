[Chapter 1: The Awakening]
The chamber was silent except for the rhythmic hum of ancient machinery. Dim blue lights flickered across metallic walls, casting dancing shadows.
Kai (confused)
Where... where am I?
He tried to move, but his limbs felt heavy, as if weighted down by invisible chains. The last thing he remembered was the blinding flash of light.
Kai
This isn't right. The tower... it should have killed me. Why am I still breathing?
A holographic interface materialized before his eyes, lines of code scrolling faster than he could read.
[SYSTEM INITIALIZED]
[Welcome, Player. You have been granted the Cheat Skill: BABEL]
Kai (skeptical)
Babel? What kind of skill is that?
System Voice (mechanical)
BABEL allows you to understand and translate any language, code, or system. Reality itself is just another language to be decoded.
Kai
A translation skill? In a world where monsters and magic rule? This has to be some kind of joke.
The chamber door slid open with a pneumatic hiss. Beyond it, a corridor stretched into darkness, lit only by emergency strips along the floor.
Kai (determined)
Guess there's only one way to find out what this skill can really do.
Heavy footsteps echoed from somewhere in the darkness. Something was coming.
Unknown Voice (hostile)
Intruder detected. Initiating elimination protocol.
Kai's heart raced. Whatever this BABEL skill was, he'd need to figure it out fast—or this second chance at life would be very short indeed.
