Chapter 394:
Hill Capture (3)
From the third round, Screamer's team abandoned the spear-to-spear
tactic and employed a more advanced strategy. Pony, the winger, moved to the central line and ran
alongside Suavi.
'They've shifted. As expected, they won't go down easily.'
With three members in the central line, the left line was essentially
abandoned.
As expected, Luman had completely retreated to the hill, covering all
three lines.
When they entered the range of the Requiem of Death, Suavi's
concentration wavered. At that moment, Pony cast a spell.
Refresh.
A refreshing sensation washed over Suavi's mind, completely nullifying
the effects of the Requiem of Death.
Although it provided no physical defense, it was an invaluable spell
that blocked most crowd control effects.
Just having Refresh made it worth including a water-type magician in the
party.
"Hahaha! How about this? Can you still stop us?"
Equipped with the combat package, Screamer transformed back into a
warrior, sweeping through the battlefield.
Maya maintained her spell until the end. Although she couldn't cut off
Suavi, just tying down Pony's presence with Refresh was more than fulfilling her role as a guard.
'As expected, Suavi is the key. She was last year too... That girl is
really amazing.'
The duration of the 20 combat package buffs varied, with the vision
enhancement buff lasting 1.13 seconds and the muscle endurance enhancement buff lasting 1.78
seconds.
Initially, they were cast in order, but after about 20 cycles, the
shorter-duration buffs would cycle 1-2 more times, interrupting the sequence.
Moreover, calculating the time consumed by the rotation made it clear
that utility magicians required the highest concentration among magicians.
'Tik-tok-tik-tak. Tok. Tak-tik-tik. Tak-tik-tik.'
Therefore, utility magicians used a unique technique called the cross
force, different from the usual force.
They set up a cross-shaped intersection, placed the core in the center,
and collided the reserved cores from four directions towards the center.
The reason they could rotate the 20 buff cycles at precise times was due
to the unique rhythm of 'tik-tok-tak-tik.'
When struck vertically, it was 'tik,' horizontally, it was 'tok,' and
when bounced again in the same direction, it was 'tak,' feeling the 0.something second error through
rhythm.
'Tik-tik tak-tik-tok tik-ka-tik-tok tik-tik tak-tik-tok.'
Bang. Bang-bang-bang. Bang-bang. Bang-bang-bang-bang.
Iruki closely observed the effects bursting in a unique rhythm above
Screamer's head.
'0.11 seconds. 0.23 seconds. 0.38 seconds. 0.49 seconds...'
The cycle was as precise as a machine. The speed at which the cores were
switched was also commendable.
'I'll cut it here.'
But that was just a phenomenon occurring within Iruki's servant
calculations.
Cancellation.
As Screamer's muscle enhancement buff was canceled, Suavi, maintaining
the tik-tok-tak-tik, frowned.
When the vision enhancement and flexibility enhancement buffs also
disappeared, the rhythm of the cross force began to tangle.
'Even for a servant, this speed is unfair.'
Cancellation was a difficult spell because it required perfectly
reversing the vector quantity of the magic.
But that was a separate issue from reading the cores passing by at a
speed of 4 times per second.
In short, Iruki's thinking speed was faster than Suavi's, even while
analyzing the vector quantities of the cores and omnipotence without any error.
"What the hell, Suavi!"
Screamer shouted, looking back. She had never made a mistake in the
force before, so the current situation was unacceptable.
"Where are you looking during a battle?"
Screamer hurriedly turned his head. Nade's hands pushed forward, and
Lightning Shock exploded.
"Ugh! You bastard!"
Screamer's body was stunned.
"What? The sound of a pig being slaughtered?"
Nade raised his fist in place of Shirone.
"Why don't you cry like a pig being slaughtered!"
Screamer's head twisted to the side as Nade's punch landed squarely on
his jaw.
Meanwhile, in Screamer's team's territory, Luman was desperately holding
off Shirone and Amy.
Using various crowd control techniques like Blind, Slow Trap, and Air
Press, he was buying time, but the forward-linker combination was incredibly annoying.
"Damn it! That cursed Hongan!"
Amy's Hongan was the nemesis of crowd control.
No matter what crowd control spell was used, her self-image memory reset
her mind, making it impossible to control her.
'No choice. I'll cut Shirone.'
Shirone's endurance was formidable, but he was more likely to be
affected by crowd control than Hongan.
'He's more popular than me despite being uglier.'
Luman acted on personal feelings. He believed he was the best.
Thinking he would be handsome if he lost weight, Shirone was a rival
worth eliminating.
"He must have kissed Amy too!"
"What are you talking about, you idiot!"
Luman's Mountain Range exploded, creating a massive 10-meter-high wall
that separated the two.
'Amy is out. Now, Air Press!'
As the powerful atmospheric pressure pressed down, Shirone's movements
stopped. Seizing the opportunity, Luman kicked off the ground and flew at him.
Amy, who had circled around the wall, widened her eyes.
Luman's belly protruded as he bent his back like a bow, exposing his
navel. His molars were clenched tightly, and he was grimacing with all his might.
"Shirone! Dodge!"
It was clear that Luman was using his signature move, Spirit
Hammer.
A non-attribute anti-magic crowd control spell that caused a stun by
colliding Spirit Zones.
Often compared to a headbutt technique, Spirit Hammer was possible
because the target also absorbed the impact energy.
'I'll crush him, Shirone or not!'
Luman intended to strike with all his might.
As proven in the survival test, Shirone's endurance was the strongest in
the graduating class. But the harder Spirit Hammer struck, the greater the impact on the target.
With the pride of a crowd control magician and the pride of being the
most handsome in the graduating class, he aimed to destroy Shirone in one blow.
"Here I come!"
Seeing the menacing Luman, Shirone instinctively prepared a defensive
mental state. As his Spirit Zone switched to defense mode, Luman swung his forehead, bouncing his
belly.
Clang!
"Ugh!"
Shirone grimaced and stepped back.
Although he wasn't actually hit on the head, it felt like someone had
struck his skull with a hammer.
"Shirone! Are you okay?"
Amy hurried over. Even from a distance, it was clear that the Spirit
Hammer had hit properly.
'But why isn't there a follow-up attack?'
When Amy turned her head, Luman was standing, panting. Suddenly, his
nose bled, and his heavy body wobbled before he fell on his butt.
Luman's eyes were completely unfocused as he sat on the ground,
supporting himself with his arms.
Although anti-magic spells were inherently a seesaw game, Spirit Hammer
was a spell where the attacker always had the advantage.
Yet, the shock from casting Spirit Hammer had blown his mind.
Was this what it felt like to hit one's head on a diamond?
'Damn it! What am I supposed to do if he's this tough?'
Having been stunned by his own attack, he couldn't even muster the
strength to stand. It seemed his mental strength would only recover by the end of the third
round.
"Now! Let's go!"
Shirone ran past Luman and up the hill.
Amy, checking their own territory, saw Nade land a punch that twisted
Screamer's jaw and followed behind.
Aider struggled, firing his air gun, but had to retreat outside the
range of Shirone's Radiance.
In the meantime, Amy pulled out the flag.
-The third round ends. The fourth round will start in 5 minutes.
Everyone's movements stopped, and the tension eased.
But Screamer's eyes still glinted with murderous intent.
Although he had mellowed considerably since entering the magic school,
the humiliation of being punched by a magician was unbearable.
"Are you crazy? How dare you hit me?"
Nade scoffed and replied.
"Why? Do you think you're something special? If you did something
to deserve a punch, you get punched. Why get mad about that?"
Screamer suddenly closed the distance and got in Nade's face.
"Yeah? Then how about we settle this outside? If you're a magician,
just use magic. Don't think about throwing punches in front of me."
Nade closed his mouth.
A fight with clear winners and losers didn't excite him. If it were a
fight where one had to die, could he provoke so easily then?
"Hmph! Coward!"
As Screamer walked away, Nade turned to his friends with a bright
expression as if nothing had happened.
"Alright! We're turning this around! Let's keep it up!"
Shirone asked.
"What happened? What did Screamer say?"
"Huh? I don't know, I forgot. Anyway, Maya! How did I
do?"
Maya realized why Nade had punched Screamer. As the one who had dragged
her into the fight, he had hit him on her behalf.
"Yeah! It felt great! Thanks, Nade."
She must have felt guilty about keeping her distance recently.
Although it was because of Shirone that she had been cold, she felt
sorry and grateful that they had thought that way.
"It's a good thing we have Maya. Even as a guard, she's debuffing
the enemies, making them confused."
"Hehe! I'll do my best in the next round too!"
Maya's face blushed, and Nade and Iruki exchanged glances. She was
completely different from a few days ago.
'Shirone praised me...'
Maya no longer wanted to hide her feelings.
There had been opportunities to stop, but she willingly jumped into the
fray. So, shouldn't she at least enjoy these small moments of happiness?
Meanwhile, Screamer frowned as he looked at Luman, who was sitting like
a defeated soldier.
He had used Spirit Hammer on Shirone and ended up like this. No matter
how confident he was, it was a ridiculous decision.
"Why did you do that? You know Shirone's endurance."
"Then should I have used it on Hongan? That's like pouring water
into a bottomless pit. At least Shirone can be broken. Ugh, but that guy really has a hard head. My head
is killing me."
Screamer sighed.
"Anyway, how are you? Can you recover?"
Pony spoke up.
"I've cast Refresh. But I think I need to take it easy until the
next round."
Luman looked at Pony and said.
"Don't just stand there, massage my head. It still
hurts."
A vein popped on Pony's temple.
How dare he ask a royal to give him a massage?
Especially since Luman was the man she disliked the most in the
graduating class, aside from Fermi's group.
'I can't ruin the teamwork. I don't want to hear that we lost because of
me.'
With the patience of someone holding back the urge to kill three times,
Pony massaged Luman's head from behind.
Every time her slender fingers pressed into his head, a thick groan
escaped Luman's mouth.
"Ah, that feels good. By the way, Screamer, you'll introduce me to
a pretty girl after this match, right?"
Pony's fingers pressed harder. She couldn't understand Luman's mindset
of using a life-changing evaluation for personal gain.
But that was the world of magic.
Luman knew his personality. If you had skills, no matter how eccentric
you were, you were recognized as a magician.
"To be precise, if we win. So recover as quickly as possible. It
would be embarrassing if we lost."
"Hmph! I'll just recharge this round. Then we'll go straight to 5
points and finish it."
At the moment, Luman's strategy was the most realistic. Besides,
Shirone's team would likely take it easy this round because of Maya.
The guard position had no other tasks besides defense, but Maya had been
continuously casting spells for three rounds to hinder Suavi.
'With her basic skills lacking, she must be exhausted.'
Screamer made his decision.
"Alright, let's buy some time. But that doesn't mean we're giving
up the round. If an opportunity arises, we'll strike immediately."
