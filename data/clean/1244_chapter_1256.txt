In an unknown forest—
"Grrrraaagh!"
A burning skeletal knight pierced through the veil of the Under World
and crossed into reality.
In his right hand, now stripped down to bare bone, he gripped a massive
greatsword that remained unscathed by the flames.
"Kiieeee!"
Like a monarch of the damned returning from Hell, he slammed the sword
into the ground, his hollow sockets staring ahead.
With a divine transcendental force, the earth trembled beneath him. His
body gradually began to regenerate, and the flames slowly died down.
"Ughhh…"
As his skin regenerated, Rian shouted,
"Hot! Damn, that's hot!"
He panted, brushing off the remaining embers with both hands, steam
still rising from his scorched body.
"Huff… huff…"
The flames of Gehenna had finally cooled. But the fire he had set upon
himself before entering the Under World had incinerated every last piece of his clothing.
"I made it back?"

middle of a forest in some unknown nation, completely lost.
"Well… Seongeum'll probably come get me."
He didn't know the exact principles, but her mastery of spatial magic
was second to none.
Lethe appeared out of the air.
"You're a monster, too, you know."

body and the greatsword embedded in the earth.
"Escaping while purifying the flames of Gehenna… No wonder Yahweh trusts

"Well… I'm alright, I guess."
"You're more than that. Enduring Gehenna's fire means you've overcome

"But you went beyond that and returned to the physical world. Your
divine transcendence… may actually rival that of a god."
Lethe truly believed it.
The Martial God, Yaksha.
At this point, there was likely no human alive who could defeat the Rian
that had returned from Hell.

"Maybe… I guess I've gotten stronger. But I can't say for sure until I

"It's a mental domain. Until you meet someone who truly pushes you to

"How long do I have?"
"I can't say exactly. But don't you have a sense of it?"
His feeling was— Not long.
"It's fine."

rest didn't matter.
"And also…"

'Sheesh, so talkative.'

Can I win?

like an enormous wall.
Lethe stared at the deeply serious Rian, dumbfounded.
'What kind of guy is this?'
