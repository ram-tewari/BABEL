Imperial City of Aganos.
The goblin's attire sharply contrasted with the corridor's decoration of
gold and treasures.
Clad in a ragged cloak and carrying a crude spear on his back, the
monster's name was Kido.
He was one of the few royal guards who could deliver food directly to
Wuorin's room.
"Stop."

"What? You scared me."
The leader of the squad asked in a sinister voice.
"…Where are you going?"
"Can't you see? That woman asked for some honey water."
The Wind Guards, staring intently at the golden tray Kido was holding,

"Don't get cocky just because the Empress dotes on you. At the end
of the day, you're just a pet."
Like most of his close associates, for the Wind Guards , the sole ruler
of Kashan was Terra.
These masters of the sword, who embodied fluid dynamics in their bodies,
protected Terraje up close.
However, since Kido entered the Imperial Palace, that pride had been
shattered.
'Just a goblin.'
From what he observed from afar, Wuorin didn't truly value Kido

She treated him like a servant, sometimes angrily, and occasionally took

"If I'm a pet, what are you guys? Cattle? Move aside. If I'm late,

"I just had a good idea."

"Let's neuter you. Then the Empress can play with you much more

The Wind Guards was serious.
Kido's eyes turned cold, but his hands still clutched the tray.

The leader of the Wind Guards, Yula.

"What, you were here?"
Wuorin, who had finished grooming herself, was peering outside from a

"Kido."
She turned her head with a smile, her appearance shining brighter than

'She's really beautiful.'
Although goblin aesthetic standards differ from humans, to his eyes,

'Maybe because she's eaten many people. Human memories have seeped into

All the desires to laugh together, walk together, talk about the book

Kido is a goblin.

Hiding her face to contain her anger, Wuorin held her belly and

"Hahaha! Look at that face."

"If you need a pet, find someone else. I'm quitting. I'm leaving

Was it because he met the Wind Guards?
'No, this is my problem.'
He couldn't bear the miserable sight of himself the more he stayed by

"Huh? Are you really going? Kido, Kido!"

Whether he could really leave Kashan was uncertain, but at least he
