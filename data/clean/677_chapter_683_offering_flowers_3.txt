Chapter 683:
Offering Flowers (3)
'The rain is getting heavier.'
Shirone spread his wings and raced toward the Living Flower at full speed.
All the concealment devices had stopped functioning, rendering their formidable anti-air
defenses useless.
But that only heightened his sense of crisis.
'Something's off.'
The state of the Living Flower, as perceived through the Ultima System, was like the calm
before a storm.
Though it seemed peacefully still, the towering iron structure was teeming with countless
energies darting around like nerves, forming its shape.
'Full power operation. They're plotting something.'
Following the Living Flower's input and output mechanisms, it was clear that a powerful
energy beam was primed.
'Don't tell me… the capital?'
It was entirely possible, and this was precisely why Lupist had risked everything to get
as close to the Living Flower as possible.
"Shirone oppa."
Mei Lei, who had caught up to Shirone via teleportation, flew alongside him, her body
drenched.
"You're safe. That's a relief."
Shirone, who had grown somewhat familiar with her, spoke casually, and Mei Lei smiled
shyly.
Though she was a mage, she wasn't classified as a combatant, lacking the exceptional
skills for it.
Had she not met her comrades, she might already be dead.
"I got lucky. I landed in an area without enemies."
"What about the signal?"
Mei Lei covered one ear and replied.
"Nothing's coming through. I've been checking, but there's not even the usual noise. This
is definitely strange."
La Enemi was no longer issuing commands.
'Is the design already complete? Or…?'
As Shirone pondered, two figures approached, leaping over buildings.
"Rian. Huh?"
His gaze lingered on the goblin following behind.
'A goblin with glasses? First time seeing that.'
When Shirone and Mei Lei stopped, Kido landed nimbly on the rooftop with a flip.
"Whew, finally meeting you."
Striking a pose, Kido adjusted his glasses and grinned.
"Uh, who are you?"
The fact that he came with Rian was enough to confirm he wasn't an enemy.
"The leader of the Speedkillers."
When Shirone showed no reaction, Kido awkwardly continued.
"…or rather, the former leader. Kido, the goblin."
"What's going on?"
Shirone turned to Rian, who stepped forward to explain.
Having already guessed La Enemi's intentions through his conversation with Etela, Shirone
quickly understood.
"I see. Corresponding to taste…"
"That's the idea. This one is hearing?"
Kido pointed, and Mei Lei nodded.
"Hmm, so touch, hearing, and taste are gathered. But to track down La, we definitely need
smell."
"Shagal must already be inside the Living Flower. We're running out of time."
As Shirone turned to leave, Kido raised a hand.
"Wait. Before that, I have a proposal."
Everyone looked at Kido.
"What proposal?"
"If it's okay with you, can I eat you guys?"
If he could gain information by eating, it'd be good to know Shirone and Mei Lei's
memories too.
"I don't mind, but…"
Shirone glanced at Mei Lei.
For Shirone, Armand could heal him even if flesh was eaten, but she had no means of
recovery.
Mei Lei touched her right ear.
"Maybe cutting off an ear would be best? Other body parts would affect combat ability.
Losing an ear might even enhance the function of the God's Frequency."
Kido stared blankly, but Shirone vehemently opposed.
This had already been discussed during the Silver Ring Guild's tests.
"That's a last resort. If we can't catch La Enemi with one sense, communication functions
are still important."
Kido smiled.
"No need to worry. Body fluids are enough; it doesn't have to be a body part. Plus,
unless the memory is imprinted on a specific organ, the information transferred is limited no matter what
you eat."
Rian looked slightly resentful.
"So you don't need to eat much."
"Hehe, that's the advantage of my ability."
Goblins are a gluttonous race, but the amount they can objectively eat is less than
humans.
"Fine, then have my blood."
Shirone sliced his left palm and let the blood drip.
Due to their height difference, Kido only needed to tilt his head up to drink.
"Mmm. Hmmm."
As Kido rolled his tongue, savoring the blood, his eyes deepened.
"I see. You're a very interesting human, aren't you?"
"You're not so ordinary yourself, huh?"
"Kekekeke! You'll be shocked if I show you more."
Kido, his lungs heaving, turned to Mei Lei.
"Goblins lack manners, but I'd rather not harm a woman. If you don't want your flesh cut,
saliva will do."
Mei Lei, expressionless, extended her wrist.
"Blood is fine."
Though disappointment flickered across his face, Kido quickly adopted a playful
expression.
"Then I won't hold back."
His sharp fangs pierced her wrist, and as his lips suctioned, her blood flowed into him.
His eyelids fluttered as specific memories transferred.
'Huh? What's this?'
While Shirone had lived an eventful life, Mei Lei's memories were on a completely
different level.
'What? What kind of memories are these?'
"How long are you going to suck? It hurts."
"Huh? Oh, sorry."
Kido hastily pulled away and blinked at Mei Lei.
"What's wrong?"
Her innocent expression revealed nothing.
'Does she not even know herself?'
