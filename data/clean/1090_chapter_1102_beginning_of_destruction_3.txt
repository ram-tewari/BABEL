Beginning of Destruction (3)
A petite woman with a hood arrived at the square overlooking the
Vatican.
Candler Eden.
Named after an ancient city, she was a Yora who practiced love.

'That's not it.' Slowly lowering her gaze, she passed the entrance of

'Underground.'

concentration.
'My feeling was right. But why is such an aura lurking in the
Vatican?'
After hearing Havitz's inner voice through Nemesis, Eden had been

One day, while chasing demons with Dante, she heard a strange
rumor.
'Satanism.'
The founder was Giyorgi, a demon surprisingly formed from Yahweh's
discarded emotions.
After parting ways with Dante, she investigated Satanism, and the trail
of evil led her here.
"The Vatican."
The Lami faith upholds the will of good.
Holy Knights made significant contributions in the war against demons,
and priests cared for the needy.
Leaving countless questions behind, Eden pulled her hood back on.
'I will confirm it with my own eyes.'
She would destroy Satanism, the spiritual foundation of Satan, and

the Vatican.
'Protect me.'

'What are you planning?'

'The Yora faith doesn't expand its organization, and the Teraphos faith

Only the Lami faith remained steadfast in these turbulent times.
'This is proof that Cria created the universe. But since when…'

'We should have nipped it in the bud from the start.'
Once the leading nation of the world was decided at this Holy War, the
Pope of the Lami faith would certify it.

At the cusp of this unprecedented achievement, Yahweh had come to the
Vatican.
"Once again, please behave reverently before the Pope. If you speak

"Hmph! Anyway, those Holy Knights…"

"I understand. It will be a short conversation. If I get the answer

The head priest grew even more anxious.
'What answer does he seek?'

"Pope, you have visitors."

Constantine.
Shirone entered confidently, with a wary Iruki and a tense Neide

"Hmm."

"There's no need for thanks. Compared to what you've done, it's

'He doesn't seem to crave honor. Well… he was never like that even in

He knew about Constantine's younger days through Omega.
'The Pope of Strength.'
Constantine's divine power was among the strongest in the
Vatican.
'He's not someone who would be swayed by evil. So why do I feel…'
The smell of Satan here?

"You wanted to see me before the Holy War. Do I not meet your

"I have no right to judge others. I'm just curious about

"Is that so? A bit disappointing."
Having lived his life without shame, Shirone's answer felt

"Alright, ask anything. As you know, I must leave for the Holy War

"What are you hiding?"

"Hmm. What am I hiding? From you? Let's see… what could I be

"When I was a child, I broke a friend's toy and didn't confess. Ah,

"The martyr code. I heard that Lami faith's Holy Knights are

"Yahweh, I tried to understand you. But why do you question our

You remember breaking a friend's toy clearly, but why don't you mention this?"

"The Lami faith has fought against evil. The Holy Knights are at

"Then I can't accept the Lami faith."

'Amazing.'
