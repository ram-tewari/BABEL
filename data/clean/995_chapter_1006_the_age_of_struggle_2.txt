The Age of Struggle (2)
Seina felt her face flush.
The Paladin of the Oracle Management Department holds the most noble
mission in the Lami Church.
Such a person succumbed to evil, swayed by mere human emotions, and
could not bear to face Shirone.
'I have no right to blame.'
If it weren't for Shirone's baptism, she would have also ended her life
by stabbing herself.

highest-ranking Paladin of the Papal Office is..."

"Shiyok is deceiving people. It's a problem that even the Lami

It was the most human evil.

'It's a pity. If she were alive, she could have testified. We need to

With that thought, she looked around, but no one approached the
paladin.
'Foolish ones.'
She was angry at those who judged only by the immediate outcome, but

"We will leave the villagers behind," Seina said, mounting her

The most secretive department within the Lami Church.
'And the most dangerous place as well.'
If the Oracle Management Department moves with faith, the Heresy
Management Department moves with hatred.
In a way, just mentioning the existence of such a department was an act
of goodwill.
"Let's go."
Shirone did not avert his gaze until Seina and the paladins left the
village.
'We fight evil, but our beliefs differ.' As Shirone's philanthropy
spread to the world, the number of conflicts would increase.
Nade asked, "Shirone, what about the survivors? We can't take them

Shirone checked Iruki's condition.
Although he was no longer bleeding, the aftermath of using Overdrive had
not yet subsided.
"Let's camp at the foot of the mountain today. I've told Mr. Lampa

'Arnold Lampa. If he's the best information mage in the Ivory Tower, he

It was crucial that the survivors who experienced Shirone's miracle
spread the word in the city.
'If we plant seeds like this, they will eventually bear fruit.
Maybe...'
He thought that the point of direct confrontation with the Lami Church
might come much sooner than expected.
In the southern city of Tormia, Gaspa, from the capital Baschka, the
demons spread out radially and continued to move south.
The kingdom's swift response prevented annihilation, but the aftermath
of emotional disease was more terrifying.
The streets overflowed with vagrants who had abandoned their homes, and
with law enforcement failing, crimes were incessant.
"It's serious."
In the eyes of those who had destroyed something precious, one could see
not just malice but even the intent to kill.
Shirone said, "The World Health Organization has established action
guidelines based on the cases collected for dealing with emotional disease. They are divided into 17
categories, with life-critical items placed later to control the threshold of emotional
disease."
"The Cross family received it too. I haven't seen it myself. How
long can one last if they follow the guidelines exactly?"
"A maximum of two months. The goal is to find a cure within that
time, but so far, there are no results."
Shirone's gaze turned towards the vagrants.
"The item about abandoning residences corresponds to category 8.
This is a guideline for at least four weeks later, but already there are many who have abandoned their
homes."
"Well, there is no rule that satisfies everyone."
Cayden, who hadn't left his house for a long time, felt a fresh
sentiment upon seeing the real world.
"Everyone is bearing their own pain. Maybe my worries are trivial
compared to theirs..."
Shirone asked, "Cayden, how did you manage to resolve your

"No different from others. My father endured by giving up one
precious thing after another. Heirlooms, property, distant relatives... The Cross family has declined to
the point of no return."
Cayden turned his head.
"But I guess I'm a bit different. The destiny of the Red Cross
extracts 100% of all talents except for what I truly desire. As long as I don't give up on Maya,
emotional disease won't be a problem."
Amy asked incredulously, "You don't get more
sensitive?"

Shirone asked, "Hardly ever means... you have been

"When I was very young. I was scared after hearing about exhaustion
disease from my grandmother. I prayed not to get sick, but the next day I caught a cold."
What you truly want does not come true.
"I was sick for about a year after that. I wanted to get better. My

Cayden shrugged.
"Of course, I grew up hearing about the destiny of the Red Cross.

Shirone understood.
"The mind is not something you can change easily."
"Right. Especially when you're young. Anyway, it wasn't serious. I

Shirone realized just how cruel the law of the Red Cross destiny

"If one has experienced such bizarre events all their life, the

Amy asked, "How about now? Because you love Maya, you can overcome

"Let me put it this way."
Determination filled Cayden's eyes.
"If I could escape this cursed fate, I wouldn't care about

No one could define Cayden's life.
"If you desperately want to sleep, you can't fall asleep. If you're

Cayden's emotional scale was twisted beyond any clear priorities.
"It might not just be a problem with his immune
system."

"Everyone ends up giving up many things they cherish. But truly

Cayden recognized her the moment he heard her.

Taking the money and giving a warning in advance.

Shirone intended to stay outside.
