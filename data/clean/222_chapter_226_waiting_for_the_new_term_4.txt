Where is Shirone? (1)
The second half of Alpheas Magic Academy has begun.
From the morning, the main gate was crowded with carriages carrying
students.
A bizarre scene was created where young students accompanied by their
parents were crying and begging because they did not want to go to academy.
On the other hand, the adolescent boys crossed the main gate with
indifferent expressions, as if they were embarrassed to even be attended to by the butler.
The entrance ceremony was scheduled for 11 a.m., so the students
returned to their accommodations and unpacked their bags.
Shirone, who arrived early in the morning, put the books he brought on
the bookshelf and laid out his clothes and daily necessities.
"Shirone!"
His friends burst through the door and came in.
"Nade! Iruki!"
Shirone, with a big smile, ran over to them. It had only been 30 days
since they last saw each other, but it felt like a year.
There were so many things happened. How would they react if they knew he
had been to Heaven?
They held each other's hands and shouted in unison.
"I have something amazing!"
Then they stared blankly at each other.
Though they didn't know what each other's stories were, they anticipated
that today would be too short to share them all.
