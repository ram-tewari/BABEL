Dream On (3)
"You should've pushed through! The more trials, the smaller the error!"
"With what ammo? Following your strategy, we'd have bet the house deeds next!"
"See! That's it! Nothing in the world can be consumed infinitely! Energy's the same!"
"Where did that come from?!"
"Hahaha!"
Shirone burst out laughing. Everyone chasing their dreams was the same. Success and
failure were secondary.
Even if the world pointed fingers, even if they mocked him as an idealist who couldn't
support a family—someone had to do it. That was how humanity had progressed this far.
'It's okay if it's not me. I don't mind… being fertilizer for someone else.'
Shirone stopped overthinking. Grateful for all the luck that had shaped him, he'd just
keep moving forward.
Iruki asked suspiciously, "Shirone, why are you grinning like an idiot? Eat something
weird?"
Shirone shook his head without answering. Steeling himself, he took a step forward.
"I'm back, guys."

Two days remained until his suspension ended.
Nade and Iruki arrived at the fourth training ground at dawn, waiting for Shirone.
Though early, summer made the surroundings bright. The two yawned repeatedly, staring at
the entrance.
Nade rubbed his sleepy face. "Ugh, what? Dragging us out this early."
"He said he had something to show us."
"What could it be? After all we've seen, I'm already amazed."
"Who knows? Maybe he got a tattoo on his dick."
"Pfft—hahaha!"
Imagining it, Nade roared with laughter. Just then, Shirone entered the training ground.
"Huh? You're here already. Why's Nade like that?"
"Dunno. Probably a pervert thing."
"Hahaha! A tattoo! There?! Hahaha!"
Uneasy, Shirone didn't pry. They were surely joking around as usual.
"Anyway, let's begin. I'm eager to show you."
"Show us what? The tattoo? Hahaha!"
Iruki sighed. "Stop. It's not funny anymore. So, why'd you call us here? What's this
thing you want to show?"
"Wait. I'll do it now."
Shirone walked to the center of the training ground and raised his hand toward a rocky
hill.
"What? Photon output? Did you increase it?"
Shirone cast the Photon Cannon. A white flash streaked past Nade and Iruki.
Boom!
The rock shattered with a dull roar.
The previously bored duo's eyes widened. Their jaws nearly hit the ground.
