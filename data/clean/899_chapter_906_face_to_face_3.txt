Face to Face (3)
"Time remaining until purification: 65,400 hours."
It had been an almost eternal period since Goffin fell into hell.
'Such an immense amount of time.'
Shirone felt dizzy.
If Omega hadn't been in thousandths, experiencing Goffin's time might
have driven him mad.
Goffin muttered,
"Time flies."
His body was emaciated, and his face was gaunt with sunken eyes.
But his mind remained intact, and that fact alone gave Shirone
chills.
'Although 600 quintillion hours haven't actually passed.'
Time was a matter of perception, and hell was a place where that
perception was maximized.
'Intense sensations accelerate the flow of time.'
In other words, the more intense Goffin's suffering, the shorter the
purification time.
'That's the nature of time.'
Shirone recalled disassembling moments of time infinitely in the state
of non-form.
The bizarre signals he saw when he delved deeper beyond each 0.666
seconds.
'Those must be special signals affecting the relativity of time and
sensation.'
It was the truth of time.
'Ultimately, what the creator of this world wants is not for evil to be
punished but for efficient data purification.'
Shirone's eyes filled with sorrow.
'Is God indifferent?'
With the same question in mind, Goffin reached the end of hell, blocked
by a black wall.
Staring intently at it, the darkness of the wall seemed to move as if
alive.
"The end of space."
Though called a wall, it was actually the entrance to a void
world.
It was also the final gate where emotions from the underworld reached
after purification.
'Ozente Guy.'
Shirone guessed what fate awaited Rian's older brother, who had passed
on his link and disappeared.
'He went to an eternal void world, but it might not be as terrible as it
seems.'
In reality, Jin Seong-eum would be wandering hell without even a chance
for purification.
"The infinite wall."
Hearing a voice behind him, Goffin turned to see an angel with black
wings like a shadow approaching.
Recognizing Lucifer, the lord of hell, Goffin raised his hand with a
rare smile.
"Are you here to see me off?"
After fighting fiercely for over 600 quintillion hours, they no longer
felt like strangers.
But Lucifer ignored Goffin's joke and turned his gaze to the infinite
wall.
"Why are you in such a hurry? You only have 65,000 hours left until
purification."
"I can't stand being here any longer."
Of course, that was true.
But it wasn't something expected from Goffin, who had endured 600
quintillion hours calmly.
Lucifer asked,
"Are you still clinging to false hope?"
Since falling into hell, Goffin had searched for a way back to reality
but found it impossible.
Unlike traversing through dimensions via astral projection, those
subject to purification couldn't escape the underworld.
'That's how this world is made.'
Goffin looked back at the infinite wall.
"Hope is a good thing. If I break through this void realm, I can
return to reality. Then even hell would have to go on strike."
From Lucifer's perspective, it was madness.
"Why are you so obsessed with reality? Hell has already been
devastated because of you. You could live happily here."
"Yes, that's true. I've spent a lot of time here. It must have been
an incredibly long time. But looking back..."
Goffin shrugged his shoulders.
"Even that eternal time feels like a moment." "Isn't that
right? Time seems to flow endlessly, but when you reach the end of the future..."
When only the past remains,
"All memories become just one feeling contained in zero seconds.
How long you've lived doesn't matter."
Therefore,
"Beings that can't transcend infinity are illusions. Me, you, and
your sibling..."
Goffin pointed at Lucifer.
"Ikael too."
Originally there were nine archangels.
Eight concepts forming the world and one opposing concept.
The archangel of chaos, Lucifer.
"Of course Anke Ra didn't create you. You weren't born. You're
negative energy belonging to nonexistent time and events."
Outside the law.
"In this vast cycle, you and angels are number two in the law. When
angels' concepts weaken, chaos strengthens..."
Goffin winked as he spoke,
"But that doesn't mean you can meet Ikael."
Lucifer wrinkled his nose at Goffin's piercing words but knew arguing
would only hurt himself.
"So you're gambling on breaking through the infinite wall? If you
fail, you'll face eternal annihilation."
"I don't care."
Goffin's eyes turned serious.
"Even after 600 quintillion hours pass, all that remains is
emptiness. If God is truly indifferent, that's fine too. It means I can become God."
Unlike his comrades who chose reincarnation, he remained as his signal
for this reason.
Goffin walked towards the wall,
"Shall we get going?"
He would break infinity.
"Wait."
At Lucifer's words, Goffin turned around,
"It's not like we're saying goodbye forever."
Lucifer shivered at Goffin's ability to smile even while heading towards
annihilation,
"Do you really think such a being can exist? A God who loves this
world instead of being indifferent?"
"Hmm... It's difficult because that's not what God is. But if I
were God..."
Looking up at the ceiling Goffin said,
"Wouldn't it be nice to plant some hope? Even if it's just one
signal."
Lucifer remained silent for a long time,
A Messiah...
If he could really achieve that...
"I will send you back to reality."
Surprised by Lucifer's unexpected words Goffin raised an eyebrow

"Oh? That took quite some time. How did you hold back all this
while?"
"It's not about methods. There's no way to stop purification. But
because of that I can send you."
Goffin realized,
"No way... You..."
"Yes. As long as purification happens it's fine. I'll take your place in

"Hmm."

"You know what kind of pain you're talking about right? It's

"In exchange... there's a condition."
Goffin chuckled,
"I figured."
"If you truly want to give meaning to this empty world, share that

It can't just end as mere signals...
"I understand your sentiment."
Goffin showed a troubled expression,
"You're different from humans. No links exist between us. You're

"Ikael."
Lucifer spoke firmly,
"Give her meaning not as part of a system but as beings like yourselves.
That's my condition."
"Ikael…"
Of all beings why her?
"I thought you hated her?"
In fact, Ikael, who represented the beginning of the law, and Lucifer,

Like how existence and non-existence cannot merge.
But because of this, Lucifer hated and envied Ikael more than

He still remembered.
The moment of creation, when the archangel Ikael was born smiling amidst

The first emotion he felt then was,
'Why am I...
...born so hideously?'
Through the early stages of Omega, Shirone knew how deep Lucifer's

'Yin and Yang.'
Two extremes that split at birth.
Coming out of his recollection, Lucifer spoke with a hollow

"I was jealous. Even though we were born in the same place, why am

It wasn't about appearance; it was a matter of concept.
"Why do I hate Ikael? Why do I think I'm hideous and Ikael is

"So did you realize?"
"Maybe."
Fighting with Goffin for over 600 quintillion hours had changed

"Because I couldn't have her."

"All I could do was hate. But not anymore. If everything is

If emotions are just signals too,
"At least let my last act be love. Love is better than hate. Isn't

'Love?'
