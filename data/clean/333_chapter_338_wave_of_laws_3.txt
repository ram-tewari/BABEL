Chapter 338:
Wave of Laws (3)
"Why don't you understand your place? Do you not realize the
situation you're in? You might be the best at school, but do you think your reputation matters
here?"
Shirone didn't hear Jocre's words.
If they held out like this, people would die. But if that was going to
happen anyway, there was a much simpler solution.
Shirone narrowed his focus like a sniper. The Ice Queen's face came into
sharp view.
'I have to end this in one go.'
Eliminate the Ice Queen in the shortest time possible.
If that happened, the strange power controlling this space would
disappear as well.
As Shirone maintained his poker face, Jocre felt psychological
pressure.
It was like someone with a sure winning hand in poker going all-in with
their assets.
'What kind of people are they?'
Even in a situation where hundreds of lives were at stake, they were
showing guts. It was something that defied his common sense.
They were looking down on him. That thought made uncontrollable anger
surge up.
"How dare you look down on me!"
As Jocre shouted, Shirone's Armand unsheathed and rushed like a
flash.
Armand's target was not Jocre but the Ice Queen.
The Ice Queen activated her law and quickly moved away. Even so, as she
was outpaced, she flew up into the sky.
'Law of Destruction.'
The Ice Queen's law enveloped Armand. However, the magic sword only
trembled and did not break.
'Why?'
It was an unfamiliar experience for the Ice Queen.
'An incarnation?'
A perfect trinity object could not be altered by law, and in heaven, it
was called an incarnation.
The Ice Queen activated the law of freezing. Frost formed on Armand's
blade, and ice began to clump.
As the speed slowed, the Ice Queen regained her composure. But suddenly,
she felt an impact on her back as if she had hit something.
'What is it?'
Without time to find the answer, she amplified the law of freezing.
Armand, trapped in a massive ice chunk, couldn't withstand the weight and fell to the ground.
The Ice Queen immediately turned around and cast a wave of law at the
point she had collided with. With a bang, a translucent dome-shaped barrier spread across the
sky.
'Something is blocking the space.'
Jocre had a blank expression. Dante, who had been setting up the sealing
formation all night, spoke triumphantly.
"How do you like my surprise gift? This area is already sealed. I
planted totems all night to set up the sealing formation. The Ice Queen can never leave
here."
Jocre slowly turned his head to look at Dante. The Ice Queen was
trapped. It was the worst imaginable situation.
"You think you can rule the world by clinging to such a monster? Do
you think the place you live is that easy? The army will be dispatched soon. Even if we die here, the
Ice Queen will eventually be destroyed. End this now, and there might be some leniency."
Jocre's jaw trembled. His breathing became rough. If the army was
dispatched, the Ice Queen would be destroyed. Of course, he and his friends would also pay the price for
their crimes.
"You've trapped me."
The Ice Queen landed. The cold she brought spread across the ground,
chilling people's bones.
Shirone did not avoid the Ice Queen's killing intent. Instead, he met
her gaze as if challenging her, grabbing the handle of the returned Armand. The jewel embedded in the
cross guard emitted a strong red light. He knew his opponent was not to be underestimated.
"Your Majesty, are you alright?"
The Ice Queen turned to Jocre, who asked worriedly, and gave him a
gentle smile, patting his head.
But when she looked back at Shirone, her gaze was completely different.
Her cold eyes pierced his heart like an ice spear.
"Why are you interfering with me?"
"Don't you see what you've done? And weren't you the one who first
disregarded other people's lives?"
"Foolish. The strong rule. Isn't that the rule of the world you
live in? I am merely trying to reign over you as the new ruler."
Cold air rose above the Ice Queen's head.
"Frozen Mist."
Cold air spread rapidly from the Ice Queen. Shirone's coat froze solid
and shattered. The cooling speed was too fast. In a few seconds, the people around would have their
hearts frozen.
"Watch out!"
Liria cast a localized sealing formation at the Ice Queen's feet. A
circular formation glowed and drew itself, and a reddish pillar of light shot up.
"Ugh!"
The Ice Queen's face twisted. It felt like something essential to her
was being pulled down, as if an object couldn't defy the law of gravity.
As the Ice Queen lifted her law and soared into the sky, Liria looked up
with a regretful expression.
"No, her activity is too strong to be contained by a localized
sealing formation. We need to bind her somehow."
The Ice Queen's face, unable to control her expression, was grotesque.
The power of the sealing formation was immense. She hadn't felt such fear of annihilation even when her
brain was burning.
"I will not forgive you!"
As the Ice Queen cast a wave of law, the laws around people's necks
disappeared, and new laws were born.
"Argh!"
People's snouts protruded like dogs, and their teeth became sharp. Their
muscles swelled, tearing their clothes, and their nails grew like wolves'. Their hardened skin protruded
like armor.
"They're evolving into a state suitable for combat!"
At Liria's words, Dante shouted.
"Let's finish this before they join forces. If we concentrate our
firepower, we can win. Liria, focus on defending against the law."
As Shirone's party was about to fly towards the Ice Queen, Vivian
muttered, placing her hands on the ground.
"Radius of the Spirit."
Vivian's body petrified, and the ground shook. Circular walls rose in
succession from her center and spread out.
Boom! Boom! Boom! Boom! Boom!
Every ten steps, walls spread out in a radial pattern. The space between
the walls was ten meters. And all this radius constituted her spirit.
"Damn! What is this!"
Shirone's party hurriedly retreated, but before they could take a few
steps, a wall rose behind them. It was a 30-meter-high wall.
As they were taken aback, the front wall melted and Vivian's face
protruded in relief. Her upper body emerged, and her lower body extended like a snake, connecting to the
wall.
"Hehe. How do you like it? My ability, the Radius of the
Spirit."
As Vivian raised her hands, Jocre and Ludvance jumped down and landed on
her palms like cranes.
"Haha! Serves you right. Acting all high and mighty, and this is
all you've got?"
The Radius of the Spirit builds walls shared with the caster's spirit at
a rate of ten meters per second. However, the further the radius extends, the thinner the density of the
spirit, so if the walls are built too far, the response drops to the level of inanimate objects. But
Vivian was satisfied with her ability. The Ice Queen had accurately implemented her psychology of
wanting to be the center of attention into her ability.
"This place is a perfect maze. If you catch me at the center, the
Radius of the Spirit will be undone. But can you find the way?"
"No need to run around like mice."
Dante cast teleportation. A 30-meter wall was nothing to jump over.
Then, countless Vivians popped out from the top and swung their petrified hands.
"Tch! Annoying."
If he collided with the petrified Vivian during a straight-line
teleportation, he couldn't avoid a fatal injury. As Dante landed with an annoyed expression, Shirone
raised Armand vertically.
"I'll take care of the Ice Queen."
As everyone turned to Shirone, he muttered.
"Diamond Armor."
The blade split, and thin frames like insect legs enveloped Shirone's
face. In an instant, he transformed into a mage with a deep hood, and Jocre's party's faces
hardened.
Dante asked.
"Is that Diamond Armor? What are you planning?"
"Armand withstood the law. Diamond Armor might be able to block the
Ice Queen's law."
Liria nodded.
"It's possible. It's the same principle as a totem. But the Ice
Queen is in the sky. We need to find a way to reach her first."
Shirone raised his hooded face. The Radius of the Spirit, which created
clones at the speed of thought, was indeed a difficult obstacle. But it wasn't impossible to find a way
out.
"Function comes from form."
Shirone maximized his photonic sculpting. Light flared from his body,
and giant wings of light spread out.
He sculpted light.
The fact astonished the mages. Sculpting light with luminescence meant
immense photon compression.
Jocre's party swallowed dryly. They knew what Shirone intended, as he
sculpted a form that performed only one function: flying.
"Take care of this place."
Photonic Sculpting - Light Wings.
Shirone bent his lower body and swung the wings of light downward,
soaring at tremendous speed. Even though it wasn't teleportation, it was like a flash of light shooting
up.
'You think I'll let you!'
Despite that, Vivian reacted. Dozens of Vivians popped out from the
walls and bit at Shirone like teeth.
'Got you!'
The impact was clear. But soon after, Vivian furrowed her brows in
confusion. The tendrils of Diamond Armor were blocking the petrified clones' attacks at close
range.
"This...!"
Vivian poured all her strength into it. The sound of stone being worn
down could be heard from where Shirone was. The force from the durability of the stone was tremendous,
but the tendrils of the Diamond Armor were strong enough to crush most rocks.
External pressure: 1.7 tons. Reached muscle endurance
limit.
Shirone gritted his teeth and pushed the tendrils downward. As the
tendrils squeezed through a narrow gap and bounced Shirone up, Vivian's clones collided with each other
and shattered.
'Eliminate the Ice Queen.'
The speed generated by the Light Wings was terrifying. Shirone pursued
the Ice Queen at near the speed of sound. As his vision narrowed, Armand adjusted his physical
abilities.
Target undetectable. Increasing photoreceptor cells.
Enhancing macular function.
As his vision widened, the Ice Queen came into clear view. The
artificial brain balanced the flight. As the distance closed, the Ice Queen turned and cast her
law.
"Blizzard of Freezing."
Sharp snowflakes, sharper than razor blades, swirled around, and Shirone
covered his face. As the cloak covering his body hardened into a metallic texture, the ice shards
scratched the surface like thousands of pieces of broken glass.
"Ugh!"
The Ice Queen's law wasn't as purposefully sharp as magic, but its scale
was on a different level. Her rules were larger than the rules of this place. That was the scale.
Shirone turned the hardened cloak back into an organic material and
looked ahead. The atmosphere had frozen, and massive ice columns spread out intricately like a human
nervous system. It felt like becoming a microorganism on a microscopic level.
'So, it's ultra-low temperature.'
The reason he couldn't feel the cold was that Armand was regulating his
nervous system. But as soon as he inhaled, the cold air immediately froze his lungs.
"Hmph!"
Shirone quickly closed his mouth.
Detected breathing blockage. Replacing with artificial
oxygen. Remaining oxygen consumption time: 1 minute 27 seconds. 2 minutes 32 seconds. 4 minutes 49
seconds.
As Armand stored air, he began to inject oxygen directly into Shirone's
bloodstream. Regaining his combat ability, Shirone flew between the ice columns and engaged in an aerial
battle with the Ice Queen.
Like a hawk hunting, Shirone folded his Light Wings and swooped down,
grabbing his wrist and firing 120 photon cannons per minute from the magic crystal embedded in his
gauntlet.
