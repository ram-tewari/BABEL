At the top of the Ivory Tower where the sky was visible—
"Gr-... Great Star..."

respond.

"Why... why me...?"

"Gah!"
Shirone collapsed to the ground, and she briefly looked down at her
bloodstained hand.
Xing lowered her gaze.
"Shirone..."
A clean, fatal blow.
The faint convulsions running through his body were all Shirone could
manage.
"You see?"
Great Star shook the blood from her hand.
"Why do something so pointless?"
Xing trembled as a chill ran down her spine, as if even her soul had
frozen over.
"Did you lie to us...?"
"What are you talking about?"
Even Xing, who hadn't fully trusted her, asked the question—because she

She is not human.
"I am an Administrator. I cannot allow someone who has made contact

"Urgh... hhk!"

Shirone gritted his teeth.
'I'm dying.'

'She embedded Law-based code... I... can't regenerate...'
His vision tunneled, narrowing to a pinhole as he drifted on the edge of
consciousness.
In his final moments, what came to mind was—
—Save yourself from the terrible truth.
'Damn it...'
Even the faint sound of breath disappeared. Shirone lay still, his eyes
rolled back.
He was dead.
"...No..."
Xing stared in shock.
Though she had watched Shirone die right before her eyes, her mind

Only one emotion moved her now—rage.
"I'll kill you."

"You're still talking nonsense. I don't possess life. And now,

"Begin."
"Hmm."
Administrators may govern independent realms, but in the end, they are

Xing didn't know how much they truly understood about each
other...
"Cell Buster."
Argones didn't look pleased.
His body began to split into countless anti-cells. Xing, snapping back

"No!"
But now that Great Star had regained her freedom, there was no one left
who could stop them.
"Guh!"
Great Star blocked Xing's Law. Argones, transformed into a swarm of
cells, ascended into the sky.
'It's over.'
Because her convictions were so strong, the moment she thought that,
even her will to resist vanished.
"Ugh..."
Xing fell to her knees, tears spilling down her face.
Great Star approached her.
"I didn't want to go this far. If I could, I wanted to live with

"S-So how... how did this..."

"In the next reincarnation..."

"Try not to make the same mistake."

Ymir's Mind.

"You—!"

Ymir's superego had already thrown a punch that nearly reached

And so—
"GRAAAAH!"

"I'll take it."

"I hate being in debt. If there's something you want, name it."

"Heh heh heh… Let's have some real fun."
The total extermination of humanity.
