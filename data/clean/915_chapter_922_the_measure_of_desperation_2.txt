Chapter 922:
The Measure of Desperation (2)
In the year 689 of Omega.
The Gaia people, who had been strong enough to check Anke Ra, saw their
population drastically decrease after Small Purification.
"Ataraxia."
As Ikael's judicial halo shone, six archangels charged at the Gaia
people.
"Damn it...!"
With the population reduced by Small Purification, the power of the
"Hand of God" had significantly weakened.
"Ragnarok."
As Uriel, surrounded by white electricity, charged, a massive path
opened in the space filled with humans.
"Maintain formation!"
As the Gaia people gathered in perfect unison, like cells healing a
wound, Metiel clicked her tongue.
"Still struggling?"
It was the era of resistance.
As she dodged the "Hand of God's" attack and raised her hand
to the sky.
"Small-medium speed missile."
Hundreds of tons of metal filled the sky and were fired at supersonic
speed.
The impact was equivalent to a small meteor collision, and the Ultima
system shook greatly.
"Hold on! For the new era!"
Before the "Hand of God" could regain its form, Raiel, the
archangel of light, infiltrated and swung his fist.
It was a simple attack, but the physical force coming at light speed was
unstoppable.
"Ultima system!"
An invisible barrier ballooned up from the center of the Gaia people and
quickly expanded.
"Hmph."
As Raiel's fist struck, horizontal flashes of light endlessly penetrated
the barrier.
Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!
Following the trajectory of the flashes, powerful chain explosions
created intense heat.
"Will you only be satisfied when we're all dead?"
As Raiel flew into the sky, spinning the accelerator, he arrogantly
raised his chin.
"Satisfied?"
Light entered the eyes of the Gaia people.
"Are you talking to me?"
As billions of voices unified into one, the "Hand of God"
clenched its fist.
"It's the same pattern. It won't work anymore."
As Raiel spun the judicial halo at light speed and prepared to launch,
Satiel intervened, spreading her arms.
"That's enough! We've fought enough. If one side is annihilated, it
will be a loss for everyone."
Raiel snorted.
"Step aside, Satiel. We've tolerated you because you claimed
neutrality. But if you side with humans, it's different."
"I'm not taking sides. I'm saying there's no point in continuing
this war."
The Gaia people thought.
'No point?'
Of those who resolved to leave the Photon System, only Goffin
remained.
'Why are we fighting?'
It had been so long that they had forgotten what they were fighting
for.
Satiel spoke to the representative.
"Request a truce. There are ways for angels and humans to create a
new world together."
"We've come too far. If this is the end, what about the wars our
ancestors fought for eons? The hundreds of billions of humans who died in Small
Purification?"
Ikael amplified her voice.
"Do you still think you're gods? Look, you've lost the war and look
pathetic. As far as I know, there's no god like that."
A voice came from the ground.
"Could I be more pathetic?"
Goffin, dressed in rags, limped and panted heavily, making Ikael
frown.
'He's still fighting in that state?'
Having swallowed Anke Ra's rage alone, he was no different from a
wreck.
'He stopped Small Purification.'
The rage that could destroy the entire world was too much for one human
to bear.
Ikael gritted her teeth.
'Foolish human.'
While the fall of a strong enemy was satisfying, seeing his broken state
pained her.
'Indeed, we've fought for so long.'
From the beginning until now.
"Give up, Goffin. The Gaia people have declined, and you're not in
a normal state. This war is our victory."
Goffin smiled dryly.
"That's not possible."
"Why? Why do you try to bear everything alone? It's hard enough to
maintain your sanity after swallowing Small Purification."
As the pain of his twisted mind surged again, Goffin grimaced and
clutched his chest.
"Ugh!"
"Arrogant and arrogant one. You are not gods. You are just mutants
destroying the stability of the world."
"Maybe."
Goffin admitted readily.
"We might be errors destroying this world. And I might be the worst
error."
"Then stop. It's hard enough to handle the rage of Small
Purification, so why fight?"
"Because I hate it."
Goffin's eyes widened.
"I hate this cold world. I don't care if I'm a god or not. I will
become a god."
"Don't mistake delusion for reality. This is a perfect world. We
are beings born from the law."
"Is that a god?"
Ikael fell silent.
"If you just make it perfect, is everything over?"
From this moment, Goffin's philosophy completely denied the world's
law.
"A grand name like god, but to the world you created, to the
creatures living in that world...!"
A massive hexagonal light rose.
"Can't you have even a shred of compassion?"
As Miracle Stream roared, a tornado of light swept the area.
Metiel muttered.
"He's not sane. He's mad with rage."
At the same time, six archangels charged at Goffin, reversing the
Miracle Stream.
The world shook with the combination of the highest concepts, and
thunderbolts rained from the sky.
'I thought he was a wreck... but Goffin was still Goffin.'
As the evenly matched battle continued, Ikael's eyes widened as she
opened Ataraxia.
The archangels' power was amplified to the extreme.
"Got him!"
The six archangels trapped Goffin in the realm of Movement Zero and
simultaneously delivered their strongest blows.
'It's over. He can't escape.'
As Raiel was convinced.
"Ugh!"
Goffin, his eyes showing the whites, pulled the Miracle Stream towards
himself.
'A cold world!'
As light smoke compressed between his palms, it shone
brilliantly.
'I can't accept this!'
Brake.
"What...!"
As an unstoppable inertia surged, the six archangels were flung in the
same direction.
Even as the attack failed, none of the archangels understood the current
situation.
"How...?"
For the first time, Ikael realized and accepted the shiver, forgetting
even the authority of the archangel.
"He stopped the rotation."
For about three seconds, the planet's rotation was halted, causing
volcanic eruptions, earthquakes, and tsunamis across the continent.
'Just one human...'
If one wishes with desperate sincerity, even the laws of the universe
can change.
Ikael unconsciously muttered.
"A god with a heart...?"
If there were a god who endlessly watched over the world and could
sympathize with the pain of creatures.
'Under the mercy of that god.'
Angels, humans, mountains, seas, fields, and trees.
'Nothing is insignificant.'
Goffin's hands, burning with the light of Miracle Stream, charged at the
archangels.
"I will become a god!"
As Uriel raised his arms to defend, Goffin's eyes suddenly
widened.
"Ugh!"
He then spewed a mouthful of blood, losing his momentum and staggering
in pain.
'Damn it!'
As Anke Ra's rage surged again, his entire body went into shock.
Uriel spun the wheel of paradise.
"...Don't blame me."
Goffin hastily activated Hexa, but before it was completed, the wheel of
paradise struck his abdomen.
Goffin's body flew like an arrow, seemingly evaporating.
"No!"
Satiel shouted, spreading her angelic wings.
"Sa-Ti-El!"
Ikael's voice, powerful enough to shake the heavens and earth, stopped
her from pursuing Goffin.
"Ugh!"
Ikael, spreading a massive Ataraxia, looked down, the most noble
being.
"Satiel, I can no longer tolerate your recklessness. I command you,
do not move a step."
The order was given to Satiel, but no angel dared to move.
'I've never seen her this angry.'
Ikael turned towards the direction Goffin had flown.
"I will settle this myself. You stay here and stop the Gaia
people."
She felt it was right to end Goffin with her own hands.
Landing where the trail of destruction ended, Ikael looked ahead.
"You again? I'm starting to get attached."
Goffin's body, leaning against the collapsed rock pile, was covered in
blood.
"Pitiful human."
Ikael walked slowly.
"We've fought for so long. If I had known the end would be this
pathetic, I would have ended it sooner."
"Heh. Heh heh."
Goffin laughed, his shoulders shaking.
"How do you feel? Defeating the strongest enemy who hindered
you."
"Neither good nor bad. I am a noble archangel. I have no emotions
for humans."
"Is that so."
Goffin recalled.
Ikael, who had been angry, annoyed, flustered, and sometimes...
smiled.
"Well, history belongs to the victors. Record it as you like. But
make sure to note that I was handsome."
"Is that all you have to say?"
Goffin looked at the sky, lost in thought, but it was a burden he didn't
want anyone to bear.
"Yes."
Flames sparked in Ikael's eyes.
"You're arrogant to the end. You came close to defeating a god with
a human body, and you have nothing to leave for posterity?"
"What can I do? I got hit properly." Ikael spread her wings
and shot forward at incredible speed, her blade-like hand aimed at Goffin.
As her hand stopped right in front of his face, Goffin looked at her
calmly and asked, "Why? Are you really getting attached to me?"
"Shut up."
It was the moment she had dreamed of all her life, but now that it was
here, everything felt confusing.
"Surrender," Ikael said.
"It's not too late. Raise the white flag and surrender. I will take
responsibility and protect you all."
"I said no."
"Get a grip! I said I would help you! I'll even record it as a draw
in history."
Goffin didn't blink an eye.
"Do you like me?"
"Don't be ridiculous. It's not because I like you. If you die, that
Hexa..."
The possibility of everyone being linked.
"Are you really going to end it like this? Is this the extent of
your compassion for the world?"
"It's impossible."
The hexagonal Hexa floated above Goffin's palm, emitting a clear
sound.
"Hexa is a signal that comes from a higher dimension to a lower
dimension. It's the same as the Idea. But to link you, the signal has to be sent in reverse, from a
lower dimension to a higher dimension."
"It's an incredibly difficult task. It's like a drawing piercing
through paper and jumping into reality."
Ikael's eyes wavered.
"Impossible?"
"But."
Goffin pointed to her holy body.
"If you can infinitely amplify Hexa's signal with Ataraxia, it
might be possible."
Ikael, considering the possibility with her archangel senses, shook her
head gloomily.
"No. It can't be combined. Even if everything is amplified, Hexa is
a signal outside the law."
"You can't be so sure."
"Huh?"
As Ikael blinked, Goffin extended Hexa towards her holy body.
"Shall we try?"
