The Killing War (1)

Everyone except Uorin.

capital.

Who was it?

Anything could be justified.
"In short, this isn't just a threat. On the contrary— Havitz has given
us something."
Albino's eyes narrowed.
"…A license to kill."
A declaration that he would take all the blame—
And that the nations should kill to their hearts' content.
Satan's intentions…
left the minds of the Twelve Nations utterly speechless.

'Did you calculate all this? Of course, you did. But you know this isn't

A love so strong, it could justify murder.

alive.

The Small Conference.

Socrates, weighing over 150 kilograms.

A Second-Class Archmage or higher can bypass even the most advanced
