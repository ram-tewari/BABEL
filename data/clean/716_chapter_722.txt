Chapter 722:
When Kai fired the flare, a similar flare responded from the other side
of the mountain.
Burying the spent flare in the ground, Kai said, "Hehe, at least
they gave me a flare, thinking I'm on their side. Maybe… they had no choice but to kill me because the
situation changed."
To deceive Kai, the other Black participants had to give him a flare
like the others.
'He wants to believe that,' Shirone thought, but he didn't voice
it.
Kai's decision to use the Magician's special ability to trade for
high-grade cards showed he already understood the situation.
"Are you sure? If you deceive your own Black team, it could be
dangerous in the final showdown."
"No one… cares about my life," Kai said, turning his head with
a dark expression.
"When my parents died, the Black village chief told me to fend for
myself. He said it didn't matter if I died."
Black was a world of survival of the fittest.
"In the Black zone, if you're not needed, you have to die. But you
saved me. To be on the same side, I have to do something too. I'll gather high-grade cards."
In White, it would have been decided by a vote.
'Rationality is always right, but…'
The desperation of a child struggling to survive couldn't be dismissed
as wrong.
'Kai can't go to White. So I have to win in Black.'
After waiting for about 20 minutes, three Black participants emerged
from the bushes.
They approached cautiously, stopping at a distance where they could
identify friend from foe, and smiled at Kai.
"Wow, Kai. You really pulled it off."
Sensing the killing intent in their words, Kai stepped back, but the
Black team leader stopped his comrades.
"Wait. There's one more."
Shirone, wanting to avoid a fight, waited in a visible spot.
"He's the one who took down Paroa."
Until the law of Whiteblack ended, no one could be declared dead.
"Kai! How dare you deceive us!"
As one of the Black team members drew a card, Kai waved his hands
frantically.
"No! He's a Seeker! He's on our team now!"
"A Seeker?"
When the Black team questioned, Shirone took out his job card and
approached Kai.
"I can choose the law. And right now, I'm on the Black team. As
long as Kai is safe, I won't change sides."
It was hard to believe, but the Black team had experienced enough of
White's meddling to be wary.
"Hmph, hypocrites. Still living by votes? Hey, go
check."
One of the Black team members approached cautiously, checked the Seeker
card, and stepped back.
"It's true. He's on the Black team now."
"Hmm, a Seeker. I thought he was a Magician."
In any case, having a skilled person who avoided the Power Bomb and took
down Paroa on their side was good.
"So… what card do you want to trade?"
Kai confidently showed the S-grade card.
"We have CPR. We want to trade it for two S-grade cards or four
A-grade cards."
"CPR?"
It was a highly valuable card, and the Black team members' eyes lit
up.
"How did you get it?"
Shirone spoke up.
"I got it. As you might guess, I'm a Magician. I can use magic
without the law of Whiteblack."
If true, the survival test's outcome was already leaning towards Black,
but the Seeker part was troubling.
'Damn! There was a Magician in White?'
Whiteblack's population was small, but it wasn't impossible for someone
to have realized the Spirit Zone.
"Then why were you in White? You should have wiped them out and
come to Black."
"That's my decision. Anyway, I'm on the Black team now. Do you want
to trade or not?"
The Black team whispered among themselves, and the leader stepped closer
with cards.
"I'll offer one S-grade and two A-grade cards. We have Medios'
Sorcerer, Bond, and Act City Zombie."
Kai muttered in surprise.
"Medios' Sorcerer…"
It was an S-grade card, effective in combat.
Medios' Sorcerer (Possession)
Description: Corrupt sorcerers are exiled to Medios for life.
Effect: Cast powerful magic for 60 seconds. Cooldown: 360
seconds.
Current cooldown: 0 seconds.
'With that card…'
For Kai, it was a lifeline.
"Wait. We offered two S-grade or four A-grade cards. One S-grade
and two A-grade isn't fair."
"It's not a bad deal. Even an A-grade has Bond. Kai should
know."
Bond (Function).
Description: Trust is proportional to time and inversely proportional to
distance.
Effect: Doubles the activation time of held cards.
Shirone's eyes lit up as he confirmed the effect.
'I see. Combining Bond with Medios' Sorcerer extends the possession time
to 120 seconds.'
This also indicated the high value of CPR.
Act City Zombie (Summon).
Description: The zombie outbreak in Act City was determined to be
evolution, not mutation.
Effect: 20 entities. Fast and strong. Destroy the head or everything but
the head.
Special Ability: Infection. Bites turn victims into zombies.
The last card, Act City Zombie, would also be a strong ally for
Kai.
"What do you think? Should we trade?" Kai asked slyly.
Pretending to consider, Shirone nodded.
"Alright. Getting more weapons is important."
"Wait. There's something to check first."
The Black team leader extended his hand.
"Show Kai's job card. Just in case. I'll reveal mine
too."
The leader's job was Thief, with the special ability Pickpocket.
'The ability to steal one card within a 2-meter radius without the
target noticing.'
While the law's effect might prevent sensing the presence, there were no
cards to steal anyway.
'The problem is Kai.'
The Magician's special ability, Magic Card, could only change one card
to a random card.
So, having changed the Old Copper Shield to CPR, there was no way to
change the job card now.
"What are you waiting for? Show your job card."
Kai's smiling lips trembled.
"Alright. But there's a condition. We check alone. If you come too
close, Pickpocket will activate."
Though he had no cards, bluffing was part of the strategy, making Kai's
proposal reasonable.
'But he can't deceive the Black team. Does he have a strategy he hasn't
told me?'
Kai reassured Shirone.
"Don't worry. I'll be quick."
Acting maturely, Kai met the Black team leader in the clearing.
"Let's check the cards first."
"Here are three cards. Now show your job card and CPR together.
They must be shown together."
"Of course. Here they are."
The job was Magician.
But the way he presented the cards so calmly caused the leader a moment
of cognitive dissonance.
'What? A Magician? Wait, a Magician?'
CPR was fake.
"You little…!"
As anger surged, Kai snatched the cards from the leader's hand and
shouted.
"Run, brother! I'll hold them off!"
Just as Kai raised the Medios' Sorcerer card, the leader's dagger
stabbed his stomach.
"You little brat… deceiving me?"
"Kai!"
As Shirone rushed over, the leader activated Medios' Sorcerer.
"Magical Fire!"
Blue light flickered from the possessed leader's body, and a massive
fireball fell from the sky.
"Activate! Act City Zombie!"
"Graaaah!"
Twenty zombies were summoned, claws raised, charging at Shirone.
"You bastards…!"
As the Photon Cannon blew off the zombies' heads, the remaining two
Black team members joined the fight.
"Activate! Wail of the Buried!"
Wail of the Buried.
Description: The people of Kvashutra were buried alive by a mad
lord.
Their voices ensnare the living.
Effect: Immobilizes the target for 2 seconds.
"Uoooooh!"
As the ghosts of the buried appeared and grabbed his ankles, zombies
swarmed Shirone.
'Light Burst!'
As the light barrier shattered the zombies' limbs, the Black team's
giant leaped.
'Special Ability: Werewolf.'
The giant, with the Druid job card, sprouted fur, and his long snout
opened wide.
"Raaaargh!"
The weapon card at the wolf's claw glowed.
Wild Action.
Description: Anyone can become aggressive.
Effect: Reproduces imagined actions exactly. Safety not
guaranteed.
"Can you dodge this?"
The werewolf's dazzling moves, recreating the scene in his mind,
targeted Shirone's weak points.
'Choeani Bardo!'
As the sharp claws missed Shirone's position, the Black team members'
eyes widened.
"What?"
Disappearing before the Wail of the Buried's effect ended meant it
didn't violate the law.
"Damn! Where did he go…!"
As the leader turned, the Photon Cannon blasted the entire Black team in
rapid succession.
"Ugh! Ugh!"
Without time to check the fallen Black team, Shirone rushed to
Kai.
"Kai! Stay with me! Kai!"
With the dagger still embedded, Kai's wound was soaked in blood as he
opened his eyes.
"Brother… I'm sorry."
"…Why?"
Why did he do it?
"Why didn't you ask for help? You knew I was a Magician. Why did
you do something so foolish?"
Kai shook his head weakly.
"I don't know. I was just… scared."
"Scared of what? What scared you so much?"
"I was afraid you'd be disappointed."
Shirone was speechless.
"I just wanted to be helpful… I'm sorry. I boasted foolishly and
couldn't do anything…"
Shirone understood what Kai was trying to say.
"You don't need to say anything more. Let's focus on treating
you."
"You saved me… I wanted to be recognized… I was afraid you'd doubt
me…"
"Stop talking! You're still bleeding!"
"I'm sorry… brother."
Kai's head fell to the side, and the heaving of his chest gradually
ceased.
"Kai! Kai!"
What is this world?
The paradox of Whiteblack is that there is no way to return from Black
to White.
'True goodness…'
It doesn't distinguish between good and evil.
Lost in thought, Shirone gently laid Kai's body in a cozy patch of
grass.
Looking at the job card Kai had held until the end, Shirone's eyebrows
furrowed sadly.
Magician: One who deceives almost everyone.
It was the first time the boy, abandoned by the world, had felt a desire
to protect something.
"CPR."
Shirone gently placed the job card on Kai's folded hands.
"Wait. I'll come back for you soon."
What are the chances that one of the hundred cards born from the harmony
of Whiteblack would be CPR?
"No, it has to be there."
As Shirone's body transformed into a flash of light, he soared into the
night sky, determined.
