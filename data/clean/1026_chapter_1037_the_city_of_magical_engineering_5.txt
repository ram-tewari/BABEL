The City of Magical Engineering (5)

"Did you really just suggest pulling out all my hair? No way am I going

'No way! I'd rather bite my tongue and die than be a slave for life!
Should I just go back? There's probably no one in this hell who can use this thing anyway.'
As she stood there, her expression constantly shifting, Ricera, who had

"Make up your mind already. Are we going or not? I don't want to get

Seeing the humans embedded alive in Laviette's buildings only made

"I'm thinking, so shut your mouth before I embed you in a wall right

In front of the duke's castle, Lethe turned her head.
"What was that?"
The armored knight Kimara also sensed the unusual impact in the
sound.
"It seems they've found the intruder. Croseide must have been

"Really?"
Since Croseide was the strongest of the Seven Generals in terms of sheer
power, Lethe opened the door without concern.
All the demons working in the castle came out to greet her, but the duke

Of course, Marguerite was a veteran among veterans who could share a

'Something is definitely up.'
Ignoring the president of the Fire Management Company was
unusual.
"Where is the duke?"

"He is in the laboratory. You know, once he goes in, there's no

"Yes, ma'am."
"Are you mistaken? Just because I'm being nice, does it seem like

"Who would dare disrespect the Mother of Hell? I am merely stating

He was prepared for annihilation and was passing the responsibility to

"Sigh."
Lethe's eyebrows furrowed.
"Why are you being like this? Are we enemies? Bielman, it's me,

"Yahweh. He's here, isn't he? And Rian too. Has anyone seen them?

As she looked around, the demons swallowed nervously.
There were few in hell who could deceive the president of the Fire

Yet Bielman remained calm, and Lethe couldn't find any lies in the
demons' eyes.
"Is Yahweh really not here? No, that can't be. They've already

Bielman's gaze turned cold.
"I'm sorry, Lady Lethe."
No demon in the castle where Marguerite resided knew Shirone's

"I serve the duke."
Because Bielman had killed them all.
"This is a serious matter. Mistakes are not allowed when the fate

"Move."
It wasn't anger at insubordination, but sadness over the years of mutual

"If this is how it's going to be, I won't be polite anymore. I need

"Move. That's an order."
"You know where the laboratory is. There's no need to take a long

"No, I don't want to. You move."

"Kimara."

"Yes, Lady Lethe."

'What's happening? Why is Bielman clashing pointlessly with Lady

When even the Seven Generals of the duke didn't know what was happening,

"This is your last chance. Move."

Ten seconds.
It was the only time Lethe could wait, reminiscing about eons of

Kimara made his choice.
'I am the duke's knight.'

"Fools!"

Kimara didn't ask.
'If the duke wishes... Kimara will ask nothing.'

"The president of the Fire Management Company is
