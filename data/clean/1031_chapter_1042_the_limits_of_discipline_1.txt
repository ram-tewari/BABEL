The Limits of Discipline (1)

The violent temperament he had shown in the tent was now controlled by

"If you say so, then you must be of royal blood. But I'm a warrior

Moonom's evolution was based on mutations.
"I overcame a 2.7% probability to reach the fourth-generation

'It was an enlightened strike. And…'

Even Son Yu-jung, who feared nothing in the world, felt uneasy in the

"You're certainly tough. But…"

"If I keep hitting, you'll break eventually."

Because it wasn't the truth.

A god?
