Chapter 690:
The Pupil of God (2)
A surge of light erupted from Mayray's slender, elongated body, shooting into the sky in
an instant.
Following the pillar of light, she ascended to a dizzying height, her fingers bending
like rubber as she formed a hand seal.
"What is that?"
As everyone looked up, her incarnation grew to a size that could blot out the sky.
**Terraforce Incarnation—Great Judge of All Sounds.**
A size rivaling the labyrinth incarnation she had used against Satan in Heaven.
A massive eye without eyelids, with a map reflected in it, spread like a fan across her
forehead.
"Now, I shall distinguish your good and evil."
The Terraforce, as guardians of order, possessed the ability to collect and analyze all
sounds in this world.
Just as humans couldn't be discussed without visual function, sound was the truth and
core of this world to the Terraforce.
And now, every sound on the planet where Shirone lived was being collected in Mayray's
ears.
"The wisdom of the Chief Justice. The order of law."
Light flickered in Mayray's eyes as the same standard was applied to all humans alive
today.
The numbers of good and evil were tallied rapidly.
**Good: 0**
**Evil: 8,724**
Through the true power of the Divine Frequency, the voices of over 3 billion souls
resonated in their minds.
**Yakma Republic.**
—I alone want to be superior. I wish everyone would look up to me.
