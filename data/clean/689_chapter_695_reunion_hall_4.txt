Chapter 695:
Reunion Hall (4)
The fake oracle's words grew faster.
Though it was just a jumble of sounds, the effect was impressive—the devotees shed tears.
"Um ssala um!"
"Um ssala um!"
Everyone repeated the priest's words in emotional unison, but Shirone's face flushed with
anger.
The words were lewd and vile.
"Before Radum, I saw a lot of guys like this."
Goblins living in the woods were well-versed in human heretical rituals.
"Usually after this, they do something pretty wild, right?"
The cult leader's only desire was to dominate and indulge in humans.
He was probably a mage who knew a few spells—enough to dazzle ordinary people.
Rian frowned.
"The world has all kinds of people. Why not just ignore them and move on? Whether it's
the deceiver or the deceived…."
"But there's a new devotee today."
From the fake oracle's words, Shirone had heard it.
"Probably that woman at the back…."
She appeared to be in her late twenties, awkwardly mimicking the others' movements and
words.
"Ah, Great Teraphos, please grant me…."
"Emma, if you're so half-hearted, Teraphos won't grant you an oracle."
"Ah, I'm sorry."
"You must pray with your whole heart. Like this. Great Teraphos, grant me an oracle!"
A gaunt middle-aged woman shouted, and as if competing, the others prayed louder.
Amid the heated atmosphere, the cult leader seized his moment.
"Bring forth the sacrifice!"
From a cave behind the altar, hooded figures carried in a goat, gagged and tied to a
thick wooden plank.
The goat trembled on the altar, but the priest mercilessly slit its belly and pulled out
its heart.
"Uooooooh!"
The sight of blood sent the devotees into a frenzy.
"This blood shall purify us. Bare yourselves before the god."
Men and women began stripping, but Emma, even in her daze, sensed something was wrong.
"What are you doing, Emma! If you don't hurry, all curses will fall upon you!"
"But, ma'am, I'm not…."
Long-time devotees who'd given their bodies and souls to the priest were pale and
emaciated.
To them, Emma was fresh blood, and they glared at her with silent pressure.
"I think I need to step in."
Shirone agreed with Rian.
"Let's cover our faces. Just in case."
"Wait. I have a better idea."
With that, Kido leaped down.
"A goblin's better than a human."
His nimble body landed silently, and he bellowed,
"Do you know where you've wandered, you fools?"
The devotees turned, and the priest's face twisted.
"A g-goblin!"
Already half-naked, their fear was palpable.
Of course, nothing whetted a goblin's appetite like naked humans, but their bony, bloated
bodies killed his hunger.
"This is goblin territory! Scram, or I'll chop off your legs one by one and taste them!"
"Goblin territory?"
It was half-true.
If this area wasn't managed by the kingdom, it was cozy enough to think of finding kin.
"R-run!"
The cave echoed with their screams as the priest roared.
"What are you doing! Teraphos will punish you!"
The momentary pause let the priest raise his hands.
"Just one goblin! Witness Teraphos's power! Uooooooh!"
Torches lining the cave flew up, converging above his head.
Shirone watched calmly.
'Pathetic.'
The torches' range was likely the limit of the priest's Spirit Zone, and even dozens
combined produced weak flames.
'A third-rate mage.'
That's why he was stuck playing cult leader.
"Die, filthy goblin!"
As the priest thrust his arms forward—
"Huh? What?"
His nose split, and his eyeballs were slashed.
"Aaaah! My eyes!"
His eyelids couldn't even close—it was that fast.
"Priest!"
The hooded figures drew daggers and charged.
"Loyalty? Or…."
Comradeship in debauchery?
Surrounded, Kido simply rolled on the ground and sprang up like a spring,
scissors-kicking.
The hooded figures clutched their severed legs and screamed.
"Aaaah! My legs!"
Kido shouted at the devotees.
"Scram! If I see you in goblin territory again, I'll boil you alive!"
"Kyaaaah! Help!"
Snapped out of their trance, the devotees fled the cave, not even covering themselves.
"Humans are weird."
Clicking his tongue, Kido looked down at the weeping priest.
"This guy's bad, so can I kill him?"
Though he'd resolved to follow human customs during his travels, Kido's standards were
low.
"Sp-spare me. Please… just my life!"
"Sorry. Eating you alive might make them mad…."
He planned to kill the priest, eat his flesh, and find a way out.
"Aaaah! Hel—! Gack!"
The priest's body suddenly convulsed, and sensing something amiss, Kido froze.
"We meet again, Hexa."
