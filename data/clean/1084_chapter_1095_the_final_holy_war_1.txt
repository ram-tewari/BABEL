North Pole, Ivory Tower.
The tower was enormous, and in the small village on the 387th floor,
about 80 people lived.
Inside a small hut, a woman who had been in a deep sleep slowly opened
her eyes.
Ivory Tower resident, Bipa.
In High Gear, she was a tutorial manager using the gear mark.
"What's the point of a meeting?"
She had no intention of attending.
Nevertheless, the reason she logged out was because Yahweh's actions had
moved her heart.
As she stepped out of the hut, other residents looked at her in
surprise.
"Bipa? What's going on? You're coming outside?"
She had been holed up for months, stockpiling food and water, without
stepping outside.
"What's the situation?"
The residents squinted their eyes but couldn't avoid revealing the
significant event.
"See for yourself."
When they reached the outer wall they pointed to, the holographic
curtain disappeared, revealing a mechanical iron wall.
At the 387th floor of the Ivory Tower, even the blizzards raging in the
North Pole were beneath them.
She looked through the window.
At the horizon, flames that evaporated the cold were blazing in the
Kingdom of Corona.
"Demons."
Click. Click.
Her pupils rotated like a camera aperture, zooming in on the scene of
the Kingdom of Corona.
She saw the stars of the Ivory Tower and, among them, a demon swordsman
slaughtering numerous soldiers.
The army of hell, led by the first legion commander, Baal.
'They've finally come.'
The fact that the only legion commander who hadn't opened the demon
realm was targeting the North Pole was significant.
'Deadly Cross. That was probably the name.'
They intended to bring down the Ivory Tower.

Southern Continent, Kessia.

many shops open for business.
The place Shirone chose for the meeting was a franchise café run by
Golden Wheel.
"Welcome."
As he opened the door and entered, a dazed-looking employee greeted
Shirone.
He saw tables where nobles were seated, seemingly also under the
influence.
'The problem is…'
The group sitting in the corner of the shop didn't look much
different.
'This is quite a sight.'
Five High Gear operators had gathered for this meeting, and none made
eye contact.
It was the expected scene, given that they were socially inept
individuals, but it was still extreme.
"Oh? Could it be…?"

"Yahweh?"
"Lollipop?"
He knew some details about the operators but didn't reveal their real

"Wow, it's just like High Gear. Welcome. The atmosphere here is a

'There's the lollipop.'

The ones who caused trouble in High Gear, Operator and Number

The Middle East, Paras Kingdom
The Paras Kingdom had suffered significant damage during the world war
initiated by the Gustav Empire. The most devastating blow was the death of King Kasartak, who had ruled
Paras for 40 years.
However, as the cradle of ancient civilizations, the powerful Middle
Eastern nation would not fall so easily.
"It's time to depart."
In the ornate Grand Hall of the royal palace Delayma, a gentle yet
resonant voice echoed.
It was King Kitra, the youngest son of Kasartak. With dark eyeliner
accentuating his androgynous features, he was a striking figure.
"Your Majesty, preparations are complete. This Holy War will solidify
Paras as the ruling power of the world."
His ministers had absolute faith in him. Ascending the throne at a young
age, Kitra's intellect made him the perfect ruler for a kingdom that prided itself on its ancient
wisdom.
"Hmm…"
The serpent coiled around Kitra's neck flicked its tongue toward the
gathered ministers, silencing them.
"Summon Zulu."
The ministers hesitated, their heads snapping up."Your Majesty,
with all due respect, the ancient civilization of Paras has never granted access to foreigners—"
Hiss!
The snake let out a sharp, vibrating sound, causing the ministers' faces
to pale."Shh."
Kitra softly shushed the snake, which then turned its head to lick him
affectionately.As he savored the venom like a kiss, Kitra repeated his order."Bring
Zulu."
A soldier rushed out of the Grand Hall. Shortly after, the doors opened
to reveal Zulu, Shirone, Canis, and Arin entering together.

Unlike most rulers who feigned courage before stars of renown, Kitra's
