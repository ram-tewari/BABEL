Chapter 85:
Light and Darkness, Good and Evil (3)
'Hoho. Is this really a human's fist?'
A punch rivaling magic in power. And it wasn't just a single strike. Etella, holding her
breath, unleashed a barrage. Each blow to the dense darkness made her wrists ache, but the golem's pain was
far greater.
Arcane gritted his teeth and steadied his mind. Etella's punches weren't just
powerful—their impact was unique. Instead of breaking the surface, they generated shockwaves internally. The
problem was the interference from overlapping waves.
'Wave-type attacks. This is dangerous.'
Grrraaaah!
As the dark golem writhed, Etella's eyes flashed. Stepping in, she struck its solar
plexus with her palm. A massive ripple spread, triggering roughly 400 interference effects—400 times
stronger than her fist's impact.
The golem convulsed as if it had swallowed a bomb, its black body swelling. It was on the
verge of exploding.
"Grrrrr!"
Arcane grimaced, straining to hold on. If his Spirit Zone broke, the golem would
collapse. As the golem's tremors subsided, Etella bit her lip.
'Still not enough? I should've stacked more waves.'
The Yin-Yang Wave Fist used ripple waves, stacking them in the opponent's body before
delivering a final, explosive strike. Though it took time to reach peak power, the resulting impact was
terrifying.
But Arcane endured. The golem had withstood 400 times a Schema fist's force, but an
archmage's mental strength was no joke.
"Impressive, little one."
"Next time, it won't end like this. Give up now."
"Kekeke! You've still got fight left. Good. It's been a while since I've warmed up
properly."
Arcane raised his hand, and the dark spiders merged into the golem. Growing rapidly, it
soon pierced the forest canopy.
Etella felt dizzy. This was the true form of Arcane's infamous dark golem—capable of
destroying a small city in days.
"How's this for impressive? Keep pounding with your wave fist. My golem's abyss is deeper
than the sea."
A golem over 10 meters tall made wave propagation questionable. But Etella clenched her
fist until it might shatter. She would become the world's light, even if it meant death.
Grrraaaah!
The golem stomped forward. Despite its size, its speed was unchanged. Moving at the speed
of thought was why Dark Minions were classified as overpowered.
Boom!
The golem's fist smashed where Etella stood. Seismic waves toppled trees, and the ground
churned as if hundreds of moles were tunneling.
Boom! Boom! Boom!
Fists rained down, shaking the earth and devastating the surroundings. As the sixth
strike landed, Etella pivoted, running up the golem's arm. Reaching its shoulder, she crossed her arms and
leaped down. During the fall, fists too fast to see pummeled the golem.
Drrrrrrrrr!
The Yin-Yang Wave Fist's ultimate technique—Thousand-Armed Avalanche Thunder Strike.
"Grrrr!"
Sparks flared endlessly in Arcane's brain. Over 30 shocks per second flooded in. At 300
ripples per second, a 2-second fall would unleash 600 ripples—3,000 interference effects. But the
technique's essence lay in the final palm strike. If it landed, the interference count would reach…
'68,000.'
A force 68,000 times Etella's punch would explode internally. Arcane's hair stood on end,
but his lips curled into a grotesque smile.
"Kekeke! This feels like the old days."
He had no intention of fleeing. If the enemy was strong, he'd crush them with greater
force. How he'd missed this moment. The glory of clashing with countless geniuses flashed through his mind.
Grrraaaah!
The golem twisted like a pretzel. Etella, landing, pulled her hands to her sides. The
final strike—Thunderclap Palm—gathered in her palms. As the golem's rotating fist descended, she gritted her
teeth and thrust her palms forward.
'Thunderclap Palm!'
Whoooooosh!
A mushroom cloud rose from the mountainside.
Trees within 20 meters were uprooted, turning the lush forest into a wasteland.
***
Stars twinkled in the night sky. Etella blinked at the starlight filtering through the
branches.
"……."
How much time had passed?
Or how far had she been flung?
Every tree along her path was broken.
At the end of the devastation, Etella lay sprawled, unable to move a finger. Her muscles
twitched involuntarily. Her fingertips and toes were numb.
'Master…….'
Etella was out of the fight.
'Archmages are truly strong.'
Even her ultimate technique couldn't stop Arcane's Dark Minions.
Was this the power of a calamitous spell?
Guilt gnawed at her for failing to vanquish evil.
If only she'd stacked more waves, mastered the technique perfectly, or amplified the
Thunderclap Palm's power.
"Hic. Sob……."
Etella covered her eyes with trembling hands. Hot tears streamed down like a waterfall.
"I'm sorry, Master."
Her sobs echoed through the mountains.
