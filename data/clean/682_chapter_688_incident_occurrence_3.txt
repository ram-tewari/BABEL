Chapter 688:
Incident Occurrence (3)
Rufist used Fly Magic to locate Shirone and his group after confirming Shagal's
direction.
Though he hadn't worried about them when Saeghwa fell, he knew they wouldn't die so
easily.
"You managed to survive."
As Rufist landed and spoke, Shirone pouted and stuck out his lips.
But understanding Rufist's grief over losing Jane, he said nothing about Saeghwa.
"Mei Rey is acting strange."
There was no trace of humanity left in her face.
"Is she a Terrafor?"
Given his position, where he was briefed on the world's secrets, he wasn't shocked by
intelligent lifeforms from outer space.
But he needed to understand their intentions in manifesting through Mei Rey.
"What are you after?"
Mei Rey twitched as if collecting sounds, looking around before answering in human
language.
"Order."
"Order? What order?"
No further answer came. With the mission taking priority, Rufist dropped the matter.
"Fine, we move out now. Shagal has made his move. Ra Enemi is nearby."
Kido held out his hand. "Give me Nemesis too."
Rufist turned to him, and Shirone gave a brief explanation. "It's his sense of
taste."
Guessing Ra Enemi's intentions by now, Rufist handed over a spare Nemesis without
protest.
"Hmm, with this..."
As Kido slipped the ring onto his finger, his imagination materialized into reality,
revealing a scene from his goblin tribe.
'Nui.'
Kido's sister, clad in tattered skirts, tore into a pig's leg with one foot propped on a
rock.
Though her wild, fierce appearance might unsettle humans, Kido watched with a bright
smile.
"Huh."
Rufist spoke coldly. "Cut off your thoughts. Interference will be fatal in
battle."
The scenery vanished instantly.
The fact that Kido could regain his focus so quickly spoke volumes about his skill.
"Let's go. Follow Shagal."
The mages took to the skies, while Kido trailed them at a distance.
After three minutes of pursuit, they spotted Shagal, stabbing wildly in a frenzied state.
"Gyaaaaah!"
Though he seemed insane, his movements were genius, vividly conveying the presence of the
illusory Ra Enemi.
"Enter Nemesis's radius."
As they stepped within 20 meters of Shagal, Shirone's eyes widened at a sensation he had
never felt before.
'This is the scent of the incident.'
Like recalling the past from a specific smell, the scent of Ra Enemi that Shagal was
tracking wafted over strongly.
'But I can't tell where it is.'
Unlike sight, the detection ability of smell wasn't hindered by concealment or cover, but
it couldn't pinpoint an exact location.
**-I am here, Shirone.**
As the divine frequency resonated, Ra Enemi's voice reached them, and everyone turned
left.
"Ra Enemi!"
Shirone shouted toward the empty space, but the reply came from somewhere entirely
different.
**-What is the universe?**
"Show yourself! Appear before me!"
**-Why can I not become a god?**
"Kieeeeh!"
Kido slid past the spot where the voice had come from, swinging his spear, but it met no
resistance.
Shirone immediately shifted his gaze.
'It's useless. He exists only in the past.'
If the voices were surging from the past, there was no guarantee he was still there.
'But it's not the distant past.'
Shagal was still here, meaning Ra Enemi was within a second of the present—close enough
for Shirone's Time-Burst Flash to strike.
'Frenzy!'
Using the time-sense explosion, Shirone cast a spell, and a curtain of light enveloped
the entire 20-meter radius.
At the same time, Shagal sniffed the air and retreated.
'He dodged it. The magic definitely works.'
Shagal's immediate reaction meant Ra Enemi was lingering in a time very close to the
present.
"Where are you?! Come out!"
Then, someone touched Shirone's shoulder.
**-Answer the remaining question, Shirone.**
The cold voice, spoken right next to him, made Shirone's heart sink.
'He's behind me.'
Everyone sensed it through Nemesis, and Kido lunged, but Shagal arrived first.
"Dieeeeee!"
"Guh!"
Shirone twisted his body just in time to avoid the dagger.
"Snap out of it! If we don't work together—!"
"I'll kill him! I'll definitely kill him!"
As the deranged Shagal dashed away, Rufist, hovering in the air, shouted, "Follow
him! That's the way!"
The chase resumed, and Kido pressed close to Shirone.
"Wait until vision is added. He's manipulating incidents on a cosmic level. He can
probably calculate the movement of air molecules. We have to assume he's the strongest enemy."
"Even if he materializes, subduing him is another matter, right?"
Kido nodded. "Finding him isn't everything. If we can't subdue him, I can't eat him,
and then we won't understand Ra Enemi's intentions."
Rufist lowered his altitude. "We might not get many chances, so assume we only have
one. When the opportunity comes, don't miss it."
Shirone gritted his teeth and nodded. "Understood."
He wouldn't miss it.

Rian, Kuan, Venezia, and Etella raced through Radum's concealed facility zone.
'What a strange feeling.'
There was no destination.
Since Ra Enemi would be wherever they went, it didn't matter if they emptied their minds.
'That's what's ominous.'
Kuan glanced at Venezia.
'Why did Ra Enemi kill her brain?'
Perhaps Venezia's free will interfered with Ra Enemi's design.
'If so, the conclusion is that we must avoid meeting Ra Enemi...'
"No need to think about it."
Kuan turned to Etella. "No matter what decision we make, we can't tell if it's Ra
Enemi's intention or our own will. We're already consumed by the incident."
The loss of free will.
"So the only way is a direct approach."
The only method left was to meet Ra Enemi and erase his existence itself.
Having decided, Kuan stopped and slipped the Nemesis ring onto Venezia's finger.
Feeling Etella's gaze, he explained, "It belonged to Ms. Aria. Now, if Ra Enemi
approaches, we'll be able to see him with our eyes."
