Venezia was being held in a punishment cell beneath the mansion.
The room was barely one square meter, with no torchlight even on the

So this is the Tri-Brained woman.
When Shirone first heard of her, he had imagined a three-headed monster
and had braced himself accordingly. But when he actually saw her, she looked perfectly human.
Only her irises were entirely blue with no whites, and her forehead bore
a water-drop-shaped gemstone—not as decoration, but as a biological organ.
"You are Venezia, leader of Spectrum?"
At Lupist's question, her lips curled into a slight smile.
"You must be Lupist."
As far as he could recall, this was their first face-to-face
meeting.
The two of them spoke at the same time.
"How do you know me?"
Lupist remained silent, and Venezia continued.
"Yes. It's short-term precognition. I analyze the present based on the

That was how she'd unified numerous terrorist factions and protected the
demi-humans from the kingdom.
Rian asked, "Even so, can you really predict what someone will say
next?"
"That's because you think like a human. What I analyze isn't events, but
time. If you can distinguish between past and present, you can distinguish between present and future as
well."
Lupist asked, "When did you know I was coming?"
"About a minute ago, by your standards."
"Then you know why I came."
"To understand Ra Enemy's intent."
"You met him?"

"That's a terribly vague question. If I were sure of that, I wouldn't

"Is it true Ra Enemy exists only as an event?"
Venezia looked blank.
"Who are you?"
"I'm Arian Shirone—"
"No. When did you get here?"
Lupist's eyes sparkled.
"She didn't foresee him?"
Shirone's time explosion destroyed time itself, creating a completely
open-ended future.
It made sense the Tri-Brain couldn't perceive it.
"So you're the reason I'm here."
"No, I—"
Lupist interrupted.
"Explain it plainly. What is Ra doing?"
"No one in this world truly understands Ra's intent."

"Tri-Brains perceive the past, present, and future with three separate

"You mean they're already over."
"Yes. Humans who live within the flow of time can't distinguish that.
But my brain structure is different. In the brain that perceives my past, Ra Enemy remains vivid. But in

brain still performs predictions. Do you know what that means?"
Shirone said, "It means the present doesn't exist."
"Exactly. From a Tri-Brain's perspective, if the past changes the future

our will."
Human will exists only in the present.
So if you rob humanity of the present, the world will move entirely
according to Ra's will.
And that was exactly why Yolga gave her life to persuade Miro.
"All I can do is imprison myself so I won't be used by Ra. But my fellow

"The resistance will fight for freedom. Ra contacted many of our

"How can this be…?"
Now the reason the Ivory Tower was searching for Ra Enemy, despite not

Ra is like air.
Air that has permeated every part of human life.

So this is Shagall's true strength.

Shagall of the Rapid Blade.
