Heaven, Sixth Heaven, Zebul.
The city of angels, Zebul, has two main systems.
One is Inglis, where the Akashic Records are kept, and the other is the
Great World War, Archangel Cariel's laboratory.
The Great World War, which had been devastated by Shirone's Photon
Cannon, was now fully restored.
The central control system, a massive pillar, was the tallest structure
in the Great World War. Next to the pillar emitting multicolored light, a galactic telescope smoothly
observed the movements of the stars.
The Great World War had regained its former appearance. But Heaven had
not.
Since Shirone's departure, a wind of change had blown through First
Heaven, Shamain. The citizens no longer believed in the absolute correctness of the Doctrine of Unity,
and some radicals had emerged.
In response, the angels mobilized all their resources to break through
Miro's Time and Space.
Leading the charge was Archangel Cariel.
The Archangel of Creation, who had tried to cast the Elixir of Life on
Amy, Tess, and Arin, who had been captured by the fallen angel Ikasa.
The most intelligent of the angels, he analyzed the Meta Gate Shirone
had left behind when he returned to his original world.
Finally, he found the coordinates to the Land of Earth. But to get
there, they had to break through Miro's Time and Space.
"Even with the coordinates, we have to break through. But it's
tricky."
"Then what's the point of recovering the coordinates?"
Cariel turned at the majestic voice.
Recognizing the voice, he smiled. Another archangel, Uriel, clad in pure
white heavy armor, floated in the air.
Angels are taller than humans, but Uriel was the tallest among them,
standing at three meters. His chest armor was so thick it reached his chin.
Cariel flapped his wings of light once, rising to the top of the central
control system. A large screen was installed 40 meters high, displaying numerous numbers.
"Not necessarily. There are quite a few Nephilim in the Land of
Earth now. Even if we break Miro's Time and Space, we can set up a firewall. But with the coordinates,
we can bypass it."
"Break Miro's Time and Space? Wasn't that strategy dismissed long

Only Anke Ra could dismiss an archangel's proposal. Indeed, Anke Ra had
forbidden investigating the Land of Earth after the Final War was blocked by the Maze.
The archangels didn't know why, but they couldn't defy Anke Ra, who
governed the Akashic Records.
But Cariel didn't give up.
The decisive factor was Shirone's appearance. His presence changed the
citizens. And that change was rapidly amplifying over time.
"I don't intend to defy Ra's will. It's a precaution. The Land of
Earth will be conquered someday. It might be too late to prepare then."
"So you're not certain about this experiment either."
Cariel smirked.
He had never been uncertain before. But every attempt had been thwarted

But today, she would have to give up.
Miro's Time and Space would be breached. Anke Ra wouldn't want it,
but... what could he do? It was fate.
Cariel flew past the galactic telescope to the western wall. Uriel

"Here it is, my masterpiece for today."
