Chapter 442:
20 minutes earlier.
After escaping from the glass chamber in the isolation room, Garas
attempted to mate with every creature there.
This event aligned perfectly with Plu's intentions, but there was one
overlooked fact.
Even Plu, who knew how terrifying Garas was, couldn't accurately gauge
its reproductive drive.
Having been confined in a narrow glass chamber for days, Garas was in an
extremely anxious state, and its reproductive drive upon gaining freedom was 200,000 times that of a
human.
The period for the genetic information Garas injected into other species
to grow from embryo to adult was typically 14 hours.
However, driven by the urgency of passing on its genes, the birthing
time was reduced to just 3 seconds.
"Kiaaa! Kiaaa!"
As the giant creature let out a laborious scream, dozens of Garas burst
from its belly.
The wriggling offspring grew into adults within 10 seconds and attacked
other creatures.
It was literally exponential.
Garas's offspring were born in every apartment unit, and soon the
isolation room reached its capacity.
With a loud bang, the iron door flew open, and Garas of all shapes and
sizes flooded the corridor like water bursting from a dam.
The creatures, forming a torrent, collided at the corridor intersections
and, following the principles of fluid dynamics, began spreading throughout the entire command
center.
"Huff! Huff!"
The corridor Garas had swept through was left with only the gruesome
remains of its victims.
Only the bald man who had been attacked by Garas was left, leaning
against the wall, gasping for breath.
His belly was swollen as if filled with fluid, and the bloating was
increasing at a rate of more than twice per second.
"Huff! Huff!"
His trembling hand grasped a dagger on the floor.
Lifting it with both hands, he reversed his grip and aimed it at his own
belly, his face contorted with fear.
"Ugh!"
He had to stab himself. It was the best choice he could make for
himself.
But in the end, the man grimaced and threw the dagger away.
"Aaaargh!"
As his belly exploded, a black creature, curled up, spilled out.
When its bent spine fully extended, it reached a length of 1 meter. It
supported itself on the floor with its thin arms and backward-bent legs, sticking out its long
tongue.
Its face was distorted as if struck by a hammer, it had no eyes, and its
brain was exposed on top of its head.
"Kikiki!"
Garas, prowling like a cheetah, detected the scent of a living creature
with its keen sense of smell.
"I… am… the king of the species."
Crack!
With four cracks in the floor, Garas's body disappeared.
Shirone and Plu ran down the corridor without daring to look
back.
No matter where they went, Garas was there, and they could feel their
escape routes narrowing.
"Senior! What do we do? We're going to be surrounded at this
rate."
A horde of Garas appeared in their path.
Plu stopped and swung Phoenix, sending a vortex of fire forward.
"Kiaaa! Kiaaa!"
The heat melted Garas's skin like porridge.
But soon, the wounds began to regenerate with Kenser's ability.
"Damn! Regeneration this time?"
Garas wasn't inherently a strong creature.
But its bizarre reproductive ability, capable of producing offspring
within 10 seconds, combined with the environment of a lab filled with various isolated creatures, had
created an absurd species.
As the number of individuals exploded in the confined space, some
mutations even attempted to breed with their own kind, making Garas stronger and more evolved.
Shirone gritted his teeth with a grim expression.
He could tell what kind of creatures Raycis had collected just by
looking at Garas's form.
"I'll handle this."
An umbra formed before Shirone's eyes, crushing the charging
Garas.
He had learned that umbra was effective against Kenser's traits, but it
was just luck.
Every creature had a natural enemy, and Garas, thriving in an
environment with infinite mutations, was becoming increasingly dangerous the longer they delayed.
"Kirik! Kirik!"
Mucus dripped from the ceiling, transforming into dozens of sacs that
hung down.
As the eyes that emerged from the sacs activated Antithesis, the two
were immobilized.
"Ugh!"
At the same time, a transparent Garas with the abilities of the
invisible ghost Hororos bit Plu's calf.
Seeing the clear bite marks on her white skin, Shirone heightened his
mental transcendence and counterattacked.
He crushed the snake-like transparent Garas with his tendrils and fired
Photon Cannons at the ceiling, causing the walls to collapse and block the exit.
Without time to think about the aftermath, Shirone checked on
Plu.
If Hororos's traits had mixed with those of a toxic creature, it would
be disastrous.
"Senior, are you okay?"
Plu looked at her bleeding leg. She could still move.
But seeing Garas grow stronger by the moment, she had already made up
her mind.
"I'll lure them away. You go find Raycis. If we don't stop her, the
mission fails."
Shirone couldn't respond.
He knew it was the only option, but leaving Plu as Garas's prey was
unbearable.
"Senior, I…"
"Hurry! You promised me! We agreed not to hesitate when we had to
make a choice!"
Shirone clenched his fists and shouted.
"How can I leave you behind! Let me…"
Slap! Shirone's head turned with the sound.
Plu's cold eyes met his.
"Go. You can do it. You know that, right?"
Shirone wasn't hurt. The pain in his chest was far greater than the
sting on his cheek.
"Take down Raycis. Then come back for me."
Both knew that wouldn't happen.
That fact was what saddened Shirone the most.
"The longer you hesitate, the less time you'll have to save me. If
you come back and find I died 5 seconds earlier, could you bear it?"
"Senior."
"Two seconds have passed."
Shirone closed his eyes tightly and stood up. For the first time in his
life, he lied to someone about their fate.
"I'll definitely come back."
Shirone pushed off the ground and ran, turning the corner.
Almost simultaneously, a horde of Garas came charging from the other
side.
Plu was no longer afraid.
Instead, she felt a sense of relief from sending Shirone away, and her
expression was more serene than ever.
'Thank you. You're a good kid.'
A dry smile formed on her lips.
"Goodbye, Shirone."
Plu stood up, gripping Phoenix, her expression fiercer than ever.
Smoke rose from her body as if she were about to explode.
Amplifying her magic to the point where she didn't care if she burned
herself, she extended Phoenix.
It was the most powerful magic possessed by Plu, an officially certified
8th-grade mage.
Hellfire!
The blazing flames filled the corridor like a cloud.
Tears wouldn't stop flowing from Shirone's eyes.
But the sorrow that felt like it would crush his chest turned into cold
anger as he reached the lab with the Fuzix Machine.
With eyes that could cut through steel, Shirone glared at the lab and
slowly opened the door.
In the silence, only the sound of bubbling could be heard.
All he saw were the bodies of researchers.
The glass spheres of the Fuzix Machine, which Raycis had shown through
memory vision, contained various creatures.
Shirone stopped in front of one of the glass spheres.
Inside was Ikasa's body, torn to pieces as if chewed by teeth.
Looking at her upside-down face, Shirone's eyes filled with bitter
emotions.
'Ikasa.'
He hadn't kept his promise.
But he had to set those thoughts aside for now.
Without looking back, Shirone asked.
"Are you satisfied now? Is this the result you wanted?"
Raycis, with lips as red as blood, walked in calmly.
"You've trapped yourself. You shouldn't have messed with
Garas."
Shirone slowly turned around.
"You're in the same situation. Give up now. The command center will
be shut down."
"Kekekeke! Do you really think that? Do you think I did this just
because I was weak and longed for strength? Not at all."
Crack! Crack!
Raycis's bones twisted, and her muscles swelled like balloons.
"I am already the strongest creature."
Her height grew to 2 meters, then 3 meters, until she touched the
ceiling.
Her entire body was covered in red skin, with intestines-like tails
tangled in bundles, and her muscular body was covered with various creatures' eyes, noses, mouths, and
teeth, all moving.
An indescribable grotesqueness.
A body filled with absolute malice, unrelated to function.
"Shirone, become one with me."
As Raycis's androgynous voice echoed, Shirone reflexively fired a Photon
Cannon.
But his target wasn't Raycis; it was the Fuzix Machine.
Boom! Boom-boom!
Raycis moved faster than the Photon Cannon, blocking the Fuzix
Machine.
Despite the powerful impact, her solid body didn't budge.
Satan's Law - Incarnation of Evil.
For a human, it was a divine transcendence that would cause the body to
collapse, but Raycis's durability, combined with various creatures, was comparable to an angel's.
"Raaaargh!"
As Raycis spewed powerful flames, Shirone teleported to evade and
countered with Armand's tendrils and Photon Cannons.
The lab turned into chaos in an instant.
Raycis didn't leave the Fuzix Machine, but even so, Shirone was the one
being pushed back.
With flames and lightning mixed together, it became impossible to evade.
Raycis lunged, grabbing Shirone by the neck and slamming him against the wall.
"Ugh!"
Without Armand, his neck would have already snapped.
Tendrils from his robe attacked, but they couldn't penetrate her
skin.
"Kekeke, did you think you could win? Just because you're a
Nephilim, the Light of Zone 73, did you think you could defeat me?"
Raycis's eyes glowed gold.
"I am the Incarnation of Evil, Satan!"
A new consciousness invaded Shirone's mind.
In the fiery space, Satan's incarnation and Shirone's incarnation
appeared, mirroring reality.
Shirone's golden wings trembled as if they would break, while Satan's
grotesque face loomed closer.
"Poor lamb, a Nephilim is no match for me."
"Ugh!"
Shirone maximized his incarnation's power through mental
transcendence.
Satan's face briefly contorted, but he soon focused his strength in his
arms.
"Ugh!"
Shirone's back bent painfully, his legs flailing.
Only the extreme of good could counter the extreme of evil.
As a human, Shirone couldn't reach that level.
'Is this how I die?'
His incarnation's presence grew faint, his strength waning.
His physical body was likely on the verge of having its neck
snapped.
'Plu, Senior…'
What was she doing now, surrounded by Garas?
'I can't end it like this!'
Shirone gritted his teeth and invoked his incarnation again.
He had to fight to the end, for the senior who had thrown herself into
hell.
"How dare you!"
As Shirone's resistance grew stronger, Satan's arm muscles
bulged.
Flash!
Simultaneously, Shirone's body was enveloped in pure white light, and
another incarnation layered over him.
It was Armand, integrated with the Ultima System.
With mind and body unified into a single signal, Shirone was pushed to a
level he hadn't reached before.
Mental Transcendence - Unity of Mind and Body.
"Raaaargh!"
As Satan's roar echoed, Shirone's body transformed into light and
exploded.
