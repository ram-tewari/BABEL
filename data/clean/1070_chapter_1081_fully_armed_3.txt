Chapter 1081:
Fully Armed (3)
While Shirone was lost in thought, the mafia group was having a secret
conversation.
What are we going to do? Stick to the plan?
I feel a bit bad. We got a lot from the settlement. Do we
really need to go this far?
We have no choice. It's the East Country. Someone stirred
up the forum, and public opinion turned against us.
Expressing their intent to settle through the news was effective, but
the problem was the West Country's exclusive forum.
People who disapproved of helping an East Country user began to slander
the mafia group.
There were even rumors that they were spies for the East Country, and
some even called for their assassination.
If the West Country steps on us, there's nothing we can
do. What's the point of equipping good parts if we can't hunt?
Right. And if a large guild issues an assassination
order, we'll have to quit High Gear.
Having reached this conclusion, the mafia group looked back at Shirone,
who was deep in thought.
Sona spoke.
"How about turning off Donatello? It consumes quite a bit of
power."
"Ah, right."
Yahweh2's power was at 16%.
'I'll need to use a backup charger to get back.'
As Shirone prepared to leave, the mafia group closely observed Yahweh2's
movements.
Did he turn it off?
I can't tell. There's no sign at all.
Since he handled Donatello so skillfully, there was no way to confirm it
from the outside.
Let's just do it. On three, we shoot.
Timing their actions through communication, they all aimed their weapons
at Yahweh2's head.
Gunshots rang out.
Shirone, who had already leaped, rolled quickly on the white sand and
knelt on one knee.
The mafia clicked his tongue and asked.
"Did you keep Donatello on?"
Shirone remained silent.
He had seriously considered the possibility of the mafia group's
betrayal after receiving Fermi's communication.
'They wanted to check if I was safe.' A victory pose?
'The only way to communicate with me when direct communication was
impossible.'
There were likely things happening outside that Shirone wasn't aware
of.
What could it be?
'A traitor in the West Country. News, or the forum…'
It was a conclusion that came easily with a bit of thought.
Dex Sensibility spoke.
"Judging by your reaction, no explanation is needed. Still, thanks
for the clean settlement. So, can you just die? We won't touch your loot."
After all, it was just 100-level parts, and legendary items couldn't be
dropped.
"If you want to kill me, go ahead."
Shirone said.
"Mutual trust lasts only until the transaction is complete. We've
settled, so now we're just enemies."
Activating Michelangelo, Shirone smiled.
"Let's start, High Gear."
The mafia group thought.
'He's not a bad guy.'
Even if the conditions were unfavorable to him, he clearly distinguished
right from wrong.
'He makes people feel ashamed. He must be even more impressive in
reality. But because of that…'
The mafia group aimed their guns again.
'I won't feel guilty.'
Firepower exceeding level 200 rained down, and Yahweh2 jumped through
space in an instant.
'Damn! That technique…!'
The impact was different from what they saw in the video.
The white sand beach of Moon Island was filled with smoke and haze, and
gunfire continued incessantly.
A flash hit a comrade directly.
Boom!
The power equivalent to a missile destroyed the machine, and the
remaining six increased their output even more.
Turn on infrared! Securing visibility is the
priority!
Shirone puffed his cheeks.
'Phew. The 150-level difference is tough.'
If it were a boss creature, he could read the pattern, but users'
patterns were in the realm of quantum mechanics.
The gap was so large that it couldn't be bridged without the senses of a
bat or temporal explosion.
But because of that, the mafia group was shocked.
'What is this?'
He dodged most bullets, and even when they tried to suppress him with
output, he jumped through space.
'Damn it! Having 15 more selective options isn't even an
advantage.'
Meanwhile, the counterattacks continued, and another comrade
fell.
Hey! Let's use grenades too! How can we win when he keeps
firing unlimited attacks?
Wait. There's one chance. It's not unlimited.
The power limit is inevitable. We'll catch him when he
tries to use the backup charger.
The battle continued, and two more comrades died.
Hey! What are we going to do?
Wait.
The mafia was just as anxious, but from now on, it was a test of
patience.
A red signal appeared in Shirone's augmented reality.
'Remaining power: 2%.'
Knowing what the enemies were aiming for, he held out as long as
possible, but he couldn't delay any longer.
"Damn!"
Throwing a flashbang to cause an explosion, Shirone moved behind a rock
and took out the backup charger.
Now!
The mafia, Dex Sensibility, and Sona's machines all opened, and the
equipped grenades were launched.
'Homing communication.'
The grenades, trailing smoke, flew towards Shirone's hiding spot and
suddenly exploded.
"Huh?"
At the same time, Shirone, who had equipped the backup charger, emerged
from behind the rock.
"Good! Let's fight properly…!"
As the smoke cleared, a slender machine was bent over in a
sword-wielding stance.
"Operator?"
The skill that split all the grenades with a black sword was beyond
awe-inspiring.
"R-really, the Operator."
Slowly straightening up and sheathing her sword, she looked at Shirone
and spoke.
"We finally meet?"
Though tense because the Operator was from the West Country, she dashed
towards the mafia group.
"Uh…"
Before he could finish speaking, her fist connected squarely with the
mafia's face.
Crunch!
With a critical hit, his face was shattered, and Dex Sensibility and
Sona were thrown to the sides.
What the heck? Why is she attacking us?
Who knows? Maybe it's an assassination order. Just
run!
The Operator, who had been watching them leisurely, drew her sword
again.
"Collapse."
As the sword struck the sand, a spherical magnetic field began pulling
the machines towards it.
"Aaah!"
Though only a meter in diameter, Dex Sensibility and Sona were dragged
at terrifying speed and collided.
Boom!
The impact was so strong that their limbs were torn off, leaving Shirone
chilled to the bone.
'It's like a black hole.'
With only his face remaining, Dex Sensibility asked.
"Why… why are you…"
"I'll end it here. I won't assassinate you, but don't betray the
West Country again. You can post it on the forum if you want."
Hearing this, Dex Sensibility fell silent, and the deceased also
disappeared quietly.
With only their loot left behind, the Operator turned to Shirone.
With a pop, she vanished.
'Behind!'
Following Donatello's detected position, Shirone quickly turned to find
the Operator's face suddenly close.
"Ugh!"
More embarrassed than scared, Shirone shrank back but soon
marveled.
'She's incredibly fast.'
This was the speed of the top-ranked player.
"Let's go somewhere quiet."
Without waiting for a reply, the Operator lifted Shirone's machine
effortlessly.
"Huh?"
It was his first time being held like this by a woman, and it felt quite
strange.
As he looked up at her beautiful face from her embrace, he momentarily
lost himself.
Boom!
With immense power, she launched herself into the sky like a
rocket.
In one leap, she landed on the highest cliff of Moon Island and set
Shirone down.
"I saw the news." Shirone turned his head from admiring the
view.
"Oh, don't worry too much. I had some misconceptions too. You and
I…"
The Operator pointed to the ground.
"Apologize to me directly."
Whoosh, the wind blew.
'She's definitely unique.'
Having no reason not to apologize again, Shirone spoke willingly.
"I'm sorry for my harsh words back then. After playing High Gear
myself, I understand what you meant."
Though she only subtly withdrew her hand in response, she seemed
pleased.
"I'm sorry too. For underestimating you."
"No problem."
"I knew about you. Arian Shirone. Graduate of Alpheas Magic School.
Called Yahweh of this era."
Shirone flinched in surprise.
"Could you be someone I know…"
"No. This is our first meeting. But I've seen you in dreams. You
appear in many people's dreams. Michelangelo was created based on that image."
The Operator looked at the moon.
"Creating a world requires a lot of inspiration. Since I don't go
outside, I get most of my inspiration from people's dreams. Though I dislike dreamers, High Gear itself
is illegal anyway."
"Why don't you want to go outside?"
Without bringing her into reality, exploring the apocalypse would be
impossible.
"I woke up every day to my parents fighting. Their hatred for each
other was so intense that they didn't care about me at all. Or maybe that hatred was directed at me
because having me defined them as a couple. I bought a separate house and set up a workshop. I could
earn money using Under Coder."
Shirone listened silently.
"I hated reality so much that I needed a perfect world to escape
to. That's when I started designing High Gear. Do you know what's most horrifying? No matter how long
you live here, when you wake up, hardly any time has passed in reality. So I became more reluctant to go
out and eventually became the top ranker."
She turned back to Shirone.
"I know there are far worse things happening in reality than what
I'm going through. But do I have to suffer while caring about all that? Even if I should care, why don't
my parents care about my suffering?"
"No one blames you. There's no such thing as deserving to
suffer."
The Operator seemed to appreciate Shirone's words and continued softly
with her small mouth.
"The person who gave you a free pass in the West Country is
probably Little Witch? Her true identity is likely Kashan's former empress."
"…Yes."
If she could hack dreams, lying wouldn't work on her.
"A guild member was attacked in reality. It may seem simple but
it's incredibly difficult to synchronize with the outside world from Under Coder."
She must have used time magic.
"She probably knows my location too. Being ruthless, she might kill
me."
"Don't worry. She won't seek your consent through such means. As
per our initial agreement."
"Hmph, whatever. Let her do as she pleases. Ignoring it isn't
because I'm scared. One minute in reality is almost 30 minutes here. If I'm injured outside, I might
miss the Solar War."
"You really love High Gear."
For the first time, a smile appeared on the Operator's lips.
"I was honestly happy when you praised my world. It felt comforting
somehow. I'll give Michelangelo and Donatello to you. Do your best until the Solar War."
"If I win, you'll help me?"
"That won't happen but…"
The Operator slowly turned towards Shirone.
"If I win, will you live with me?"
"Huh?"
"I'll give you everything you want to see, have, or do. I can
create anything for you. You're struggling so much in reality. Let's travel beautiful places together
and play High Gear too. Time here is slower than in reality. You don't have to worry about others'
opinions. Don't you want to take a break from everything for a while?"
The reason he couldn't outright refuse was because what she said was
true.
Shirone shook his head.
"Sorry. Your world is beautiful but it doesn't feel real to me. My
heart belongs in reality."
"…I see."
She accepted it without resistance.
"I saw it in dreams. You fought fiercely to save the real world. To
you, everything here must seem fake just as reality seems fake to me."
Slowly approaching him, she tilted her head and kissed him on the
lips.
After a brief kiss and stepping back, Shirone looked at her
questioningly with his eyes.
"It's a reward for your praise. Everyone enjoys themselves freely
in High Gear but I've never done this for anyone else before. Think of it as respect from this fake
world to you."
Leaping high into the air while looking down at Shirone, she disappeared
into the moon halo.
"See you at the Solar War."
