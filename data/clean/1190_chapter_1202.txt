The Underworld.

Son Yoo-jung was surprised at her own breakthrough.
'Why?'
Because it was right.
If one sat alone in a quiet room, closed their eyes, and reflected, they
would always find the same answer—what is truly right.
'This is wisdom.'
If the methods of evil are easy yet complicated, then the ways of good

And once that simplicity is fully accepted into one's way of life,

This was something…
That evil could never possess. A sacred realm that existed only for the

'If I stay here, I'll lose track of Yahweh. I have to stop him from

Even if it meant abandoning the System Control Branch.

"I won't."
Not anymore.

"Boss!"

"What is that?"
A demon? Or a god?

"You pathetic creations dare to interfere with Hell's overseer!"

'Is this the true form of Hell's Mother, Lethe?'

'Yahweh, binding me here won't help you achieve your goal.'

"Because now, I can finally say it."
No matter what happened before her eyes…
"The one who is right—"

Ymir, Deep Layer 1.

A black hole.
The light flowing along its edges could not send a signal
anywhere.
If the sun symbolized law, then the ultimate extreme of the

Arius spoke.
"If light cannot move, it means there are no signals. In other

"That's right. Every form of matter asserts its existence within

This was the primal subconscious. Ymir's words were the fundamental

"How is existence expressed?"

"Weight?"
That was merely a human perspective.
"Hardness?"
Not a perfect definition.
"Existence."

"How much can one assert their existence within time and space? If

"There are things even stronger."
The assertion of existence.
No law could prevent Ymir from doing whatever he wanted.
As he took a step forward, the light of the sky was sucked into the

The outer world.

"The black hole is a phenomenon that represents Ymir's unique mind,

The singularity—an infinite density with no volume.

"What we seek is inside."
"Can you do it?"
If Arius could understand the mind of a target, he could plunder it—but

"I don't mind dying if I go in, but even then, I doubt I can steal

"Understood."
What was there to fear?
Miro's mind was a perfect work of art.
Even if Gaold had taken her flesh, her blood, and even her heart, Arius

"I'll let you go."

There would be no pursuit.

Inside that black void, which had pierced even Ymir's primal

What would be waiting for him?

"Aaaaaah!"

"It's fine. This isn't my physical body."
This was still a dream.

If he could glimpse Ultima's true form, his chances of plundering it

Light.

A massive, overwhelming radiance.
