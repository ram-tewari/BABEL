Search algorithm terminated.

Strike approved.

Eliminate the subjects.

Infinite Judicial Halo - Valhalla Action.
Ikasa's unique ability, Valhalla Action, has the power to reverse cause
and effect.
When she desires something, the result is realized on the condition that
she repays the time and effort required for the action later.
For example, if she desires to go from point A to point B, Ikasa can
reach the distance that would normally take 10 seconds instantly.
However, if there are obstacles between points A and B, she must also
pay the cost of bypassing or destroying them.
If it takes 5 minutes to destroy the obstacles to reach point B, Ikasa,
upon arrival, will be incapacitated for 5 minutes and 10 seconds to repay the cause.
Calculating this causality is the core of Valhalla.
When you press a balloon with your finger, the opposite side bulges.
When she sets the desire A, the result B automatically follows.
Therefore, desires beyond Valhalla's calculation ability cannot be
realized in reality.
For example, if she desires to enter a rock, it is impossible because
there is no space inside the rock.
However, if there is any space inside the rock, even if Ikasa is unaware
of it, it will be realized.
This means that Valhalla's computational system is influenced by the
Akashic Records, encompassing the whole rather than mathematical truth.
Similarly, desiring to cut off a human's head cannot be realized.
Valhalla cannot calculate a human's future actions, thus preventing the
"action."
