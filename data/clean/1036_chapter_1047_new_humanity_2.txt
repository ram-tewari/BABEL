New Humanity (2)

groove, as if a giant snowball had rolled across the ground.

A red-skinned demon was approaching with his hands behind his

'If someone with ill intent tries to exploit her, she'll become a deadly

Son Yu-jung bared her teeth.
"Grrrr!"

'What do I do? She's using some weird techniques. Honestly, this is even

'Huh?'
It was two of the Seven Generals—Ilgak, an ogre with long horns, and
Baeksa, a woman with white snake-like skin.
'So that weird feeling was coming from them. If they want to fight, they

'Right. I was wondering why Yahweh disguised himself as a demon—he's
wary of those guys.'
According to Richelra, Yahweh was the most hated being among the

'I don't remember the incantation for the Geingoa chain.'

"Hehehe! Just try and catch me!"
Even as her voice quickly faded into the distance, Shirone's expression

"Come back."

A scream followed.
"Aaaah! Damn it! You damn Yahweh!"
Son Yu-jung, clutching the golden ring around her neck, writhed on the

"What the hell?! Why is this happening?!"
Richelra, who was tumbling alongside her, froze in fear at the sight of

Her veins were bulging, and a storm of emotions swirled in her fiery
eyes.

"Son Yu-jung."
"Argh! Stop it! Please stop!"

around him.
"I'll give you three seconds. One… two…"
"Eeeeeek!"

Shirone.

"Yeah. You've ruined everything, as always. So now you have to help

"Eek!"
Geingoa, a weapon that responds to the true will of those who have

"Stop! I told you not to do this!"

'They're gone.'

"Fine! Let's die together, you bastard!"

"For now, just follow my orders. You'll understand why soon

"Cut it out."

"Stop picking up bad habits. You have a mission to change the flow of

'I thought I could talk my way out of this. Turns out Yahweh's no

'Lette must have heard by now. That makes the fortress the most

"Yes? Yes!"
