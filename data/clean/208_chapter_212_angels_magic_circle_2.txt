Angel's Magic Circle (2)
"You said Goffin. Is it the same Goffin who created Goffin's Gate? Why
did Miro call out his name at the last moment?"
Ikael blinked in surprise.
It was unexpected that Shirone had heard that voice. But she soon
continued explaining in a calm tone.
"Goffin was the first gatekeeper who protected your world from
Heaven's army. Miro is, so to speak... Goffin's successor."
"What? Are you saying Miro wasn't the first?"
"Their circumstances were different, but essentially, yes. McLaine
Guffin, he's probably one of the most brilliant humans in your world. No, he was more exceptional than
any Nephilim. That's probably why he separated humans from God."
"So where is that person now?"
Ikael shook her head.
"He left for a place I cannot oversee, leaving behind a successor
named Miro."
"But that means…."
"Yes. She must forever act as the barrier in a place where time
does not flow. Otherwise, Shirone's world will be destroyed."
Shirone's lips trembled. The last time he saw Miro's face, it came to
mind vividly.
How would she feel, isolated from the world and forced to face
indefinite loneliness?
At that moment, a terrifying thought crossed his mind.
Conversations he had with the headmaster began to resurface one by
one.
At that time, Alpheas' face looked sorrowful. No, sometimes he wore a
terrifying expression, as if he were angry.
What if Miro didn't choose that path herself?
What if she was forced into isolation by someone?
Shirone got goosebumps thinking about that.
This couldn't even be called a crime. It was a decision that condemned a
single human to an eternal hell.
"How long will Miro have to...?"
"Even now, Heaven's army is fully prepared to fight the final war.
It might not take long. That's because the coordinates are recorded in the Meta Gate that Shirone's
party brought."
Shirone's mind went blank.
Miro had blocked Heaven's gate using Scale Magic. But the Meta Gate was
practically opening a new gate to Heaven.
"No way... That can't be..."
"No. It is possible. With the Meta Gate, Heaven's technology can easily
bypass Miro's space-time."
Shirone felt as if his heart was breaking.
This wasn't just a matter of life and death for them. It was the
potential annihilation of all humanity.
He couldn't believe it was true. What on earth had they done?
"That's why I'm trying to help. Shirone's friends are captured in
the Great Hall of World. Kariel will implant Nephilim information into the bodies of the
girls."."
"Why? You said they were preparing for the final war! What do the
Nephilim have to do with this?"
"Because the situation has changed. Miro is not a Nephilim. Yet she
opened the Immortal Function on her own. Such cases are extremely rare, but it happens frequently in
Shirone's world."
That was why Shirone had been confused when he heard the word Nephilim
at Nors Shelter. There were too many Unlockers to be descendants of angels.
"It means Shirone's world has moved far from Ra's will. Goffin
bought time so humans could understand the essence of the world. And now, that will has passed on to
Miro. Of course, the time she defended your world is pitiful compared to Goffin. But considering the
speed of growth, it is a level that cannot be overlooked."
Shirone also agreed with that statement.
The speed of magical development was indeed accelerating. It was at the
point where it was being analyzed that the achievements of the past 100 years were outpaced by the last
10 years.
"So, this time, humans won't be easily defeated."
"That's right. Although they are a minority, the rulers of the human
world are probably aware of the existence of Heaven. They are probably preparing in some way. Right now,
they need more Nephilim, even if it's just one more."
Shirone trembled. The idea of injecting angelic information into human
bodies to annihilate humanity was unbearable.
He couldn't stand the thought of Amy giving birth to someone for such a
reason.
"Why did you stand by and let this happen? As an archangel,
couldn't you ask for their release?"
Ikael looked at Shirone with sad eyes.
"Ah…."
That's right. Why does Miro hate Ikael? Because she was the angel who
commanded Heaven's army. Ikael, too, was once an archangel who punished humans.
"Yes, I am an angel. If the final war happens... I will also have
to fight against humans."
Shirone felt like all the strength was leaving his body.
Was this how it would end? Would he have to watch his friends suffer
without getting any help from Heaven?
"If… it was before I met Goffin."
Ikael gave a wry smile and stuck out her tongue.
"Don't worry, Shirone. I want to help you. Unlike angels, humans have
limitless potential. I believe that the final war must never happen."
Shirone had a blank expression but then realized the weight of her
words.
If an archangel who had risen to the rank of chief archangel were to
stand on the side of humanity, there would be hope even if the final war broke out. (천사장까지 오른 대천사가 인간의

She mentioned before meeting Goffin. While Shirone couldn't ask what
sins Ikael had committed, he could guess it was related to him.
"However, don't be too reassured. I am a being with my power
sealed. Moreover, I can't even leave my room, so even if I want to help Shirone, I can't provide direct
assistance."
"But… I can't defeat an angel with my own strength."
"That's right. Angels are strong. Moreover, Kariel is a powerful
archangel, so strong that even before I lost my power, I couldn't recklessly confront him."
"Then what should we do now?"
She wouldn't have brought this up just to demoralize him. Shirone
clinging for a glimmer of hope and waited for her to come up with a solution.
"There is one way. It might increase our chances of saving your friends
a little bit."
"Really? What is it?"
"It's the angel's magic circle. Shirone, you need to master
it."
"You mean... Halo?"
"That's right. Angels activate their abilities by accumulating
information in their halo. If Shirone learns how to do it, you might be able to create a single
opportunity at a crucial moment."
Shirone recalled the events at Shamain. The awe he had felt when Ikasa
unleashed her halo still lingered in his body.
And now he was being told to master it himself?
He didn't think it was possible, because humans don't have Over
Spirit.
Without a Holy Light Body, there is no space to engrave the information,
so how could he create a magic circle?
"Of course, it won't be easy. Shirone will have to overcome at least
three obstacles to use the angel's magic circle."
Ikael said, holding up three fingers.
"First, creating a Holy Light Body. Although human spirit resides
within the body, I know that it can be taken out through a special method. Can you do it?"
Shirone nodded. The concept Ikael had mentioned was a Deviate Type of
Four-Way Zone.
Indeed, the Deviate Type could potentially mimic the angel's Over Spirit
to some extent.
"The problem starts from now. Even if you create a Holy Light Body,
unfolding a halo is on a different level. You need to draw a perfect circle. If the circle is even
slightly distorted, the computation speed of light will significantly slow down, and if it slows down by
even 0.1%, the angel's abilities cannot be manifested."
Humans cannot draw a perfect circle. But in the realm of the mind, it's
not impossible. Grasping the mathematical concept of a circle is crucial.
It wasn't an easy task, but it was a hurdle that had to be
overcome.
"That's the first hurdle. The second is even more serious. An angel's
abilities come from the immense amount of information embedded in the halo. In mathematical terms, it's
a quantity that a human couldn't record even in a lifetime. The essence of the halo is to compute this
information at the speed of light."
Shirone recalled the scene where Ikael caused the Galliant volcano to
erupt. The ability to increase a dormant volcano's activity by 20,000 times and trigger an eruption was
beyond human capability.
"Even if I can draw a perfect circle, what about the equations? A
human mind can't calculate such enormous information in an instant to cause a volcanic
eruption."
"But there is insight. Here's what we'll attempt. We will do spirit
union. I will become Shirone and execute the halo. That experience will then transfer to Shirone's mind,
becoming part of your experience."
"Such, such a method…."
Ikael's eyes turned fierce.
"Don't call it a method. It's nothing more than the most extreme
violence that can be inflicted on a human being. If something goes wrong, Shirone's spirit could
shatter. And my spirit, merged with Shirone's, would not be safe either."
Shirone felt nauseated. If it was to save his friends, he didn't mind
losing his spirit. He had risked his life from the beginning. However, if even Ikael was in danger, it
was a decision he couldn't make rashly.
"Even if I fail…."
"Shirone, don't think about anything else. I said I would help you.
Don't worry about me, and for now, just think about saving your friends."
Shirone took a deep breath to calm his mind. It was the only thing he
could do, as she said.
"What is the third hurdle?"
"Even if the magic circle's equation is completed, whether you can
withstand it is another issue. If your mental endurance is not enough, Shirone will be destroyed. Of
course, this is separate from my own danger. However, if you cannot endure, it would be better not to
attempt this in the first place."
"Don't worry about that. I'll definitely endure."
He was confident about this last hurdle. Ever since he entered the magic
academy, his mental endurance had always been his strength.
Besides, having reached the state of Indestructible Diamond, he believed
he could withstand it to some extent.
"You don't understand. I know that Shirone has a mental power that never
breaks down. But that alone might not be enough."
"How can something unbreakable still break? That seems like a
contradiction."
"No. The former and the latter are on different levels of concept. If it
were a vessel that could hold Shirone's spirit, its current durability would be sufficient. But an
angel's concept, born from a higher dimension, is different in quality. The vessel may be sufficient to
hold water, but if that water freezes, any vessel will break."
"I, I see."
It was a mountain beyond a mountain. Of the three hurdle she presented,
none were easy to overcome.
No, perhaps this was normal.
Since this was a task for humans to master the archangel's magic circle,
it was necessary to bear this level of risk from the beginning.
"The halo will cause your mind to explode from within, Shirone. The
choice is yours. If you want to attempt this despite knowing the risks, I will help you."
For an angel, the collapse of the mind was equivalent to the
annihilation of life.
If Ikael was willing to risk everything to help, Shirone had no reason
to refuse.
It was for his friends, but the chance to learn an archangel's true
intentions would never come again.
"I'll try. No, please let me. I'll accept whatever the outcome may
be."
"I understand. The magic that I'm going to use on Shirone from now on is
called Possession. It involves merging spirit and dominating the human. It is usually used by dispatched
angels to convey Ra's will."
Shirone had read about Possession through various texts. At the time, he
was half-believing, but now that an archangel had spoken directly about it, there was no room for
doubt.
"If Possession is successful, my spirit and Shirone's will merge. If I
harbor any malicious intent, it will be easy to harm you, Shirone. Likewise, if you lose your purity,
that malice will seep into me."
It was a warning that if they were not careful with each other,
something irreversible could happen.
Shirone nodded again without hesitation. He knew Ikael would never
harbor any malicious intent.
Moreover, even if he lost his purity, she was not a weak angel who
couldn't control it.
