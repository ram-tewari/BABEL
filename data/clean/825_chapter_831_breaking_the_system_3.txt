Breaking the System (3)
Rian dreamed.
Or perhaps the signals embedded at the genetic level manifested as
images through the dream.
Ymir looked down indifferently at Smile, who had perfectly
transformed into a human form.
"Has he really gone?"
The only injury he sustained from the blow was a single molar.
As his true form, he could regenerate at any time, but he didn't feel
like doing so.
"This doesn't feel satisfying."
The feeling of incompleteness was even more unpleasant than the
emptiness he felt after destroying countless weaklings.
He wanted to fight until his whole body was shattered.
A fight so difficult that even if he coughed up blood, screamed, and
unleashed all his power, it wouldn't be enough.
While there were a few others who could be considered Ymir's natural
enemies, like the three-horned Mara Ashur of Ikael, Ogent was the first person to push him to his limits
with sheer strength.
'This world is too light.'
As Ymir reached out to the sun and clenched his fist, the air shook
left and right.
"It doesn't even feel real."
A world made of paper that crumbled before he could even savor the
sensation of touching it.
To him, a formidable opponent was the only tool that could make all
his senses vivid.
"Did he kill him?"
Kariel, covered in dust, approached after being struck by
Ogent.
As his halo emitted light, his injured body quickly recovered, but
his expression remained grim.
"No, he's asleep."
"What do you mean?"
Ymir pointed to Smile.
"He collapsed the boundary of life and reverse-evolved. He
returned to his original form and merged with the woman."
"That's impossible."
At the end of the cells converging through reverse evolution was
Argones, known as the father of life.
"It's against Argones' law. No creature can become mucus on its
own."
A creature without characteristics is called mucus.
"It's impossible."
Ymir nodded calmly.
"But he transcended that impossibility. Form is merely a shadow
of a specific signal. As long as the sun is up..."
As Ymir stomped on his shadow, the ground sank a foot with a
thud.
"Shadows can't be destroyed."
They can only be transformed.
"This is why he could reach breaking the System with a mere
human body. To destroy him, you need to destroy not the shadow..."
Ymir looked up at the sky.
"You need to destroy the sun."
Kariel, who had been lost in thought, opened his cold eyes and
approached Smile.
"Perhaps it's for the best."
In any case, the element of breaking the System had disappeared on
its own, and killing Smile would eliminate any further risk.
"I will kill this woman."
There was no need to use angelic law; he could simply cut off her
head.
"Wait."
Ymir stopped Kariel.
"You can't kill her."
The reason he promised Ogent was to one day return and settle their
score.
"Don't be arrogant, Ymir."
Kariel's eyes hardened.
"You've already broken the System. Even if it's Anke Ra's order,
if you stop me, you'll face severe punishment."
"I don't care."
The spot where Ogent had struck still throbbed.
"Everything will feel meaningless for a while."
Seeing Ymir casually handle the archangel's killing intent, Kariel's
face twisted in frustration.
"Tch!"
But in the end, he had no choice but to turn away.
Unlike Ogent, a giant who reached breaking the System through pure
brute force was unbeatable.
'Only Ra can stop Ymir now.'
As Kariel watched him leave, Smile let out a small groan and opened
her eyes.
"Ugh..."
After tossing and turning for a moment, she suddenly opened her eyes
and saw Ymir's face three meters above her, causing her to jump up.
"Brother!"
Ogent was nowhere to be seen.
Realizing she was naked, she stepped back and glared at Ymir.
"Who are you?"
"The king of giants."
Thinking she couldn't explain the current situation, Smile finally
asked.
"Where is my brother?"
"You've already died once."
Ymir recounted Ogent's actions while Smile was trapped.
"...That's how you were able to survive. Cherish the life he
passed on to you."
"My brother..."
Smile touched her belly.
In a way, this was also a form of the Elixir of Life, but she didn't
feel scared or repulsed.
'My brother is inside me.'
Together forever.
"What will you do now? If you want to go to Heaven, follow me.
I'll protect you until then."
Smile shook her head.
"No. I'll stay here."
"Stay?"
From the sky, it seemed like humanity existed, but it was a
completely different society from Heaven.
"Are you serious? You might never be able to return. You're
abandoning your family and hometown."
"No. A new life is beginning."
Smile looked beyond the forest.
"My name is Smile."
After a moment of thought, she smiled and looked back at Ymir.
"Ogent Smile."
Seeing the sincerity in her eyes, Ymir nodded and turned away.
"We will meet again someday."
A bat followed Ymir into the forest, chasing him from high in the
sky.

'What!'
Instinctively raising his hand to block, the sharp sword cut into his

"Hup!"

The hunters were fighting numerous soldiers, and Xenia was also

"Rian!"

Rian.
As the seven spear tips entered Rian's body, the middle of the spear

It must have been a forced state of unconsciousness.
