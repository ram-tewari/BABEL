darkness in the face of Shirone's light.
Nade couldn't bear the holiness that embraced the entire world.
The overwhelming presence of Shirone, superimposed countless times, was
a new kind of fear for Nade in his magic-transformed state.

heights.
"Aaaargh!"

"What an absurd graduation exam."
If it were just Nade or Shirone alone, the 2000 system, built with the

could predict if the system could handle their clash.
'You don't understand, Shirone.'

The contradictory pain of not being able to cut them off or leave them

'This won't extinguish my anger!'

wouldn't be so miserable.
Magic Transformation - Thunder God Rebirth.
"Aaaargh!"

Shirone.

'Nade, I know how hard it's been.'

At least Nade hadn't given up.
