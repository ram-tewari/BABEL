Chapter 13:
First Steps Toward a Dream (5)
Rian spat out the water he was drinking. It was neither the time nor place, but to hear
that Shirone was in danger was shocking. He had been planning to see his friend off in a few hours, making
it even more perplexing.
"Shirone? What happened?"
"Well... um..."
"Speak quickly! What happened?"
"It seems Head Butler Louis reported to the lord. I don't know much, but from what I
heard, the young master and a boy named Shirone were like friends..."
"Damn it all!"
Rian immediately picked up his clothes that were drying on a rock. Kaite, watching this,
asked, "What's going on? And who is Shirone?"
"A friend, sir. I didn't mention him because he's a commoner, but it seems Louis has
snitched. That despicable man! Master, let's end today's training. I need to go to the mansion."
"No. That's not possible."
Rian, who was about to dash off to the training ground, turned back to Kaite with an
astonished face.
"What do you mean? My friend is in danger."
"You said he's a commoner."
"What does that matter? Master, were you always like this? You said it yourself!
Anyone who can converse through swords can be a friend!"
Kaite clicked his tongue. His thoughtless disciple had finally caused trouble. Though he
didn't know the exact circumstances, having lived a long life, he could roughly outline what had happened.
"What are you going to do when you get back? Are you going to whine? Or do you plan
to act cute to change your father's mind?"
"Master!"
"Rian, anyone can be a friend. But that's between you and that boy. Your friend has
directly challenged the hierarchy of the Ozent family. This isn't something that childish whining can
fix."
"So you're saying I should just sit here? I was the one who reached out to Shirone
first. I don't want to be a coward!"
Kaite stared into his disciple's eyes. What fine eyes they were. Truthful, passionate,
and above all, pure.
