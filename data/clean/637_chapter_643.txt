A star orbiting intelligence, and a satellite orbiting and assisting

The vast three lines dividing the magical world.
Anyone aspiring to be a mage knows about the Black, Red, and White
lines.

liked to daydream expected it to have any connection to their lives.

So, if they were selecting a candidate from among the students, it had
to be the best graduate.
"This time is a bit different. The first place is a bit ambiguous.

Ranking by the Ivory Tower meant Shirone was the fourth best among
graduates worldwide.
'Shirone, you're a real monster.'
Having seen the graduation exam, there was no room for disagreement, and
the next thought was that there were three more people ranked higher than Shirone.

Though his tone was gentle, it was also a request not to take up too

"Shall we go?"

his friends.

'What are you doing? Go, you idiot!'

"Yes, I understand."

"Please wait a moment."

face with the fan.

"What is it?"
"No matter how prestigious the Ivory Tower is, isn't this out of
order? Currently, Shirone is a criminal who committed a serious crime against the kingdom…"
"Sigh."
Arte let out a small breath and slowly raised the fan covering his
face.
"Wind Chime."
Ding-ding.
A clear bell sound rang in Shirone's ears.
The sound from the small bell at the end of the fan was too grand, and
everyone's expressions hardened as if they had the same thought.
'Time…'
In the feeling of slowing down, only Arte's right arm holding the fan
was moving.
"Officer! It's dangerous!"
As the guards, sensing the killing intent, passed by Lyman and drew
their swords, the fan made a popping sound and pushed the wind backward.
"Ahhh!"
As the huge gust of wind blew, the swordsmen planted their swords in the
ground.
It felt more like a wall pushing in than wind, and it felt like their
flesh was being torn from their bones.
"Ahhh!"
They tried to withstand it with the pride of the royal guard, but the
wind force was beyond human endurance, and finally, they all lifted off the ground and flew away into
the sky.
Tokey, hanging on Arte's shoulder, put his hands over his eyes and
watched as grass, trees, humans, and carriages all disappeared beyond the horizon.
Nothing remained in the place where the gust of wind had swept
through.
Even the teachers couldn't close their gaping mouths at the power that
had blown away even a single blade of grass, and a shiver ran down Shirone's spine as he watched.
'A magic of the Blow series.'
But could this even be called Blow series?
It was not wind but a typhoon, and it was not strange that Gaold came to
mind with the power of a different dimension of magic.
"No matter how prestigious the Ivory Tower is, there are no
repeated words."
When Arte flicked the fan with a displeased expression, the small bell
rang.
Though it was separate from the magic, just hearing the tinkling sound
made the students flinch and shrink their shoulders.
"It seems the message wasn't conveyed properly. Let me repeat.
Shirone is a candidate to receive the title of Star from the Ivory Tower."
It was the same statement as before, but this time it was properly
conveyed.
"Shall we go? I still have one hundred and eighty-seven problems in

possible."

"May we use the meeting room?"
"Of course."
From the school's perspective, having the Ivory Tower intervene would
alleviate the burden of resolving Shirone's issue.

'This might be Shirone's only chance.'
Watching Shirone and his friends follow Arte inside, Alpheas prayed

'Please, let him seize this opportunity.'

"Um, excuse me, but can my friends come with me?"
