Chapter 738:
"Come in. This way."
Minerva's room on the third floor, number 12, where she stayed, was a
small space furnished with modest furniture and a single bed.The fact that there was only one bed
posed a problem, but even if they were a married couple, it would be difficult for two people to sleep
on it given its size.
Shirone noticed a long broom leaning against the wall, which he presumed
to be hers.
'A witch's broom.'
Minerva's broom was known in history books as one of the fastest objects
in existence.
Minerva removed her coat and asked casually,"What kind of play do
you like? I'm not picky, so just tell me, and I'll go along with it."
"As I said before I came in, I didn't follow you here for that
reason."
Minerva lifted her head and laughed."Who's going to believe that?
Men, honestly. You followed me all the way here, so you must have something in mind, right?"
Though the dialogue seemed reversed between genders, Shirone didn't pay
it any attention."I'm just too tired, and I would've gone anywhere that allowed me to sleep.
Besides, you promised to take me to the Ivory Tower."
Fully undressed, Minerva turned to Shirone from in front of her neatly
folded nightgown."And I kept that promise. This is the Ivory Tower."
Shirone's eyes widened."This is the Ivory Tower?"
"To be precise, it's more like the terrace of the Ivory Tower. The
Corona Kingdom was established by the residents of the Ivory Tower.Since the residents can never
leave the tower for life, they needed a place with various amenities. It's also useful for hosting
guests."
"Then where exactly is the Ivory Tower?"
Minerva slipped on a silk robe and said,"You can reach it by using
a magic circle located beneath the royal palace. Other than that, there's no way to access the Ivory
Tower—at least, not normally."
Because there are no absolutes.
"But you're special. If you're a wizard who has reached transcendence in
a human body, you might find a way."
Minerva collapsed onto the bed in a seductive pose."If you make me
happy tonight, I'll let you use the palace's magic circle. What do you say? It's a fair deal."
Shirone looked at her with an expression of pity."I'm used to that
look. Anyway, welcome. Let's get along since we're both in the Five Stars."
"The Five Stars? Me?"
Minerva's gaze turned serious for the first time."Mahagaruta has
left this world. There's currently a vacant seat, and I've returned to the Ivory Tower to honor
him.Nothing is decided yet, but I believe Great Star shares my thoughts."
The vacant seat in the Five Stars would likely be filled by
Shirone.
"By 'left the world,' you mean…"
"Yes. He fully opened the Immortal Function. Unlike you, who returned to
this world."
Minerva suddenly sat up straight."I don't get it. Why did you come
back? All this world has to offer is eating, excreting, and fighting. That's it. Honestly, I agree with
Nane. If he had ended everything…"
Minerva shrugged."It wouldn't have been so bad. You'd return to
the state before birth. Did you know? Before you're born, you don't even think about being
alive."
"But we are alive," Shirone said.
That attachment to existence itself was the only reason Shirone was able
to return to this world.
"Defining murder as evil is something only humans do," Minerva said with
a snort."If Nane killed most of humanity, he'd be labeled the worst murderer.But if he
killed everyone, it wouldn't be a matter of good or evil anymore. It would transcend existence."
Shirone remained unshaken."It's thanks to an extremely low KAR
score that we can love even the smallest creatures equally.But in the end, you're human too. You
should think about why Garfin couldn't fulfill his purpose and was forced to transcend the Photon
Realm."
'Garfin left as well.'
When Shirone crouched against the wall, Minerva patted the bed beside
her and said,"Don't be like that. Just come up. I promise I'll only hold your hand while we
sleep."
There was no reply.
It must have been a nightmare.
Despite the dazzling multicolored sword Nane was plunging into his
chest, Shirone couldn't even lift a finger.
"You're nothing but a speck."
The pain turned into despair, overwhelming his mind.Even though he
knew it was a dream, he couldn't wake up.
"Ugh!"
The gaze of near-perfect KAR looked down at him with such severity that
it froze his heart.
"You are the lowest of the low in this universe…"
The dark sword slowly pierced down through his chest.
"…the most insignificant existence."
"Aaaah!"
Light burst from Shirone's body, dispersing Nane's silhouette, and
darkness enveloped him.Recognizing it as Armand's domain, Shirone's mind began to
stabilize.
"Prey always fears its predator."
From the flickering torchlight, the sound of sharpening a sword could be
heard.
"Armand…"
The chaotic mass of monsters that once formed her body had now
transformed into a beautiful female figure.
"Without a predator, there is no cycle. A world that does not cycle is
dead."
Since her birth as a demon sword, Armand had continuously honed her
blade. Now, it was so thin it appeared transparent like glass.
"What are you sharpening your blade for?"
"…Make me a promise."
The blade vanished completely.
"There will be one chance."
Armand turned her head, curling her lips into a smile that revealed
sharp fangs glinting in the torchlight.
"One chance to wield me for the sake of all life. Don't miss it."
She raised her hand, and soft, fluffy particles of light floated up and
drifted toward Shirone.
"Material."
As Shirone gently enclosed the light in his hands, the darkness faded,
and a distant, massive eyelid opened, allowing light to pour in.
"Huh?"
The fierce winds of the polar regions howled, and a bitter cold rushed
in, making his body shiver.
"So cold…"
Upon awakening, he noticed that Armand's robe had vanished without a
trace.
'It wasn't a dream.'
Each time Shirone broke through a new level, Armand, who was fused with
him, evolved in function.
'Material.'
When Shirone opened his palm, a heavy mass of light ballooned outward
like a floating sphere.
"I see…"
As an infinite mage, if he could control all information with Yahweh's
light, there was no need to maintain the sword form.
"For example…"
As Shirone thought of something, the glow of Material faded,
transforming back into matter.
It became a solid lump of gold, glistening in the morning
sunlight.
"Original information that can transform into any material."
Dropping the gold to the ground like a stone, Shirone recreated
Material, forming soap, toothpaste, and a toothbrush in quick succession.
"…Feels kind of wasteful."
If backed by engineering knowledge, he could produce far more complex
objects, but for now, this was sufficient.
Though it was a tool befitting the infinite mage, Shirone pondered
Armand's final words.
"She said one chance."
And when that chance came, Material would become Armand again, wielding
the concept of infinity.
"What are you mumbling about?"
When Shirone looked up, Minerva, now appearing as an adorably youthful
girl, was smiling at him.
No longer needing to confirm her true nature, Shirone stood up and
dusted himself off.
"Nothing. So, are we going to the Ivory Tower now?"
Despite witnessing the harmony of Material firsthand, Minerva seemed to
understand without needing an explanation.
"You were so noisy last night. Crying and babbling—I couldn't get a wink
of sleep."
This was why sharing a room was a hassle.
"Sorry. I had a nightmare."
"That much was obvious. You were freezing last night too. Your coat
vanished, and no matter how much I shook you, you wouldn't wake up."
Shirone squinted suspiciously."Is that really all?"
"Of course!"
Minerva grabbed her broom and headed for the door."Wash up and
come out. I'll wait outside."
Despite her quick retreat raising more suspicions, there was no way to
find out for sure.
'What kind of Five Star acts like that?'
The fact that he wasn't afraid of the Witch of Terror was proof that
Shirone had earned his place among the stars.
When Shirone descended to the first floor, he found the residents still
playing cards from the night before.Minerva, holding a broom taller than herself, waved at him to
follow.
Trudging through the knee-deep snow that had fallen overnight, they
arrived at the palace, where the royal bloodline greeted them.Unlike officials from other nations,
they showed no fear of the stars, revealing the close relationship between the Corona Kingdom and the
Ivory Tower.
"Welcome back, Lady Minerva. You remain as radiant as ever."
"Hoho! Not bad for someone over 700 years old, right? You've aged a lot
yourself, Ambassador."
"It's only natural for humans to age after 20 years."
Despite Minerva's blunt remark, the ambassador smiled.
"You must have returned home because of the altar, correct?"
"Yes. It seems we won't be able to stop Nane. I believe this world will
end soon…"
Suddenly furrowing her brows, Minerva glanced at Shirone and tapped his
forehead with her finger.
"It's all because of him. An infinite mage can't even handle something
like Nane and ended up here."
Shirone felt wronged."Why don't you try facing him yourself before
saying that? If it's so infuriating, why don't you stop him?"
"For someone who's supposedly well-educated, that's a stupid thing to
say. If you couldn't stop him, what makes you think I can?"
The ambassador bowed slightly toward Shirone."Thank you for
protecting humanity. The Five Stars have always been a great help to us."
"I'm not part of the Five Stars yet. And… I didn't fight solely for
humanity."
The ambassador, who had welcomed countless stars over the decades,
understood Shirone's heart perfectly.
"I see. You must have your reasons. Please, follow me. I'll guide
you."
As they followed the ambassador, Shirone asked,"So, what exactly
is the altar?"
Despite having opened the Immortal Function and gained infinite
omnipotence, Shirone's omniscience was limited to extrasensory perception.Knowledge, after all,
doesn't inherently exist.
"It's a gate that erases the boundary between reality and the hidden
world.Essentially, it's meant to bring the hell created by humans into reality.According to
the Ivory Tower's investigation, there are 3,600 altars worldwide."
"Three thousand six hundred…"
"When the altars activate, this world will become hell. The law will
tilt in Nane's favor, and after that, eternal extinction."
"Why will it tilt in Nane's favor?"
"You're the only one who thinks otherwise."
Only Shirone denied Nane's purpose.
"How many humans do you think will still cling to hope amidst unbearable
suffering?Nane is right. The only true sanctuary for living beings is eventual extinction. You're
the one denying that."
Minerva's gaze turned cold."Imagine… If you were born as a
creature that felt pain from the start,if every breath tore your throat, if every sight burned
your eyes, if every word blistered your tongue…"
She stopped walking and looked back at Shirone."Would you still
choose to live? Could you endure such torment every second of every day, waiting for your life to
end?"
Shirone couldn't be certain.
"I know someone like that."
He knew someone who endured such pain, but he could never ask others to
live that way.
"Gaold, right?"
"Yes. His love for Miro drove him to endure the pain. But in the end, he
fell into hell.And now, I'm… telling everyone to live like that."
"That's all you need to understand."
It would be a difficult battle.
"If Nane hadn't gone to such extremes, you wouldn't have ended up
standing at the opposite extreme.For ordinary people, it's just a case of shrimp caught in a whale
fight."
Minerva patted Shirone's shoulder.
"Anyway, you'll have as many supporters in this universe as your KAR
value. Start from there and push your way up."
The ambassador spoke."We've arrived."
They had reached the magic circle leading to the Ivory Tower.
