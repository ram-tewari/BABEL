Chapter 740:
Despite the grand name of the Great Sanctuary, a long silence followed
Shirone's question.
"Shirone," Great Star began heavily, "Humans have the
ability to name things that exist. This is intelligence."
Naming is the essence of intelligence.
"But just because we have the ability to know something doesn't
mean we can know everything."
Many scholars explore the ends of the universe.
"If you think you will someday know everything, it is merely the
limitation of a human who has not yet grasped the essence of intelligence."
The answer lies beyond infinity.
"Therefore, even though I know everything in this world, I cannot
assert what lies outside the universe."
"But I am here."
It wasn't about the outside.
"Right here, you can tell me what you see beyond the infinite
veil."
"I truly don't know. I have no reason to lie to you."
"Can't you at least guess?"
"Guessing is not something I should do."
Great Star was firm.
"When you stand on the ground and look up at the sky, you can make
many guesses. It's free to let your imagination soar. But at the end of the world, you must not do that.
Shirone, you know how dangerous that is."
You cannot believe the essence of the world based on guesses.
"Beyond the veil, there may be only nothingness. This could be the
beginning. Why are you denying yourself?"
"It's not denial; I want to acknowledge it. I heard that Mr.
Mahagaruta attained enlightenment. And he did not return."
Had Mahagaruta awakened from the dream by now, or had he achieved
annihilation in eternal nothingness?
"I was able to return to this world because I let go of everything
in the realm of infinity…"
This was why Shirone was sad.
"If I had crossed the boundary, I wouldn't have returned
either."
You cannot return to a dream from outside the dream, and thus Shirone
knew Nane was right.
"To protect this world against Nane, or to surpass him, I think I
need to define Hexa first."
Great Star sighed deeply.
"Infinity…"
Then she turned to Shirone and asked,
"What is it? What is emptiness? What is a human? What is a star?
Everything is just a name given to things that exist without reason. They just are, and we define them
according to our needs. Therefore, infinity and emptiness are just names we have given."
Great Star pointed her finger at Shirone's forehead.
"Even gods."
"……"
"To humans, the concept of a god doesn't need to be complicated.
It's a matter of faith, and it was named for that purpose. But when you enter the realm of gods, as
Shirone, the Infinite Magician, has…"
Great Star lowered her arm.
"You encounter all sorts of contradictions. You can handle all the
information in the world. You are a god. But the problem is, that information is from a human
perspective."
Shirone had indeed become a god, but only a god as defined by
humans.
"I understand why you asked the question. The idea you realized
with the eighth sense just exists; it doesn't need to be or not be…"
Thus, you can control all phenomena freely.
"But that too is just the infinity named by humans, so you wonder
if the true meaning lies outside."
Great Star continued immediately.
"Also, because Hexa is made of light, in other words… it is just an
abnormal signal flowing in the system, not a specific entity."
Hearing this from Great Star felt like his heart stopped.
"So, I am…"
Great Star's expression softened.
"This is my hypothesis."
"Hypothesis…?"
"Yes. Let's try it. What you want to know. Let's guess beyond
infinity at the end of the world."
Great Star's kind heart was evident.
"You know, Shirone, I am the reincarnation of a star. My name,
Gaia, governs all stars. Let's say… I am actually a program. The Gaia Program."
Great Star emphasized that this was a hypothesis.
"The universe is born, and within it, the natural world forms.
There are two giant programs that make up the natural world. One is me, Gaia, governing planets. And the
other…"
Great Star looked up at the ceiling.
"The Stellar Program, Ra."
Ra was another name for the sun.
"The Gaia Program builds new systems through the energy transmitted
by the Stellar Program. The lower system of the natural world, the biological world, is
born."
"And from that biological world…"
"It's too early to talk about humans."
Great Star interrupted.
"When Gaia creates the environment using Ra's energy, the
biological program Argones spreads cells. The possibilities of evolution are infinite, but the essence
is the simplest information transmission substance."
Shirone's eyes widened.
"Argones?"
It was the name of the alien organism that Pisho, who specialized in
insect magic, had become a host for during the graduation exam.
"Ra's energy, Gaia's environment, Argones' cells. Combining these
three elements infinitely creates specific spaces in this vast universe where life can exist. Then the
basic preparation is complete."
"Preparation for what?"
"For the user to use this world."
Shirone fell silent.
"Assuming Shirone's guess is true. Even if life exists, building a
mind is another matter. It must seem real, and yet offset the fear of death."
"A god."
"Yes. The mind program, God. Since it is derived from the stellar
source of life, humans call it this."
Great Star raised her finger.
"The eternal Ra."
Anke Ra.
"Even so, Anke Ra wouldn't know it is a program. It would only know
itself as a being that suddenly awakened in the realm of nothingness. When it defines all concepts
subjectively and stores them in the Akashic Records, they are transmitted to all life in the universe
through quantum transmission. That's why Nane called this world Anke Ra's dream."
Everything Shirone had sensed with super cognition became vividly
clear.
"But an unexpected problem arose."
Great Star's eyes darkened.
"The mind program activated, and all users were to achieve eternal
life through reincarnation. But the first humans from Gaia, the Gaians, began to reject the god
program."
Shirone recalled the records of the Gaians he had seen in Babel.
"They tried to escape from Anke Ra."
"Yes. It was a fierce war. Every time Anke Ra tried to reset to fix
errors, Gaia desperately resisted. They believed their enlightenment was more important than the
universe. They had to pass it on to future generations."
Shirone swallowed hard.
"Anke Ra used all the concepts it had defined to attack them. The

preserve their enlightenment."
"That method was…"
"Go outside and directly inject the code. Insert a special signal

Shirone shook his head.
"How is that possible? Mr. Mahagaruta, having attained
enlightenment, can no longer affect this world."
"There is one way."
Shirone's eyes widened in sudden realization.
"Could it be…"
"Yes. Goffin's erasure."
Great Star waved his finger.
"Escape the photon system at the moment of reset and inject a new

Shirone clenched his teeth.
"Then Hexa is…"
"The mind program transmits information through quantum, the

"Hexa."
At the same time, tears streamed down Shirone's cheeks.
"The concepts of cause and effect are just definitions interpreted

"The light that makes up Hexa mimics human information. So Shirone

'Not a single one…'
None of them were connected.
They were just part of a program created by some visionaries to escape

"I told you, Shirone. This is just a guess. I don't know the truth

Goffin was erased, but…
