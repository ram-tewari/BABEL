death of his firstborn, it was a different matter entirely.
"Ooooh… oooooh…"

"OOOOOH!"
Was it a cry of grief?
"OOOOOH!"
Or the ultimate expression of wrath against whoever had struck Mikhas
where it hurt most?

"Yes, Your Majesty!"

The First Yora.

"Hyah! Hyah!"

Damn it!
To capture the one who killed his son, King Delkido had mobilized the

No one knew whether he loved his son enough to forsake a conquest war,
but…
In his obsession with revenge, one could glimpse a wounded monarch's
pride.
Yorahan, bound and thrown into a mobile prison cell, crawled to the
barred window.
Once again, a Fireborn settlement—one they had moved so many times—was
being reduced to ashes.
Why…
Why were humans so relentless?
King Delkido subjected Yorahan and the Fireborn to every form of torment
imaginable.
Yorahan was imprisoned in solitary confinement.
With all the tendons in his limbs severed, he was left to listen each
day to the screams echoing from outside.
"Why…?"
Tears fell.
How can anyone be this cruel? How can a being inflict something so
horrific upon another?
Sure, tearing someone else's nails out didn't make your own hurt…
But it wasn't impossible to imagine it.
We can never truly know another person's truth. And so humanity remains
trapped within the prison of 'self'…
All they can do is act in self-interest.
During his time in prison, Yorahan pondered the selfishness inherent in
the concept of "I."
Love that escapes the self…
He had abandoned his dream of changing the world. Perhaps now it wasn't
even possible. But with his limbs ruined, all he could do was think.

reappeared.
"You stubborn bastard."
Delkido's gaunt eyes gleamed with a murky madness.
"Even after hearing the Fireborn scream every day, you still haven't

"Do you know why I spared your eyes, ears, and tongue even after

endured."
A scream rang out from the hallway.
Yorahan no longer held any hope that anyone had survived. He just wished
for them to die painlessly.
"All this for the revenge of one man? Isn't it enough? Your son killed
Fireborn too."
"RAAAAGH!"
Delkido lashed his whip.
"You dare compare my son to them? He was a prince! Their lives aren't

"All life is equal in value. The pain you feel from losing your

"My son is dead! What does any of that matter?! My son died! My
son!"
Me, me, me.
Can humanity never escape from that?

"Heh…"

"…No…"

Planted in the pot was not a flower… but Armand, the woman he loved

"Well? Like it? Bonsai, I think it's called? I cut off what I didn't

To treat a human like a plant…
"Yorahan."

His body is so damaged…
They didn't need Small World Resonance. Just a glance, and they knew

Love is…
