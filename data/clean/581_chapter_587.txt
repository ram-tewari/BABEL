Eden felt fear for the first time as the immense mass of the shockwave
converged on a single point.
'Fear? Me?'
It was an emotion she couldn't accept, being invincible under God's
protection.
'This can't be!'
Eden resisted with all her might.
For humans, even a planet is vast, and they might never experience such
a terrifying phenomenon in their lifetime. She desperately clung to her faith.

If God existed beyond human reach, then Shirone's Quasar was also a
god.
'No, no!'
God had to reside far above that.

"Aaaargh!"

shaken.
'Give up, Eden!'

'It's not absolute! That's not God, Eden!'

She felt cracks forming in Absolute Barrier.
'This can't be happening!'
It also meant her faith was collapsing.
"God…!"
Tears streaming down, Eden prayed.
"I believe!"

'Amazing. Truly amazing.'

"Stay with me! Watch over me forever!"
Eden wouldn't be destroyed.
God existed, and she believed in that.

'What did I do wrong? What is wrong?'
