Chapter 509:
The Blowback of Evil (3)
The look of disgust disappeared from Erga's face as she glared at Zulu
in heavy silence.
Chirurururu.
With a cute sound, the small monster disappeared, and Zulu stood
up.
Circulation.
If she stopped for even a moment, she would be sucked into the endless
abyss again, so she expelled the rising feelings of malice from her heart countless times.
Smooch.
She put the fallen pacifier back in her mouth and surveyed the scenery
of Zion.
Most of the objects were destroyed in the battle with Kali, but there
were countless Metagates.
Zulu grabbed one of them and checked the time.
It might already be too late.
Did Etella, who was supposed to find the disk, and Armin's team, who had
to extract the coordinates to return from Ingris, complete their mission?
Not knowing until she checked, she hurriedly moved towards the
ground.
"……"
But just before leaving Zion, she stopped.
It was right to assume it was already too late.
For certain missions, it is better to prepare for the worst-case
scenario than to rely on a small chance.
Zulu looked down at the Metagate in her hand for a moment, then turned
back towards Zion.
It was time to make a choice.
Thousand-Hand Avalokitesvara Thunder Strike.
The endless barrage of strikes is the thousand arms to drive out the
world's evil, and the final powerful strike is the single enlightenment to destroy the demon.
That is the essence of the Yin-Yang Wave Fist's ultimate technique, the
Thousand-Hand Avalokitesvara Thunder Strike, but the more Etella struck Satan, the more doubt filled her
heart.
"Huuuuh!"
A groan escaped through Etella's tightly clenched teeth.
How could this be?
No matter how much she pushed the waves in, there was no interference as
Satan's body grew larger.
'Just a little more, just a little more!'
In theory, the Yin-Yang Wave Fist can destroy any object from the
inside.
But it wasn't particularly effective against humans.
There was no need to cause internal destruction to humans with even soft
skin.
Thus, it was a technique solely for destroying evil.
If it didn't work on Satan, the symbol of evil, it felt like even the
faith of the Karsis Monastery would be broken, so she struck with all her might.
"Ughhhh!"
As the speed of striking the solid muscles increased, the feeling of
impact finally began to show on Satan's face.
'Almost there. If I can just push a little more to unleash the powerful
strike…'
"Hehehe. Hehehehehe."
As Etella found a glimmer of hope, she heard Satan's laughter.
Without looking up, she knew what his laughter meant.
Satan's body began to grow rapidly again, and the waves she had driven
in with all her might were fading.
"It has perfectly tilted."
Satan spread his arms wide and leaned back as if he had nothing to fear
anymore.
It was a gesture to hit him as much as she wanted.
'A great power is surging in.'
With Kariel's disappearance in Aravot, only four archangels
remained.
And the Garas were still reducing the number of angels, bringing them
shame.
As the scales of law tilted towards Satan for the first time, his body
began to grow endlessly without knowing its limits.
"Ooooooh! Ooooooh!"
In front of the rapidly growing body of evil, Etella shed tears.
"Why, whyyyyy!"
Since joining the Karsis Monastery, she had fought countless
evils.
But she had never felt so miserable as she did now.
"Ah, it feels good. Hit me more, make me happier."
Satan continued to grow without end.
As his height surpassed 20 meters, 30 meters, 50 meters, and 100 meters,
even the surrounding buildings collapsed.
"Hehehe, poor human."
Dududududu!
Etella did not stop her attack.
In the feeling of her lifelong faith collapsing at once, what supported
her was the voice of her master who had taught her the true meaning of the Yin-Yang Wave Fist.
-Etella, you must believe. You must believe that good exists.
Swordsmanship.
The skill of handling a sword.
If you swing a sword at a scarecrow, a very simple single motion is
enough.
But swordsmanship is a game of tag with each other's lives on the
line.
And from that moment, the situation becomes serious.
The compatibility of movements, the trajectory compatibility, the
judgment compatibility, countless compatibilities intertwine to derive the immediate next result, and
that result becomes the cause of a new judgment to bite the opponent.
The duel between Kuan and Nameless felt like snakes of murderous intent
wriggling freely to bite each other's necks.
Nameless was close to perfection, and Kuan was unstable.
The two sword paths, rushing to the extremes, awakened the existence
that had stayed outside their thoughts, and the level continued to rise.
I am getting stronger.
In a state where life was ignored and only thoughts were everything,
Kuan expressed chaos with his whole body.
Like staggering on a ship caught in a storm, his legs twisted and his
upper body wobbled, causing Nameless's sword to shake.
He was playing with the sword.
As new patterns endlessly permeated his body, Kuan's thoughts became
simpler.
It was a journey approaching the essence of the sword, a necessity so
close to the truth that it didn't even need confirmation.
'So easy. The sword… is this easy?'
Kuan's sword pierced Nameless's blind spots with a cold
sharpness.
As wounds increased all over his body, Nameless finally realized.
'It's a pity I have to die.'
Truly.
'It's frustrating to disappear without conveying anything, leaving
behind such a wonderful world.'
Kuan, who had slashed Nameless's waist, spun like a top and circled
behind his enemy.
Nameless hurriedly turned, but Kuan's figure was already gone.
'Hehe, I knew it. There's no way he'd be there.'
A human who fought for the first and last time without even being given
a name.
Is there a stronger swordsman in the world than this man?
Nameless hoped Kuan would become the strongest swordsman.
Otherwise, it would be too unfair for his talent to be buried
here.
'No, that's not it.'
Nameless searched everywhere for Kuan, but it was as if he had
disappeared; he couldn't even see his afterimage.
'But you must be watching me.'
He didn't live a day, but it was a wonderful life.
'Because I met you.'
Nameless slowly lowered his sword.
Movement Zero.
No matter where he turned his gaze, he wouldn't find Kuan, so further
movement was meaningless.
"Because there are two, it is infinite."
Slash!
With a refreshing sound, Nameless's neck was cut from behind.
Kuan, who had slashed diagonally with his sword parallel to the ground,
spun as if to gather the remaining inertia of the external force as soon as his intact foot touched the
ground.
Tap, roll.
Only after the rotation stopped did Nameless's head roll on the ground,
and blood spurted like a fountain from the still-standing body.
"Yes."
Kuan muttered with indifferent eyes.
"Because there are two, it is infinite."
When a perfect one meets another one, the future opens, and all
possibilities spread infinitely.
That is swordsmanship and the logic of the world.
Blood spurted from Kuan's shoulder, where his arm had been cut
off.
It was a fight that pushed him to the limit, and he could no longer
maintain his schema.
"Damn!"
Armin kicked off the ground and ran.
But Shina arrived first and caught Kuan's upper body with her whole
body.
"Mr. Kuan! Are you okay? Please wake up."
Kuan just wanted to sleep.
But hearing the caller's voice, he lifted his trembling eyelids.
'Women… have such a nice scent.'
For Kuan, who had lived his whole life smelling blood and sweat, it was
a truly fresh shock.
'But this scent is not mine.'
Yes, it was a scent he could smell in exchange for his life.
"I'm fine. Go to Ingris."
Etella and Zulu were still missing, but they had to extract the
coordinates as originally planned to prepare for the future.
Rumble, rumble, rumble!
At that moment, the building shook as if an earthquake had
occurred.
"What is it?"
Armin looked up at the ceiling and shouted in surprise.
"Get out of the way!"
The building was collapsing.
As Shina struggled to lift Kuan, Armin cast a flicker spell, taking both
of them with him.
At the same time, a huge boulder crashed down where they had
been.
Rumble, rumble, rumble!
Outside the building, Armin realized the cause of the earthquake, and
Shina also looked up at the sky with a bewildered expression.
"What… is that?"
A gigantic monster, unimaginable by human standards, was still growing
at that moment.
As the monster bent its waist, its evil face suddenly appeared below the
clouds.
Hahaha!
A ferocious laugh that seemed to swallow the world erupted from its
mouth, shaking the ground.
Armin spoke with a serious expression.
"We have to go."
The building's collapse had blocked the way to Ingris, but seeing the
current situation, it wasn't the time to look for coordinates.
"I don't know what that monster is, but things are definitely
messed up. It looks like Miss Etella is there too."
The human fighting the monster looked like a small dot, but Armin's keen
eyes could see clearly.
While Armin moved immediately, Shina hesitated, unable to leave
easily.
"Go."
Kuan, looking at the sky with an indifferent expression, said.
"But Mr. Kuan, you are…"
"Sympathy is a disgrace to a swordsman. I'm not going to die, so
don't worry."
Not that he was sure he would survive.
In fact, how could a human who had never died know how much injury it
takes to die?
'Shina…'
Armin was waiting at a distance.
The mission was the top priority.
But having already abandoned Kuan to save Shina, he had no right to
persuade her with such words.
'Yes, there's no time.'
Shina decided to go.
Although her heart was overflowing with worry for Kuan, if she didn't
solve the immediate problem, all that would be left was total annihilation.
"I'll be back soon."
You don't have to come back.
Kuan swallowed the words that rose to his throat, knowing they would
only be a burden.
Then Shina turned back and asked,
"Until then, will you stay alive?"
Kuan didn't feel it was worth answering.
Even if he wanted to stay alive, it wasn't something he could do by his
own will in his severely injured state.
"If I come back alive, then…"
Shina hesitated for a long time before blushing slightly and
saying,
"Let's have a meal together again."
Leaving those words, Shina fled with Armin to where Satan was.
Even after being left alone, Kuan showed no change in emotion and just
stared at the sky.
"Phew!"
Then suddenly, he burst into laughter.
His wounds throbbed, and his face twisted in pain, but his lungs still
felt itchy as he panted.
'Hehe, what does she want to do with me? With this body, I can't even
cut a steak anymore.'
Fighting Nameless, Kuan had pierced through a needle's eye of
probability to reach his current state.
But as a one-armed, limping man, he felt ashamed to fulfill the role of
a woman's partner.
'Yes, it's all over. This place is my grave.'
As a wave of depression, making him want to give up everything, washed
over him, the bleeding he had been barely holding back started again.
-Let's have a meal together.
"Hah."
Kuan let out a sigh that was almost a groan.
"Really… people can drive you crazy."
