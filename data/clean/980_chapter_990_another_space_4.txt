Chapter 990:
Another Space (4)
Hwangseong Yeomra.
Shirone, accompanied by the Earth Dragon Gaitan and the White Dragon
Asraiker, knocked on the gates of Yeomra.
Before confirming their identities, the gatekeepers stood in a daze, as
if their souls had left their bodies.
'Such a beauty in this world... White Dragon Asraiker.'
Her appearance, which reached the pinnacle of all living beings, left
not only the soldiers but also the officials in a daze with their mouths agape.
"I am Shirone of the Five Stars of the Ivory Tower. The Emperor of
Jincheon..."
As everyone's gaze was fixed on Asraiker, Shirone cleared his
throat.
"Ahem, we are here to see His Majesty the Emperor."
There was no response.
As Shirone sighed, already familiar with the situation, Asraiker spoke
directly.
"This is Shirone of the Five Stars of the Ivory Tower. May we have
an audience with His Majesty the Emperor?"
"What? Ah, yes! Please wait a moment!"
Even the gatekeepers of Yeomra, known for their rigidity, willingly
complied due to the overwhelming presence of the White Dragon.
Three minutes later, guards arrived at the gate with a small but
luxurious carriage.
The same situation occurred as before, and even the maids were
mesmerized by her.
'...I shouldn't go to crowded places.'
A few words exchanged were fine.
But if Asraiker conversed with a specific person for more than five
minutes, the situation became serious.
'They would risk their lives.'
Many kings, leaders, artists, scholars, and clergy throughout history
had died from lovesickness.
Even more frightening was the will of a king who took his own life 1,400
years ago.
'He said it was better to know her and die now than to live forever
without knowing her.'
Such was the fear of the White Dragon who dealt with souls.
Upon arriving at the inner court, the Grand General of Jincheon,
Oryongjang, was performing a martial arts demonstration on a high platform.
They were Yucheong, known as the incarnation of the sword, and Hwang
Iseong, nicknamed the demon of tyranny.
'It's a close match.'
Shirone briefly admired the dazzling martial arts of the two, wielding
swords and axes.
Not stopping the martial arts demonstration despite the visit of the
Five Stars was a unique characteristic of the Jincheon Empire.
"Ta-ha!"
As Hwang Iseong raised his axe and charged, it felt as if a wave was
rising from behind him.
Yucheong, who thrust his sword forward, dashed like a flash and
shattered the wave, causing a strong wind to blow.
Gaitan stood with his arms crossed indifferently, but Asraiker's
expression grew increasingly displeased.
'These people...'
Were they deliberately ignoring the Messiah who would save the
world?
She turned to the officer and spoke.
"Are all the generals of the Jincheon Empire this rude? It's a bit
disappointing."
"Oh, I'm sorry!"
Although he was the Grand General, the pinnacle of the military, the
officer responded immediately.
'They are relying on me.'
Showing an unworthy appearance in front of Asraiker was worse than
death.
"Grand General!"
The officer, making his voice thick unlike a civil servant, shouted
loudly while clasping his hands.
"Shirone of the Five Stars of the Ivory Tower has arrived! You must
show proper courtesy!"
Yucheong and Hwang Iseong's movements came to an abrupt halt.
'Are they crazy?'
They already knew about Shirone's visit.
But the martial arts demonstration of Oryongjang, symbolizing the power
of Jincheon, was not to be stopped even by the Emperor.
"Stopping a warrior's demonstration is..."
Hwang Iseong, who had placed his axe on his shoulder, frowned but soon
his expression softened.
"Huh?"
The moment he saw the woman staring at him, he felt as if his soul was
ascending.
'Who is that woman?'
Hwang Iseong was straightforward by nature.
Moreover, his wife was Princess Yeon, the most beautiful woman in the
Moon Kingdom, but he couldn't ignore the feeling of extreme admiration.
'I want to have a conversation with her, even if it's just a
word.'
He swallowed hard, and even Yucheong beside him couldn't take his eyes
off her.
"Ah..."
Hwang Iseong belatedly showed his respect.
"I was so engrossed in the battle that I didn't notice. I am Grand
General Hwang Iseong."
Yucheong, not to be outdone, clasped his hands while holding his
sword.
"I am Yucheong of the Oryongjang of Jincheon. Please forgive my
rudeness. When it comes to the sword, there is no compromise..."
Shirone just wanted to enter the inner court.
"No, it's fine. I came because I have urgent news for His Majesty
the Emperor."
Asraiker added.
"Thank you for your kind hospitality. Please continue your training
without worrying about us."
"Oh, yes. Then..."
As the two Grand Generals resumed their demonstration, Shirone walked
towards the inner court.
Perhaps conscious of Asraiker, the sound of the cold weapons clashing
was several times louder than before.
'They are watching.'
As Asraiker passed by the platform, she slowly turned her head to watch
the demonstration.
'They are watching me.' Yucheong and Hwang Iseong, thinking the same
thing, exerted all their strength.
A loud roar echoed throughout Yeomra.
"Yaaah!"
Yucheong's face turned red up to his ears as he was pushed back by Hwang
Iseong's strike.
'Ugh!'
Still feeling Asraiker's gaze.
'What a disgrace!'
Thinking about how insignificant she would see him made him feel like
dying.
"Hwang Iseong! Do you think lightly of me?"
As the martial arts demonstration turned into a deadly battle, Hwang
Iseong also used his ultimate technique.
In a situation where it wouldn't be strange if one of them died, Shirone
suddenly stopped walking.
Without looking back, he could imagine the scene of the demonstration
just by the sound.
"Here I come, Yucheong!"
Asraiker watched indifferently as the deadly strike aimed at his
comrade.
'I will win! The one who will be loved by her is...'
Just as the cold weapons were about to crush each other's bodies, a
blinding light exploded.
"Ugh!"
Everyone around felt their hearts calm down, drawn by an unknown
force.
"Huff! Huff!"
The two Grand Generals, barely stopping their swords, panted with dazed
expressions.
'What is this? Why did I...'
Try to kill my comrade?
As the two suddenly realized and turned their heads, they saw Shirone
facing them.
"Asraiker."
At the cold voice, her shoulders flinched.
"Yes, Messiah."
"I told you clearly, didn't I? Never stare at a specific person for
too long."
The White Dragon was a weapon in itself.
Just staring at someone could stir their hearts.
"I'm sorry. I didn't realize how much the Messiah was sacrificing
and acted out of pride..."
Although he understood her feelings, if this continued, the unification
of humanity would be difficult.
"Someone almost died. If you think you can add something just
because you are strong, there is no need for you to be by my side."
At Shirone's stern words, Asraiker's face turned pale.
"I'm sorry! I won't do it again!"
"Let's go."
As she followed dejectedly, people stared in a daze.
A similar situation occurred in the inner court, but she didn't make eye
contact with anyone.
Upon reaching the throne room, Jingang spoke.
"You have arrived?"
'Emperor Jingang of Jincheon.' His steel-like gaze was unchanged, but
everything else seemed precarious.
Setting aside his emaciated appearance, there was no vitality that a
person should have.
"I have something to tell you."
"If it's about the matter with the Demon Realm, I've already been
informed. The Empire's doctors are working on a countermeasure."
Jingang glanced at the two apostles guarding Shirone.
Asraiker made eye contact, but there was no change in Jingang's
emotions.
'What a soul.'
She felt pity.
'He's alive, but not really living. There's nothing more to squeeze
out.'
Any parent would feel the same if they thought their child was writhing
in eternal pain.
"I will go to the underworld."
A glimmer of light appeared in Jingang's eyes.
"If we purify hell, the Demon Realm will be destroyed. Preparations
are underway in the Kingdom of Tormia."
"...I see."
The reason he didn't ask about Seongeum was probably because he was
still the Emperor of Jincheon.
"I can't save her. But if hell is purified, I can release her from
her suffering."
Jingang's eyes reddened.
"Seongeum, the magician, saved humanity from the depths of despair.
We were able to fight this far because of her. If I meet her in hell, I will take responsibility and
kill her myself."
Jingang struggled to hold back his tears.
The Emperor Jincheon wanted was a ruler of all people, not a human bound
by familial love.
"I will soon enter the underworld. Before that, I came to seek
permission for Seongeum's destruction."
He knew why he was seeking permission.
"...If my daughter is destroyed, the spiritual realm will open
again."
"Yes. Of course, if hell is purified, there will be no problem. But
if we have to choose between the two..."
The purification of hell and the destruction of Seongeum.
"I will choose to release Seongeum, even if the spiritual realm
opens again."
Now it was the Emperor's turn to respond.
"Then Jincheon will fall. So will humanity. Are you asking me to
abandon the empire and save my daughter?"
"It depends on Your Majesty's heart." A heavy silence
fell.
"You knew the outcome of Seongeum and still made the choice. Do you
regret sending your daughter like that?"
"Regret...?"
Jingang turned his head, recalling his feelings at the time, but he
could no longer speak.
"If you can't choose, I will."
As Shirone was about to lead the apostles out of the throne room, a
hoarse voice was heard.
"If it were me..."
Jingang stood up.
"If it were me, I wouldn't have been able to do what my daughter
did."
Then he respectfully clasped his hands and deeply bowed to
Shirone.
"Please release the hero of Jincheon."
"I will do my best."
Leaving those words, Shirone headed for the door, and all the officials
bowed and shouted in unison.
"Please release the hero of Jincheon!"
The sight of a living human burning is more beautiful than any
flame...
"Whoo!"
For a moment, such a crazy thought crossed his mind.
"Rian!"
If life were visible, wouldn't it be burning in that form?
Whether standing and breathing or crawling on the floor, the flame
always reached upward.
'The fire is lit. What should I do now?'
As his body, filled with pain, endlessly regenerated, Rian wandered
aimlessly.
'It's much harder than I thought.'
The thought that it would be better to die quickly grew and overwhelmed
his mind.
"Ugh!"
He would not scream.
Rian made that vow and thought he kept it until the end.
Even as Tess shed tears watching him writhe and scream on the floor for
three hours.
"Rian, Rian!"
Even two hours later, when Clump bit his lips until they bled.
"Ahhh!"
Rian thought he never screamed.
"Shirone! It's the limit. He's going to go mad! Let him rest for a
while."
Shirone coldly shook his head.
"If we stop now, we'll have to repeat this process from the
beginning. This is the best way."
Tess covered her ears at Rian's screams.
"Please! Please stop!"
"Ha ha."
When Tess turned her head at Rian's laughter, he was already
standing.
"Heh heh! Heh heh heh!"
At first, she thought he had gone mad.
But his voice, clearly focused on something, was unwavering.
"I can see it, Shirone."
As the flames surged, countless scenes overlapped, not of this
reality.
"Oh..."
In Tess's retinas, the demons screamed.
