A form of irregular magic that allows the caster to output and replicate
what they have memorized in their mind.
"This thing's pretty expensive. And the reason is…"

was perfectly duplicated.
"Because it can revolutionize labor. As long as you memorize it once,

That was why time had been so tight.
"So? What are you planning to do with it?"

"Like our little personal tastes. Those should never leak out,

"Wanna die?"

Image Copy.

A future entirely different from God's will.
'I will wait.'

'Until the true king returns.'
