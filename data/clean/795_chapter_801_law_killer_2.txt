Law Killer (2)

"How can this be…"
Despite Havitz's notorious reputation spreading worldwide, over 300

"Hmph, you're the same. I'd prefer we keep quiet about it. From now

"I'm not…"
Unlike the other candidates, Abella had come to kill Havitz.
"Is it for the money?"
The woman looked back coldly.
"What else? Regardless of background, the pay is unbelievable, and
the employee benefits are excellent. Above all, you become a maid at the imperial palace."
A commoner's worth is determined by the nobility, and being a maid at
the palace was a position even the nobles couldn't look down upon.
"But he's a tyrant, isn't he?"
Everyone turned to look at Abella, and the atmosphere at the scene
froze like the Arctic.
"Hey, what did you just say?" The captain of the guard, leading the
maids, approached menacingly.
"It's okay. I'll be fine."
The most crucial part of the project for Abella was pretending to
love Havitz.
If Alpha Fish loses its original purpose, the law twists, and then
everything ends.
"Repeat what you just said. You'd better pray my ears are deceiving
me."
"Havitz is a tyrant." The guard captain drew his sword at her
disrespectful reply.
"If you kill me, you die too."
The blade stopped abruptly near Abella's neck.
"What do you mean?"
"I didn't come here to serve the emperor. I came to meet Havitz
personally."
"You know His Majesty the Emperor?"
"If you're suspicious, just report it to your superiors. Tell Havitz
that Abella is here." He didn't have the courage to do that.
"This is troublesome."
Moreover, if her words were true, it would be imperative not to
commit any rudeness.
"Let someone else handle it. Getting involved could be
deadly."
The captain sheathed his sword and returned to his position,
muttering.
"Stay quiet. Everything has a procedure."
He was clearly retreating, and Abella stuck out her tongue at the
captain's back.
A woman watching the absurd scene close by spoke with shocked
eyes.
"Are you crazy? Why would you tell such a lie?"
While the candidate was quick-witted, if the truth came out, she
would be executed on the spot.
"For now, I need to survive. And that captain won't check, he's too

The woman smirked and extended her hand.
"Let's formally introduce ourselves. I'm Catenia from Manaka,

"Yes, I'm Abella, twenty-three."
After introducing themselves, Catenia asked.
"So, you really have guts? How can you insult the emperor of the
empire so easily?"
Honestly, I don't know.
"Havitz."
When I hear about his nearly criminal acts, it makes my blood boil,
but somewhere inside...
-It's okay. It's okay.
When he patted my back and said it was okay, it was the first time in
my life I felt I had a fence.

Abella shrugged nonchalantly.
"I mean... I'm honestly scared too. But being older, I hope I won't

Unlike Catenia, Abella was of an age to potentially share Havitz's
bed.
"It's okay. I actually plan to become a concubine."
"What?"
Even if the money was good, Catenia would have reconsidered if it
meant being close to Havitz.

The guards opened the gate, and thirty people rushed through the
arch.
True to being the largest man-made structure in the world, the

"We're here."

first interview.
The assassin of Queen Woorin, El Crouch.
Once a doll maker, he had learned black magic from a doll maker in

"I have two lives left." Having distributed his life among porcelain

"Well, I've lived long enough."
On the day he left Kashan, Woorin had said.
"I'm sorry, Crouch."

"We've known each other a long time. No one but you could transfer

Crouch's mission was a transporter.

"It's not because you have two lives that I entrusted you with this.

"This life was given by you, Your Majesty. I won't resent it even if

"That's not what I meant."
