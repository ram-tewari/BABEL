'This is terrifying. Damn it.'

"Huh? Huuh?"
The Dragon's Tooth Soldier arm came crashing down at a terrifying
speed.
As the virtual sword passed through Thad's body, the percentage gauge
floating above his head dropped by 50 percent.
Thad tried hard to maintain a calm expression.
"As you can see, Teacher Thad's mental strength has been reduced by
half. This is because being struck by the sword would result in death. Since the synchronization rate is
at 50 percent, only half of his mental power was drained. If the mage is judged to be dead, the hologram
will penetrate. This is referred to as a 'Penetration.' Now, let me show you a defense scenario. Teacher
Thad?"
As expected of a seasoned teacher, Thad immediately cast his magic. As
he employed Air Skin, a layer of compressed air enveloped his body, and the percentage gauge only
decreased by 1 percent.
Since mental strength is quantified, the gauge decreases not only when
attacked but also when casting spells.
"Now, let's take a look at the Dragon's Tooth Soldier's attack."
The Dragon's Tooth Soldier raised its arm and swung its sword down. This
time, unlike the penetration scenario, the blade stopped as if it had struck Thad's head. The percentage
gauge decreased by 1.8 percent.
Olivia nodded in satisfaction.
"Impressive. By spending 1 percent of his mental strength on Air Skin,
Teacher Thad managed to mitigate 48.2 percent of the attack's damage. In a real battle, this means that
despite being hit by a full-force strike from the Dragon's Tooth Soldier, his mental state remains
mostly intact. You see how strong Teacher Thad is now, right?"
All the students answered with a loud "Yes." If an attack
strong enough to reduce a 6th-rank certified mage's mental power by 1.8 percent could do that much
damage, it would be fatal for the advanced students even to graze it.
Thad puffed out his chest with pride. There was no need to reveal that
he had rapidly deployed a complex sequence of defensive magics just before the impact.
"When the hologram doesn't pass through, it's called a 'block.'
Penetration and block—these are the two judgments made during an attack, and they involve using
anti-magic and mana control techniques to drain mental strength."
Mana control is a technique that blocks specific circuits in the brain,
preventing concentration.
Spirit Zone involves the activation of the entire brain, so even if only
part of the cerebral network is blocked, it becomes impossible to maintain focus.
When one circuit is blocked, the brain immediately reroutes the
information through another pathway.
However, since the magic control device also changes the blocking path
at a rate of 20 times per second, a phenomenon occurs where thinking is possible but entering Spirit
Zone is impossible.
Of course, it is possible to bypass the blocking route if one can
surpass the speed of 20 blocks per second and account for irregular patterns.
However, the Teacher Association's official stance was that it was
impossible for anyone other than a certified 2nd-rank archmage or higher.
Although this is indeed an impressive technique, the students weren't
particularly interested in mana control.
Since it is a technology that causes mental disorders and was originally
developed to be used on criminals, it was natural that it would not be of interest to anyone unless they
aspire to be an engineer.
But they couldn't help but be afraid of the word anti-magic.
"Principal, about Anti-Magic…"
"Yes, I understand your concerns. It is a magic that can damage the
mind. But don't worry, unless the synchronization rate is above 80 percent, there's no significant
risk."
Olivia firmly concluded the topic and moved on to the next
subject.
"Now, let's move on to attack judgments, known as 'Impact.' Teacher
Thad?"
This time, it was the Dragon's Tooth Soldier's turn to be attacked. But
Thad couldn't help but feel hesitant.
Without the use of holy magic, one would need the physical power to
crush a Dragon's Tooth Soldier or the firepower to melt it.
While it wasn't impossible to defeat the Dragon's Tooth Soldier if he
gave it his all, he didn't want to struggle in front of the students.
"Um, the Dragon's Tooth Soldier …"
"Oh, I see. I'll handle it."

She raised her hand slightly while looking at the Dragon's Tooth Soldier
and cast the holy magic Holy Cross.
As a radiant light burst from her palm, a large cross of light exploded
against the Dragon's Tooth Soldier's chest.
The impact caused the Dragon's Tooth Soldier to stagger and
retreat.
"This is what we call 'Impact.' Although virtual information can't
physically push your body, a hologram certainly can. The death judgment of a monster also falls under
the category of 'Impact.' Let me show you an example."
Olivia gently pursed her lips, and a high-pitched sound emitted from
between her sensuously parted lips.
When Holy Cross was cast, a cross four times larger than the previous
one obliterated the Dragon's Tooth Soldier.
The sight of the bones dancing in the air left the students
wide-eyed.
Teachers were just as stunned.

"Come forth, Merces."
Boyle summoned a Merces from the Arctic Sea.
This creature, part of the Monstera Echinodermata order, was a spherical
organism about 50 centimeters in diameter with spiky cilia growing from its surface.
Though it lacked intelligence, once it latched onto a target, it would
emit cold air in an attempt to recreate its natural environment. Therefore, it is the monster of choice

As a creature that couldn't survive for more than a minute at room
