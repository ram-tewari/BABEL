Chapter 562:
"Woman… what?"
Amy asked with a bewildered expression, but the boys who had perused the
red book had nothing to say.
"Ahem, anyway, I don't know who the president of the Women's
Anatomy Research Group is."
Amy recalled what she had heard in the dining hall.
"So that was the red book? Shirone, have you seen it
too?"
"Huh? Oh, well… there's a book, and I have eyes…"
It was a ridiculous excuse, but lying wouldn't work in this
situation.
Surprisingly, Amy didn't scold him. Instead, she turned her head with a
relieved expression.
If she knew the subjects in the red book were people from the school,
her reaction would be different, but that was a secret to take to the grave.
Having dodged a bullet, Shirone asked, "But you must have some
candidates in mind. Who do you think it is?"
"Hmm, it's really hard to say. It's so well-hidden that I can't
even guess. But if I had to pick…"
Nade looked around the store's counter.
"It could surprisingly be Eden."
"Ma'am, one soda, please."
Shirone turned to look at Eden, who had just entered the store.
Currently ranked 10th in the graduation class.
A devout follower of the Yor Church and a specialist in defensive
magic.
'That's right, Gaold was once a follower of the Yor Church too.'
Given that the religion abhors violence, it made sense that Eden focused
solely on defensive magic. Yet, her ability to stay in Class One showed her exceptional magical
talent.
"But why is Eden a candidate?"
"I heard that Eden gets stronger the more she
undresses."
Shirone spat his cocoa back into his mug.
"What nonsense is that?"
"You know, there are things that enhance abilities through special
actions, even if they're not official techniques. Magical Action is one of those. The Yor Church is
non-violent, so they don't carry weapons. In other words, they believe God protects them, so they don't
even need clothes."
"Hmm, could that be true?"
Amy spoke up.
"I've heard that too. That's why some guys in last year's
graduation class went all out during combat sessions."
The three boys asked in unison, "So what happened?"
Amy looked at them with disdain.
"When she was cornered, she did take off her outer clothes. Of
course, her defensive magic became even stronger."
Nade nodded.
"Exactly. According to sources, when Eden is completely undressed,
her abilities become so strong that even professional mages can't scratch her."
Iruki said, "If that's true, she was unlucky in last year's
graduation exam."
Amy recalled the events.
"The first part ended quickly under Fermi's lead, and the second
part was a mental strength test, so magic wasn't crucial. But that's the graduation exam. They evaluate
two out of six categories randomly, so you have to be prepared for everything. Got it? We don't have
time to worry about this."
Amy pointed and lectured, but no one was really listening.
"If that's the case, it's not strange for Eden to be a candidate.
But the main feature of the Women's Anatomy Research Group is their incredible drawing skills,
right?"
"Hmm, in that case, Conger is also a possibility."
Nade pointed to Conger, ranked 6th in the graduation class.
Despite his large build and orangutan-like face, he was shyly playing
cat's cradle.
As a steel mage who could transmute metals, even the strings for cat's
cradle were made of steel.
"He's feminine and good with his hands. His appearance might be
off-putting, but that makes it more fitting."
Shirone shook his head.
"He has a unique personality, but he's a good person."
Conger was loyal and kind to everyone.
"I think the most suspicious one is Fjord."
A student in a mage's robe with a hood, breaking the legs of
insects.
Because of this, the area around him was empty.
A poison mage, he was short and frail, with a face that looked about
sixteen, but that was due to drug side effects. He was actually twenty-three.
Originally in Class One, he had been pushed to 11th place by the
juniors' progress.
Iruki agreed with Shirone's assessment.
"He's the most wicked. His balance isn't great, but he was voted
the student people least wanted to face in combat. If he's the president of the Women's Anatomy Research
Group, it'll be a tough fight."
Nade clutched his head and shook it.
"Ugh, I don't know. If we think like this, anyone could be a
candidate. Honestly, who doesn't like women's anatomy?"
Amy squinted and said, "Do whatever you want. I'm not getting
involved."
Iruki said, "Anyway, we can assume these are our enemies. And they
have advanced class members, so they might attack during graduation class sessions. Like this morning.
So Shirone, you have to protect Istas."
Shirone had suddenly become the gatekeeper of Istas.
"Got it. I'll handle it."
He was confident he could fend off any advanced class intruders.
Amy checked the time and stood up.
"Let's go. And Shirone, don't use Time Explosion in Istas for now.
It could be dangerous."
"Yeah, I was thinking the same."
As the students left to continue their afternoon graduation class
schedule, only Shirone and the store owner remained.
"Hmm, underground research groups."
The last drop of cocoa slid down Shirone's throat.
That evening, at the Golden Wheel.
The executives were gathered at their hideout in the northeastern
mountains of Alpheas Magic School.
Unusually, there was no banquet, and Fermi sipped strong liquor in the
cold atmosphere, lost in thought.
'Miro has returned.'
That meant nothing could hinder Fermi's plans anymore.
'This might be my last year at the school.'
Finishing his thoughts, he looked up and asked, "Is everyone
here?"
"Yeah, except for that one."
The Golden Wheel had five executives, including Fermi.
Present were Sona's Hershey, the Electric Monster Lycan, and the
alchemist Richard.
"Why the sudden gathering?"
"Today, Prings proposed an alliance."
"The Hide-and-Seek Research Group?"
Hershey, the strategist, added, "It seems they've already proposed
to other research groups. The presidents of the Doll Collection and Ant Language groups asked me about
its authenticity."
"In that case…"
As the executives' eyes gleamed, Fermi smiled.
"Yes, it's a scramble royale. The target is the Paranormal Psychic
Science Research Group. We'll drive them out and take over Istas."
"Hehehe, the long-awaited day has finally come."
Richard, the alchemist, grinned as his eyes glowed blue.
"That means we need to send a representative to participate in the
scramble royale."
"I'll do it."
Lycan, the Electric Monster and second-in-command of the Golden Wheel,
raised his hand.
Currently ranked second in the graduation class, he was a powerhouse
only Fermi could control.
"No, not this time. You have something else to do with
me."
Without question, Lycan accepted Fermi's decision.
"Then who will you send?"
"Hershey will participate. The scramble royale is just a
preliminary skirmish to get closer to Istas's secret. Gathering as much information as possible is
crucial."
In that sense, Hershey was the perfect choice.
Though he had fallen to 13th in the graduation class rankings, his
information-gathering skills rivaled those of the Hide-and-Seek Research Group.
"So our side will have Hershey, Prings for Hide-and-Seek, Anchal
for Doll Collection, and Pisho for Ant Language. Four participants?"
"No, we'll also include the Women's Anatomy Research
Group."
"But they don't usually participate."
"They will this time. Go tell them to join."
Fermi was confident.
"If Shirone moves, they won't have a choice but to
join."
Past midnight, Hershey arrived at the dormitory and stood in front of a
door.
Following someone was impossible.
Her sound magic, Sona, could pick up even the sound of a heartbeat if
she wanted.
'No one can stop their heart.'
With a confident smile, Hershey was about to knock when a voice came
from inside.
"Come in."
Opening the door revealed a scene of madness.
The room was covered in drawings, all featuring Maya as the
subject.
"Your intuition is still sharp, Kaiden."
Kaiden of the Cross Star, currently ranked 7th in the graduation
class.
An executive of the Golden Wheel and leader of the Women's Anatomy
Research Group.
"What do you want?"
Hershey approached the table and watched Kaiden work.
The unbroken line perfectly captured Maya's likeness.
"You know why I'm here?"
"No."
"The underground research groups proposed a scramble
royale."
"I'm not interested."
"And Shirone is on the opposing side."
The line drawing Maya's neckline suddenly veered off course.
"Do I look like someone who acts out of petty
jealousy?"
Hershey just laughed.
Kaiden's honest appearance matched his upright personality. A
cross-shaped longsword stood in one corner of the room.
Ironically, Kaiden was originally a swordsman.
Specifically, he was a promising talent from the Cross family, the most
famous swordsman family in Tormia Kingdom.
'Swordsmanship and magic. Truly a monster.'
People born into the Cross family under the influence of the Cross Star
were born with special talents determined by their star signs.
Kaiden was born on Red Cross Star Day, which occurs once every 43 years.
He could maximize his potential in any field.
A kind of universal law allowed him to graduate at the top of Kaizen
Swordsmanship School at fourteen.
But if the legend that children born under Red Cross Star lead
unfortunate lives was true, Kaiden had a stronger passion than swordsmanship: art, specifically
drawing.
Of course, Red Cross Star's law granted him exceptional drawing
skills.
But art requires more than just skill; it needs something to elevate it
to true artistry.
Kaiden could draw anything he imagined perfectly but lacked what it took
to turn it into art.
"Impressive. How about making it a bit more daring?"
Hershey pointed at the drawing, causing Kaiden to frown.
"Don't insult art."
If art's power lies in evoking human emotions, then the human body is
inherently artistic regardless of gender.
Kaiden's lack of artistic expression might have led him to draw
semi-nudes as his only option.
"So… you're not participating?"
Kaiden silently stood up and raised his family's cross-shaped longsword
vertically, looking at his reflection in its blade.
"…I will."
'I knew it.'
No one could match Fermi's ability to read and manipulate human
emotions.
Kaiden just needed time to rationalize it. Using terms like "petty
jealousy" was proof he was already jealous.
"Then we're all set. I'll inform you of the schedule
later."
As Hershey left the room, Kaiden looked at a large drawing on the
wall.
He had abandoned his family and fled to Alpheas Magic School like an
escapee.
Meeting Maya here made him realize why he lacked artistic sensitivity:
The god of art had chosen Maya instead.
"Maya…"
Recalling the divine thrill he felt when he first heard her sing, Kaiden
knelt before his drawing.
"If I can be by your side, I'll do anything."
It was his private knightly vow to his artwork.
