Chapter 708:
The Arctic.
In the middle of an endless expanse of ice, the Ivory Tower rose towards
the sky.
Usually a serene place dedicated to pure academic pursuit, today the
atmosphere was different.
'This is truly strange.'
Araka, the chief internal affairs officer, stroked his beard as he
pondered in front of the elevator leading to the Grand Sanctuary.
'A gathering of all the stars currently residing in the Ivory
Tower.'
It was the first time in 29 years that the Great Star had summoned stars
from each department instead of holding individual meetings.
"It's been a while, Araka."
The first to arrive was Arte, a three-star resident from the Balance
Department, known as the Ghost Goblin Star.
"Welcome, Star of Wisdom."
Arte, dressed neatly and waving a paper fan, greeted him.
Satellites were excluded from this meeting, so Tokei was not
present.
"This is an unusual event, gathering so many."
"Oh? Arte, you're here too?"
Zuo Yi, a two-star resident from the System Inspection Department,
walked down the mirror-like smooth corridor.
"Yes. I arrived yesterday."
The number of stars varied by rank, but hierarchy within the Ivory Tower
was not strictly defined.
"Do you know what's going on? Personally, it's my first time seeing
all the stars gathered."
Arte fanned himself and replied,
"The last time something like this happened was when a giant meteor
was predicted to collide with the planet. The Five Stars managed to prevent it…"
Most of humanity was unaware of this fact.
"Hmm, a giant meteor. What could be as significant as
that?"
"It likely concerns the altar. It might also be related to Ra
Enemy's current state."
A rough voice came from down the corridor.
"Well, well, all the unlucky stars have gathered here."
A man dressed in Eastern blue robes and covered in yellow talismans
walked towards them, his face completely obscured.
He was Heukgangsi, a three-star resident known as Law Teacher from the
Law Department. He managed all flows of law in the world and was a master of Incarnation Magic who had
sealed his own incarnation.
Arte spoke coldly,
"Unlucky is your perspective. Being summoned by the Great Star is
an honor."
"Heh! Is that so? Well, each to their own judgment. But even Ghost
Goblin fears the Great Star?"
Arte snapped his fan shut with a sharp sound.
"Mocking others only reflects your own thoughts. If that's your
limit, it applies more to you."
"…"
Heukgangsi remained silent, but a fierce battle of killing intent raged
between them.
Zuo Yi didn't mind, but Araka, despite being 117 years old, broke into a
cold sweat.
'This is why they shouldn't gather.'
With their extreme convictions and stubbornness, any clash could lead to
major incidents.
"I'm late. Hello. Nice to see you."
A woman with her legs bent backward over her shoulders crawled in like a
spider.
Her long black hair dragged on the ground; her pale face lacked eyebrows
and eyelashes; her lips were skin-colored and indistinguishable from her face.
She was Borbor from the Balance Department, known as Mollusk. Her
flexibility wasn't just physical; she had no fixed opinions and could even communicate with
microorganisms. She was a master of mental magic with extraordinary mental fluidity.
Zuo Yi blinked and asked,
"Borbor? I heard you went south? Did you resolve the demon
terrorist incident?"
"No, that…"
A voice came from Borbor's crown,
"I told her to come."
Upon closer inspection, a thumb-sized woman clung to Borbor's hair. Her
face was so small it was hard to make out her features without careful observation, but she was clearly
beautiful.
She was Mini from the Integrated Universe Management Department, known
as Super Giant. She specialized in scale magic and often stuck close to Borbor out of loneliness.
Zuo Yi frowned at Mini,
"How could you call her away from her mission?"
"It's fine. Borbor said it was okay."
"No, that's not right. Borbor would agree to anything…"
Mini hugged Borbor's crown,
"And we're best friends. Right, Borbor?"
"Huh? Oh, right. We're best friends."
Mini beamed at Arte and waved,
"You still look great, Arte. Want to sleep with me?"
Arte hid his face behind his fan while still engaged in a battle of
wills with Heukgangsi,
"I'll pass. I don't want to drown."
He then turned his gaze to Araka,
"It seems everyone is here now? Shall we go?"
"Great Star would be disappointed if we left without
me."
Everyone turned to see an elderly man with white hair approaching in a
suit and smoking a pipe. His neatly trimmed beard and non-dreamlike attire caught everyone's
attention.
"Even Luber…?"
"He called us specially. It must be important."
Luber was a four-star resident known as Dreamer from the Integrated
Universe Management Department but originally from Drimo. He currently managed human dreams.
'What could be so important that it requires a four-star
resident?'
Arte pondered behind his fan while Heukgangsi also sensed the gravity of
the situation this time.
Borbor looked up and said,
"Shall we go up now? Is everyone here? Hello?"
"Not yet… one more is coming."
Luber turned his head towards what appeared to be a human brain floating
in mid-air.
It was just a brain and thus named Brain.
"…"
Brain was a four-star resident known as Someone's Brain from the Human
Safety Enforcement Department. It appeared in various forms through illusion magic but never spoke to
anyone.
As Brain cast magic, it transformed into a woman with long hair
revealing her forehead.
"You always turn pretty when I'm around," Mini grumbled, but
Brain ignored her and walked towards the elevator.
Luber said,
"Now we're all here. Inform Great Star."
"Understood."
Araka wasn't young either but couldn't compare to Dreamer's time
span.
'It's fortunate Luber is here.'
Even as a three-star resident controlling dreams, Dreamer wouldn't act
recklessly before the Great Star.
"Great Star, the stars request an audience."
While waiting for their summons, Zuo Yi asked,
"If it's something as big as a meteor collision, could it really be
about the altar?"
Heukgangsi shook his head,
"It's not urgent. The Council of Ten haven't shown any significant
movement either. I think it's related to the Ivory Tower test."
Mini said,
"You mean Nane?"
Borbor rolled her eyes upward,
"Nane is dangerous. His Sword Transformation is impressive. But is
it as serious as a meteor collision?"
"It might be more serious," Arte said,
"It's happening much faster than predicted. At this rate, he'll
reach enlightenment before taking the test."
Zuo Yi stroked her chin,
"Could it be because of Shirone?"
Dreamer Luber turned his head slightly at this suggestion while Mini
raised an eyebrow,
"Hexa is also significant but not bigger than Nane."
"There were rumors that the Great Star cast one vote for Shirone.
It might have been ceremonial…"
"No way. Even I doubt I could handle Nane. It's not
comparable."
Until recently, Zuo Yi thought so too,
"But after meeting Shirone…"
Before she could finish speaking, a pillar of light descended into the
elevator shaft and whisked away their forms.
Screams echoed through the labyrinth.
"Stay calm! The Andre Tourism Department has a counter-terrorism
unit and is supported by the Ministry of Land Management…!"
The guide tried to calm the crowd, but people, gripped by fear, ran
towards the exit without looking back.
"Rian, get ready. We won't be able to stop them."
Even the counter-terrorism unit of the vast Kashan Empire would be
helpless against the S-class Maga Bandits.
Kido spoke up,
"Are you sure? Even if we open all the doors simultaneously, we
need to maintain the Spirit Zone. The main body must stay intact."
The likelihood of maintaining mental integrity while multiplying into
19,000 versions was nearly zero.
"Shirone will be fine. I'll stay too."
Hearing the distant sounds of battle, Rian drew his greatsword.
"Go. I'll protect this place while you break the
seals."
Rian could be trusted.
"Please. Kido, be careful too."
As Shirone dashed deeper into the labyrinth, the guide reached out and
shouted,
"Where are you going? There's a secret passage here!"
"We're not leaving!"
The guide, stunned by their response, turned to Rian and Kido.
"You're not leaving either? The bandits are attacking!"
Kido, having gathered all the water bottles dropped by tourists, drank
deeply and waved his hand dismissively.
"You should run. Soon, the seals of the labyrinth will be broken.
Then you'll know what hell is."
"…They're crazy."
Convinced they were mad, she ran towards the secret passage while Rian
assessed the situation at the entrance.
"They're impressive. Hardly any stragglers."
Nearly 40 Maga Bandits had quickly overpowered the counter-terrorism
unit and were charging towards Andre.
"Is that woman from the Ten Demons here too?"
Rian frowned at Kido, who was continuously drinking water.
"Why are you drinking so much? How will you fight with your belly
sloshing around?"
"Huh? Oh, sorry."
Kido walked over with his tongue hanging out like a dog, and Rian sensed
something was off.
"What's wrong? Is there a problem?"
"No, nothing. Just thirsty from crossing the desert."
He said that, but his thoughts were different.
'This is strange. Why am I still so thirsty?'
No matter how much water he drank, his thirst wasn't quenched.
'This is driving me crazy. I want more.'
It felt like an insatiable hunger or a parched land craving rain.
"They're coming, Kido."
Standing beside Rian, he saw Paknyeo and the Maga Bandits covered in
sand approaching.
"They don't look tired at all. Can we handle this? Shirone said it
would take at least seven days."
They had to hold off the Maga Bandits for seven days.
"We won't need that long. I'll cut them down."
"That's ridiculous! That woman is a monster…"
Kido's eyes widened as he saw an intense aura emanating from Rian that
he'd never seen before.
'He's serious?'
Rian's divine transcendence was always powerful…
'I will protect Shirone.'
But when it came to protecting Shirone, his incarnation was
unmatched.
"I'm coming!"
Divine Transcendence - Deny.
With a will that defied the laws like a Mach force, Rian's body shot
towards Paknyeo.
