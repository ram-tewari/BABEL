sphere.

'No one knows yet.'
No one knew that she had opened the sanctuary door on her own or that

The lack of response from Anke Ra meant...
'He plans to cover it up.'

"I need to meet Anke Ra."

"Orders have been given for all angels to stand by! No one can

"It's alright."
Ikael opened the door.
"He will be waiting for me."
Flying into the sky of the Archangel's domain, Ikael headed straight for

Where did she find such courage?
'No, it was my choice.'
The heart can choose anything, but one must also take responsibility for
those choices.
"Anke Ra, it's Ikael."
Receiving no response, she reached out to the door.
Just before her fingertips touched it, the door creaked and slowly moved
inward.
Seeing Anke Ra pulsating as usual, Ikael took a deep breath.
Goffin was still caught in Anke Ra's nerves, and her heart ached

"Come in, my daughter."

Rumble!

"Why..."

"Yes. And I want to know exactly what happened during the meeting

Anke Ra pushed Goffin's body towards Ikael.
To keep her halo from shaking, she had to muster her utmost

"Goffin made me an offer. To see who is worthy of becoming a god.
Betting everything on it."

His face, devoid of soul, emitted light only from his pupils, and she

"Ikael."

"Did I do something wrong?"
Hearing those words, Ikael felt like her mind was plummeting into an
abyss.
God does not make mistakes.
"...Anke Ra."
"The world simply exists."

"There is no right or wrong. I exist perfectly, and I maintain that

"My daughter, everything in this world is under my definition, but

"I understand."

"When I didn't know my existence, a powerful light exploded within

"At the moment of amplification, the world split into two. The

"Satan Lucifer. The antithesis of the law. Born at the same time as

Ikael's halo shook violently.
"Where is he now..."
"He's gone. He purified himself and disappeared in the other world.

"Lucifer made a deal with Goffin. To protect you, Ikael, Goffin

'So that's why he...'

'No.'
Even the first kiss that made her realize her feelings.
'It can't be!'
Even the countless interactions with Goffin during fierce battles with

"It wasn't... me."

What had she been expecting?
'How happy I was.'
When Goffin said he would stay in Heaven, her heart had swelled

"It was just an illusion, Ikael."
Anke Ra's nerve endings approached and wiped the tears from her
cheeks.
"The heart is just an illusion created by the system. Goffin was an

Ikael felt Anke Ra's touch.
"Because there was no need. You were simply infected by the error

"My God, I..."
"Do not fear. I have defined this world. And you are the beginning

If Anke Ra was the brain, Ikael was the thought.
"Yes, I couldn't swallow Hexa. But look. Even now, I control Goffin

"My beloved daughter. If I were deceiving you, I wouldn't be a god.

"This is right."
Anke Ra does not deceive.
Because he is cold.

"Did you meet Anke Ra? Why did this happen? What happened to

"Don't make me say it twice."

'I want to see him. No, even if I could just see his face. Just once,

Then she felt she could forget everything.
-The heart is an illusion.

'Everything is over.'
