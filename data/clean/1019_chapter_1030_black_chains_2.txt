Black Chains (2)

BOOOOM!

"Yaaah!"

–Krrrgh! How...

"Be careful. That's the pinnacle of a single-strike technique."
It resembled the zeta function.
Just as patterns in prime numbers are difficult to discern among

'This rhythm doesn't even exist in Hell. Even the gods would frown at

Beilouk, a sword technique that encapsulated every possible form into a
single linear slash.
'Approaching him recklessly will get us sliced instantly.'
It was a technique of time, not space, born not from talent but sheer
effort.
"Yaaah!"
Slashing in every direction, Rian moved forward without
hesitation.

Wherever Rian ran, hands rose up, swaying like a field of reeds.

"Don't go! You cannot leave!"

"Of all times... Damn it!" At that moment, chains from Gehenna burst

WOOOOO!

"Urghhh!"

If you are free,

"Ughhhh!"

–You hated Rai, didn't you? You wanted to kill him. Why was he given

The person Rian truly wanted to kill...

Something that any human could do.
