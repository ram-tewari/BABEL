Chapter 418:
Shirone's group headed towards the command headquarters barracks where
the battalion commander was, passing through the battlefield where bullets were flying.
Even though it was called the command headquarters, it was right in
front of the battlefield, so they had to shout to be heard.
Only after entering the barracks did the noise subside somewhat.
The company commander, who had already finished his report, was standing
at ease in a corner, and Kanya accompanied Shirone's group to facilitate smooth communication.
The battalion commander did not take his eyes off the strategic map even
after Shirone's group entered.
"I've heard the story."
He was a cold-looking man with white hair slicked back, and his thick
mustache made him look stubborn.
"Are you the Nephilim?"
Shirone did not deny it.
Regardless of the truth, it seemed more effective to use the rebels this
way.
The battalion commander moved on to the next question as if he wasn't
interested.
"I'll ask directly. Why did you come back?"
Shirone didn't understand the question.
"You are like a myth to the rebels. But myths are most beautiful
when they remain myths. Some of the soldiers think of you as an irresponsible deserter. Why did you, who
had already left the battlefield, come back here?"
Shirone instinctively knew that the battalion commander was one of those
who thought of him as a deserter.
"I have no grand reason. As before, I am just fighting with
conviction as a single magician."
"Conviction..."
The battalion commander pulled back the tent flap and looked at the
battlefield.
"The light you brought to Sector 73 is now the sun for 70,000
rebels. I don't want to extinguish that light. If you came here just for your conviction, I'd rather not
have met you."
Shirone realized that the battalion commander would not move.
What was in his cold eyes was not determination, but a sense of mission
driven by the belief that he had chosen the wrong path from the beginning.
"Even a small conviction can have the same purpose. I want to speak
directly with the commander. Please let me go to the rebel headquarters."
"I refuse."
The battalion commander's eyes were filled with undisguised
hostility.
"Fine, I'll be honest. The war is already lost. No, it was on the
verge of defeat. If it weren't for the sudden halt in Heaven's offensive, the war would already be over.
The gap was that overwhelming. Many soldiers lost their lives. The only card we have left is negotiation
with Heaven. If you plan to recklessly ignite the fire again and run away, the light of Sector 73 will
only be a hindrance."
"But the war is still ongoing. And Heaven is currently not moving.
This could be an opportunity. If we can accurately grasp the situation, we have a good
chance."
"Hey, light of Sector 73."
The battalion commander leaned in.
"Don't evaluate the Mecha tribe with the mindset of the Nor. It was
the Nor who started the rebellion in the first place. But now? Only our Mecha tribe is dying. The reason
Heaven stopped the battle? Who can find that out?"
"I can find out."
The battalion commander fell silent. His teeth ground together inside
his closed mouth.
"Listen well, kid. Do you think I don't know what you're trying to
do? No, actually, I don't know. But I can already see that whatever you do, you plan to use us. People
call you a myth, but to me, you're just a seed of disaster. A myth with no ability, who barely defeated
a fairy chief. Do you know how fiercely we fought while you ran away? What do you plan to change
now?"
"I will meet Ikael."
"Pfft..."
The battalion commander let out a breath.
"Puhahaha! Puhahaha!"
It was truly laughable.
The Archangel Ikael. Meeting the highest authority of Heaven's
law?
If it were that easy, tens of thousands of citizens wouldn't have
died.
"Now I know for sure. You're not a myth. You're a butcher who led
countless citizens to their deaths with your silver tongue. Company commander! Arrest this guy! Don't
let the soldiers know."
Just as the company commander was reluctantly about to follow the order,
the tent flap opened and the adjutant rushed in.
"Commander! Emergency! Giants have broken through the rearmost
defense line and are attacking."
"What? Why? Which idiot lured the giants here? What's the
reason?"
"It's not confirmed. Since Guroi Team 3 lost contact, the defense
line has been continuously breached..."
The battalion commander slammed the tactical table.
"Damn it. Why?"
It's rare for variables to arise in giant elimination operations. The
reactions of giants, who have no identity, are always the same.
But now the rearmost defense line was breached.
He didn't know where the law had gone wrong, but living the same day as
yesterday, he could only think of one thing.
The battalion commander grabbed Shirone by the collar.
"See? You're the root of all this. Because of you, Guroi was
destroyed, and now there's a tactical hole. Are you planning to annihilate our battalion

Kanya shouted with all her might.
"Commander! That's an unfounded accusation! Shirone
is..."
"Shut up! Does a pilot who couldn't even protect his own equipment

"Commander! Give us orders! If we leave it like this, we won't last
30 minutes and we'll be annihilated!"
The battalion commander put on the piper hanging on the wall. After
pocketing the arc, he grabbed the signa and exd and glared at Shirone as he left the barracks.
"Leave here quickly. I don't want to see you."
Kanya shouted.
"Commander! They can also be of help!"
"Didn't you hear me? Shirone is the main culprit who changed the

As the battalion commander left, Kanya had no choice but to
follow.
She couldn't just watch her comrades die as a soldier.
"Shirone, I'm sorry."
She gave a sad smile as she left the barracks.
"But I'm so glad to have met you."
Shirone knew it was her way of saying goodbye.
But Shirone still hadn't given up hope.

"The situation is worse than expected. Can we really move the

No one could.
In Heaven, where the Akashic Records materialize, the law is an
artificial system, but in the world where Shirone's group lives, it's just the principle of the
universe.
Just as a butterfly's wings can cause a storm, it's impossible to
pinpoint where the law changed because all minor variables are involved.
Gaold proposed his own method.
"Let's wipe out the giants first. If we save lives, they'll let us

"Leave that to me."
"No. It's too dangerous. You have a special mission. Don't think

"Do you have any guesses?"
"Yes. But think of it as an experiment rather than a guess. There's

The cockpit was destroyed, and the terrified pilot screamed. But even

"Damn it! Aaaah!"
The battalion commander, who arrived late, fired the arc repeatedly. It

Even so, the giant was hit.
Even so, the fact that it was unharmed made the battalion commander feel
miserable.
"Damn it! Damn it!"
From the beginning, they were beings that humans couldn't handle.
What did the elixir of life matter? What did it matter that their
lifespan was limited?
Humans lost the path to immortality for defying the gods. And now they
were facing extinction.
"Aaaah!"
When the battalion commander struck the giant's leg with the signa, the

Seeing the dilated pupils sent chills down his spine.
"Exterminate... the heretic."
His legs trembled, and he couldn't suppress the groan escaping between
his teeth.
"Hic. Hic."

He had witnessed many deaths, but his own death was incomparable to any
of them.
"Exterminate... the heretic."
"No, I'm not a heretic..."
When the giant's fist came down, the battalion commander screamed
silently and closed his eyes tightly.
Grrrr...

Hearing a tremendous low growl, he slowly opened his eyes to see a beast

Roar!

Her poor little sister. It was sad to send her off without experiencing
anything good in life.

Her smart sister must have known. The bodies of their comrades were all

Giants massacring humans looked no different from humans massacring

"Argh!"

The Mecha tribe was stunned.
Shirone's current appearance was exactly like the being that the

Giants were also beings of the law.
Even in a state of lost identity, Ikael's magic circle exerted a
