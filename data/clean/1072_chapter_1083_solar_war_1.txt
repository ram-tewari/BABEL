Solar War (1)
Eve of Battle.
The cities surrounding the Zeta Plateau, where the Solar War was set to
take place, lit up the night with endless fireworks.
Participation was open to all.Every user of High Gear was
converging on the plateau, hour by hour.
Anyone could fight.Even allies from the same nation could be
designated as enemies, promising chaos.
"Don't worry about the small fry."Once the battle
begins, individual desires are swept away by the collective will of the masses.
"The key is the Solar Palace, especially the low-gear zone. It'll
fall eventually, but the goal is to hold out as long as possible."
The Ascension Guild's first squad was in a tactical meeting at an annex
near the Solar Palace.
Beyond Ascension, the top five guilds of the Western Region Rankings had
each secluded themselves in their own quarters.
The operator spoke."Hold out for 24 hours, and we win. This
Solar War, as always, places Ascension Guild as the top-ranked Command Center. We'll deploy special task

"The eastern defense will be handled by Ogre. The west by Rubymix.

"Meeting adjourned. Feel free to enjoy yourselves until 3 a.m. Just

The eve-of-battle festival was one of the most anticipated events for
High Gear users.
There wasn't anything particularly unique about it, except the sheer
number of participants and the excitement brewing before the Solar War.
Barriers of the heart crumbled, and the collective euphoria made
indulgence effortless.
It was a night of revelry and ecstasy.
As the first squad of Ascension left to unwind, the Little Witch
remained seated.
Breaking the awkward silence, the operator asked,"Not going
to join the festival?"
"What about you? Aren't you the one everyone's waiting
for?"
"I've never been interested. Even if users are clamoring for me, my
freedom in High Gear includes not participating in the festival."
The Little Witch scrutinized the operator.Her golden bangs fell
over her eyes, concealing an alabaster face devoid of expression, yet hauntingly beautiful.
"I see."
"For many, this false world is an outlet for their desires. But for
you, it's the only reality, isn't it?"
"I don't know about that. I just... dislike trades I didn't agree
to."
"What did you talk about?"The Little Witch suddenly

"When you went to Moon Island, you met Yahweh2, didn't you? You

"Offer?"
"I invited him to live with me in High Gear. He turned me down. So,
I said my goodbye in my own way."
The operator glanced at the Little Witch and touched her lips

For a brief moment, a palpable tension emanated from the Little Witch,
but it was likely a trick of the mind.
The operator asked,"Why? Does it bother you? Are you going to
retaliate?"
"Pfft!"The Little Witch chuckled, her shoulders
shaking.
"What does that even mean? It wasn't a real kiss. This place is

right now?"
The Empress of Kashan always knew how to provoke others.
"...You're right."A fake gesture of affection.
"Yahweh2 probably doesn't feel anything either. His only goal is to
defeat me in a duel and pull me into reality. But that's why he can't beat me."
"I agree."
Woorin had also discerned the core issue.
"Shirone, unless you understand the significance of that kiss,

Without that realization, neither mining the Apocalypse nor eradicating
the demonic realm in reality would ever happen.
Ultimately...
"He'll come to me."
The Solar Palace sky erupted with a brilliant fireworks display.
Flashes of light lit up the windows.
"So pretty," Death Princess murmured, gazing out at the
spectacle.
Meanwhile, Golden Wheel's first squad wrapped up their meeting.
Fermi addressed them."The key is to keep Yahweh2 alive until

"Understood."

But there was one person who could challenge him."Let's

Everyone in Golden Wheel's first squad turned, including the group by

Fermi asked,"Why?"
The fact that he seriously inquired showed even Fermi sensed something
amiss.
"I don't think we can beat the Operator," Shirone said.
A tense silence fell.
Wild West, the squad member in charge of leveling up, adjusted his

Fermi considered this for a moment."If that's how you feel,

Shirone laid out his honest thoughts and the changes he proposed.
Fermi couldn't refute him.
"Long live the East! Long live the East!""The West

break.
