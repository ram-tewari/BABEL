'He withstood it?'

If the atmospheric pressure was enough to buckle Ymir's knees, the city

It meant that the Great Star was not in normal condition.
Shirone approached her.
"Are you okay?"

"Don't worry. A star won't be destroyed by this level of impact.
It's fine."
Shirone knew this too, but it was also true that her suffering was

"Listen carefully. The reason I came here is to seal

He was a being whose strength couldn't be completely suppressed even by

"Shirone, give me the key to Hexa Planet."

Five Great Stars of the Integrated Universe Management Department.

What she was attempting now was literally using stars and planets as a
pentagram.
"These insignificant things keep gathering."
Ymir, who had been listening from the pit, clenched his fist and floated

"You intend to seal me?"
"Yes. Unfortunately, your existence is meaningless in this
universe. If you didn't affect anything, it would be different, but since you've taken action, we have

Ymir slowly raised his knee.
"Is that so?"
The next moment he stomped down hard on the ground, sending an immense
shock into the Great Star's incarnation.
"Ugh!"
"Great Star!"
As her companions hurriedly supported her, Ymir looked down at the
ground he had stomped on.
"Hmm, quite solid."
Despite intending to collapse everything around him with that stomp,

"I'll give you some credit. But do you think that's enough to stop
me?"
Even 'Law Killer' couldn't find a way to annihilate his existence
completely.
"It can be done."
The Great Star hooked five pendants onto her fingers and brought her
hands together as if in prayer.
"Stop Ymir for a moment."
Though those gathered here were unprecedentedly strong individuals in

move himself.
"This is an interesting game. I'll gladly play along. But I need

"The game ends when I kill you."
"Stop him!"
Shirone's group moved simultaneously. The Hand of God combined with Sun

'The Great Star is preventing planetary destruction.'
Seeing her suffer was truly painful but...

Steel magic engraved on his sword,
"I'll chop off an arm at least."

Not quite the realm of breaking, but Ymir had already absorbed enough of

The thrilling feel of his hand struck Freed's mind sharply.

"That tickles."

The balance of the world.

Ataraxia - Physical Realm.

"Ugh!"

If you wish to do it, just do it.
