Chapter 15:
First Step Toward the Dream (7)
Shirone led his parents to the reception room. Having cried his eyes out since the front
gate, the three of them walked the hallway with puffy eyes.
It would take time to compose themselves. Whether the meeting would be prolonged or not
was up to Clump's consideration, but Shirone would have plenty of time to reunite with his parents in the
reception room.
"Let me see, my son. Your face has weathered much since I last saw you. You've
suffered. Truly suffered."
Tears welled up in Vincent's eyes again. Though Shirone looked healthier than before, to
his parents, it only seemed like he hadn't eaten or slept properly.
"I haven't suffered. I had fun reading books and passing the time. How have you
been, Father?"
"Of course! I ate well and lived well. Look how plump your mother has gotten. We
almost had another child because she became so pretty. Hahaha!"
"Dear, what nonsense are you spouting in front of our son?"
Shirone smiled sadly. After a year and six months apart, his parents looked much thinner.
No doubt they had worried about him every day, unable to eat properly. The thought made
his heart ache again.
Vincent suddenly grew curious. At first, he had been frantic, thinking something had
happened to his child, but seeing Shirone so composed, it didn't seem like anything bad had occurred.
"So, why did you call us here? If your work is done, you could have just come home.
Don't tell me they're trying to keep you here against your will?"
