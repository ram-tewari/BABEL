Kuan stomped the ground with his foot. Then, as he lifted one leg, his body tilted toward

The cadets stared in disbelief. Without ropes tied around his waist, maintaining such a
posture should have been impossible for a human.
"The amplified force from schema maximizes the shift in the center of gravity. Right now,

Kuan's body snapped upright like a roly-poly toy.
"With external gravity, the possibilities of movement become limitless. For example, this

Kuan stomped the ground again and leaned backward until his back nearly touched the
ground. His body rotated in a conical motion.
"External gravity isn't actual gravity. It's just an extreme form of inertia. So, if you
apply rotation to external gravity, your body will rotate too."
As Kuan extended his sword outward, the rotation slowed. But as the tilt corrected
itself, centrifugal force accelerated the spin.
Whoosh—!
Kuan, now standing vertically, spun at an incredible speed before stopping abruptly in a
poised stance.
The cadets stared in awe. If they had been in his place, their ankles would have been
sliced off first, followed by their bodies being shredded into dozens of pieces by the accelerating blade.
Defense was impossible. Just as humans couldn't stop a tornado caused by atmospheric
pressure differences, this explosive external gravity was akin to a rotating blade machine.
'This is the Death Magician.'
A nickname given because his opponents died without even understanding how.

"Whoa!"

"External gravity ultimately uses inertial force to cancel gravity. So, right now,
gravity isn't acting on my body."
Kuan walking on the ceiling was beyond wondrous—it was bizarre. But this wasn't magic.
Even circus performers could run along walls without schema. Kuan simply maximized
inertia with stronger and more precise force.
