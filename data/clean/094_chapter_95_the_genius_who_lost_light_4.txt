Chapter 95:
The Genius Who Lost Light (4)
Arkane knew someone had invaded his dungeon last night. But he didn't care. Countless
thieves targeted a Grand Mage's dungeon. By now, they'd likely been killed by monsters or traps.
Dismissing the thought, Arkane focused on his research. His recent interest was dark
magic's effect on memory. As he scribbled notes, his hand suddenly stopped. He removed his glasses and
looked toward the door. Mechanisms were being disarmed at an alarming speed. This was no ordinary intruder.
"Did I catch a big one today?"
For Arkane, who could hole up for years, dungeon intruders were entertainment. Today's
catch seemed particularly interesting.
With a thud, the iron door opened. Arkane checked his watch. Exactly 32 hours, 28
minutes, and 5 seconds since entry. The time taken to disarm every trap and reach this point.
