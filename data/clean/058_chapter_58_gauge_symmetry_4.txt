Chapter 58:
Gauge Symmetry (4)
Armin recalled those days.
"Losing sight is like losing space. Without space, time disappears too. In the darkness,
I realized: I had already transcended time. Thus, Stop magic was born."
Shirone's heart raced. He was hearing the secrets of Stop magic, a feat few had achieved.
"Don't be too impressed. It's not transcendent. It only works within Spirit Zone's range
and has many limitations."
Armin looked at the frozen Siena.
"She's a good sister. Tough on the outside, tender inside. Do you agree?"
"Haha! I'm not sure about tender, but she's definitely great."
"How does she look to you now? Is my sister beautiful?"
"O-of course. She's popular at school."
"Then what if? In stopped time, could we do something… inappropriate?"
Understanding, Shirone blushed.
"What are you saying? You're not suggesting…"
"Don't worry. It's impossible. Our mass is near zero. Without mass, we'd disintegrate.
The point is acceleration. That's why Stop is called the realm of light-speed."
"That's why Neid felt solid. No mass means even clothes can't be wrinkled."
"Exactly. We're infinitely fragile. But no one can touch us either. That's Stop's true
purpose. You asked how I knew you'd notice? Simple. I've had the same realization as you."
"The same realization… You mean?"
"Yes. The Immortal Function."
Shirone's heart pounded. Here was someone who had experienced the Immortal Function
firsthand—a top-tier time mage.
"The Immortal Function is the realization of essence. Since essence is singular, our
wavelengths resonate. Some call it the eerie sensation."
"What is the Immortal Function?"
"The gateway to infinity. You've opened it. In magic society, such individuals are called
Unlockers."
After a pause, Armin asked.
"When you opened the Immortal Function, did you feel your ego dissolving?"
"Yes! I thought I was disappearing."
"You would have, if it continued. When the mind transcends numbers, Spirit Zone expands
infinitely. But humans are finite. So Unlockers use their unique Spirit Zone to contain infinity."
Shirone grimaced.
"Then what should I do? Even top mages are cautious, but I knew nothing. Now I have
nightmares and feel that eerie sensation alone."
