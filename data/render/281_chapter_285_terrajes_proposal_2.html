So you can't accept me?
Uorin scratched her cheek with an apologetic expression.
Uorin
I'd like to help, but there are many daughters of Teraje in numerous kingdoms who are my rivals.
I am particularly young and do not want to stand out yet.
Uorin
And Kazra isn't exactly a strong foundation either.
There's no need to draw attention and become a target for my older sisters, right?
Shirone
Fourteen years old. That was the age of the girl sitting in front of him.
Shirone
'You're really something, Uorin.'
He also realized anew how ridiculous his own behavior had been a few days ago.
Even now that he understood the reality of the royal palace, it was chilling.
Shirone
What kind of guts did he have before to try to deal with a woman like this?
Fine. If there's no room for negotiation, I'll leave. I need to find Amy.
Shirone
Shirone stood up as if there was no need to listen further.
If his assumption was wrong, everything would spiral into the worst-case scenario, but there was nothing he could do.
Shirone
Because there are no fools in the royal palace who miss the time to act no matter how great the danger.
I wish you luck.
Hearing Uorin's parting words made his heart drop.
But he walked towards the door without showing it.
Shirone
He must not show hesitation or fear. He had to truly let go of everything and leave this room.
Ataraxia is not needed. Is that really so?
Perhaps to Uorin, it didn't matter much.
But even if it was just a sliver of gain, people always found it hard to give away even the smallest advantage to someone else.
Shirone walked, holding on to a small hope as if it were his lifeline.
Even as his hand reached for the doorknob and began to turn it, Uorin did not respond.
The door opened, and Shirone clenched his eyes shut.
This was the end of everything.
Shirone
But if perhaps...
Shirone's steps came to an abrupt halt.
Shirone
If I were to make the maximum concession... that is, if we consider it as borrowing against Oppa's future...
Shirone slowly turned to look back at Uorin.
Shirone
You could earn at least an opportunity to negotiate with the daughter of Teraje.
Uorin
An opportunity to negotiate?
Even if he pledged Ataraxia as collateral, all he would gain was the chance to sit at the negotiation table.
Shirone
Yes. A negotiation. I can't just help you for nothing.
Like I said earlier, I don't want to put myself in a dangerous position unnecessarily.
Shirone
There's one condition. It shouldn't be a particularly difficult condition, but anyway, if you agree to this condition, I'll be on your side.
Shirone raised his chin as if to say, 'Go ahead, tell me'.
Uorin
Tonight, spend the night with me.
Shirone
Wha- What?
It was absurd for a condition put forward at the end of such an intense psychological battle.
What reason could there be for him to spend the night with her?
Uorin
Don't worry. I didn't mean it that way. Just stay with me tonight.
Well... if you happen to have other ideas, I don't mind if you try.
Shirone
What on earth are you saying? You're my half-sister.
Uorin
Well, that's something we'll only know after the paternity test is done.
And honestly, it doesn't matter.
Uorin
Same-sex marriage is common for royalty who bring a whole kingdom as a dowry.
Shirone
Why on earth? What does that condition have to do with helping me?
Uorin
The daughters of Teraje Family have always been the ones to ascend to the emperor's throne.
It might sound strange, but for us, men are merely tools to be consumed according to their use.
Uorin
That's why a daughter of Teraje doesn't keep a man who has another woman in his heart by her side.
If Oppa is thinking of Amy, then I cannot accept you either.
Shirone
I understand what you're saying. But I don't have Amy in my heart.
Uorin
I think Zion Oppa is interested in her.
Shirone
Something hot welled up in Shirone's chest.
Shirone
Of course, if she rejects him, nothing will happen.
Despite how he seems, Oppa has too much pride to force himself on a woman who turns him down.
Uorin
Well then, let's do this. I just want to confirm your feelings.
From this moment on, I'll guarantee the safety of Amy and everyone else.
Uorin
I could send people right now and give the order.
All you need to do is spend the night with me.
Shirone
Shirone didn't know what kind of threat Amy was under that made her follow Zion, but if he accepted Uorin's offer, Amy could be saved.
Shirone
Zion… Where did he take Amy?
I can't reveal that yet, not until you've made your decision.
Uorin
It's very simple. You just need to trust me.
Show me that I take precedence over that woman.
Shirone
A trembling that started at his fingertips spread throughout his body.
Uorin
Why are you hesitating? I'm Uorin of Teraje.
Not only do I not lie, but isn't this proposal what you wanted?
Uorin
If you can prove that you don't have feelings for another woman, I'll support you wholeheartedly.
I'll spare no resources to help you grow your abilities, and when I become empress, you could become the greatest mage in the world.
Shirone
It was truly a dream-like story for Shirone.
Shirone
And she had the ability to turn that dream into reality.
Even archmages are writing resumes wanting to work under Uorin.
Shirone
Such a huge opportunity was lying right before his eyes.
Shirone
Lost in thought, Shirone finally made his decision.
Shirone
I'm going to Amy. I won't be accepting your proposal.
Uorin sighed, as though frustrated.
Uorin
Are you throwing away the incredible opportunity to form a connection with Teraje?
No, more than that, you could protect the family you hold so dear.
Uorin
And yet, you're willing to throw it all away over a woman?
Shirone
That's right. That's what I intend to do.
Uorin closed her mouth as if shocked.
Uorin
But Uorin, for humans, that's everything.
The only world that exists is the one we see and feel.
Uorin
Even if you truly save Amy, if I can't see it for myself, then it's just an unrealized world.
I can't leave Amy in visible danger while trusting in some invisible safety.
Shirone
That's why I have to go to Amy.
Sadness briefly flickered in Uorin's eyes.
Uorin
You make life so hard for yourself.
It would be easy if you just made a choice.
Shirone
Shirone smiled bitterly.
Shirone
He often thought the same thing himself.
Shirone
But that's precisely the shackle of intellect. Once you know something, you can't act as if you don't.
I understand how great this opportunity was.
Shirone
I also know how much you, the heir to the emperor, conceded to help me.
I'm not doubting you at all.
Shirone
But I have to go.
Uorin walked up to Shirone and hugged him, burying her face against his chest.
Uorin
You're such a fool, Oppa.
I'm sorry I couldn't do anything for you.
Uorin
Personally, I really liked you.
