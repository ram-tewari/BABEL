079 Chapter 80 The Dark Intruders 4
Ethella remained in the training hall, wearing the same outfit as yesterday.
Her eyebrows twitched as she sat cross-legged without moving a muscle.
Her forehead wrinkled, and her nose bridge twisted.
The veins on her neck bulged as she gritted her teeth, and her body began to shake violently.
The building shook along with Ethella's body. The walls and ceiling vibrated, sending dust raining down.
Finally, her eyes snapped open, and she exhaled a rough breath before collapsing forward.
Ethella
Hah! Hah!
Ethella, who had been battling a mind demon since dawn, was drenched in cold sweat.
Ethella
What was that?
She couldn't pinpoint it exactly, but it was a type that infiltrated human minds and blocked memories.
Dark magic belonged to the orthodox school, but the malice embedded in the magic was intense enough to push Ethella's vigilance to the extreme.
Had it not been for the laws of the Karsis Diocese, she too would have lost her memories without a fight.
Ethella
It's dark magic. But it's strictly forbidden within the school.
Ethella checked the time.
Though it was summer and still bright, it was past the time when school classes would have ended.
Ethella
Does that mean I was trapped by the mind demon for over 12 hours?
Ethella
Considering Ethella's supreme mental strength, it was an incredibly powerful magic.
Ethella
What was even more astonishing was that no one had come looking for her despite her skipping classes entirely.
Ethella
She wasn't the only one affected.
Once that thought crossed her mind, Ethella rushed out of the training hall without even changing her clothes.
A flash of light passed over the magic school's sky.
Then, as if drawn by a magnet, it curved toward the ground and plummeted.
Without even a sound of impact, a single man landed.
Sade
Dammit! Am I too late?
Sade, who had ridden a carriage all night to reach Creas, used spatial teleportation to arrive at the main gate as soon as he entered.
As expected, the guards stood motionless, showing no reaction.
It was dark magic.
And an extremely high-level one at that.
Sade
I need to find the master first.
Sade turned his steps toward the faculty quarters.
Ethella
Teacher Ethella! What's the situation?
Sade
Teacher Ethella! What's the situation?
Sade, who had just returned from a trip, asked as if he already knew about the incident at the school, making Ethella puzzled.
But since every second counted, she cut straight to the point.
Ethella
It seems we've fallen victim to dark magic. Everyone—students and teachers—has lost their memories.
Sade
Sade had expected it, but facing the reality was still shocking.
No matter how insane, launching a surprise attack on Alpheas Magic School, one of the kingdom's top five prestigious schools, was unthinkable.
Sade
There's no time. First, tell me what's happening.
Ethella
Scanning with the Spirit Zone, the entire school is heading up the mountain. Judging by the direction, it seems to be toward the Uncrossable Bridge.
Ethella
There are three escorts, and three others are tailing them. Among the teachers… it seems I'm the only one who managed to escape.
Sade
Arcane's dark magic was at the level of a grand mage, but Ethella was also a bishop of the Karsis Diocese, so he couldn't have dominated her mind.
Sade
That alone was a relief, Sade thought as he explained what he knew.
Sade
Let me brief you quickly. The one behind this is Viltor Arcane. A grand mage who threw the world into chaos 40 years ago.
Ethella
I've heard of him. Why would such a person target the magic school…?
Sade
Sade bit his lip.
Sade
It was related to the personal history of his master, Alpheas.
Sade
But the only person he could rely on now was Ethella, and given her character, she would accept the truth without prejudice.
Sade
Viltor Arcane… is the headmaster's master.
Ethella
Ethella blinked.
But as Sade expected, she didn't imagine anything beyond what she'd heard.
Ethella
I see. But Arcane wasn't among the ones leading the students. What's the situation?
Sade
We can't. If you're affected by mental magic, it's not easy to break free. Trying to save the students one by one will only backfire.
Ethella
But we can't just leave them like this.
Sade
You mentioned three people are tailing them. Do you know who they are?
Ethella
I couldn't confirm exactly… but it might be Shirone.
Sade
Shirone?
Sade
Hearing Ethella's words, Sade also remembered.
Sade
Arcane's magic was likely cast around 24 hours ago.
Sade
The only students who could have been outside the school at that time were Shirone and his group, who were on suspension.
Ethella
That's probably correct. If it's them, they'll be fine for now. They're smart kids, so they'll know what to do.
Sade
Let's leave it to them for now, and Teacher Ethella, please find Arcane.
Ethella
What will you do, Teacher Sade?
Sade
I'll look for the headmaster. Finding the master and dispelling the dark magic is the priority.
Ethella
Understood. Then I'll find Arcane.
Sade looked around the mountain ridge.
The school grounds were so vast that he had no idea where Arcane might be.
Sade
Hundreds of people were affected simultaneously. A magic of this scale would have drained a significant amount of mental energy.
Sade
He's likely hiding in a secluded place. The mountain is the best place to search.
Ethella
How far does your Spirit Zone extend in Jonah Mode?
Sade
Hmm, roughly a diameter of two kilometers.
Sade
Sade nodded after a rough calculation.
Sade
Marking about five points should suffice. Patrol around the training grounds. I'll head to the school.
Ethella
Alright. I'll capture Arcane and then save the students.
Sade
Don't push yourself too hard. Arcane is…
Sade
Sade swallowed his words.
Sade
Telling her how strong he was would only fuel her sense of duty.
Sade
No. I leave it to you.
Feeling the responsibility as Alpheas's disciple, Sade bowed his head on behalf of his master.
Ethella
I don't know the details, but I'm also a teacher at this school. I'll do my best for the students.
Sade
Yes. Then…
From now on, it was a race against time.
As Sade teleported toward the school, Ethella took a deep breath and focused her mind.
Her sequence formula rapidly ascended, expanding her Spirit Zone.
The unique expansion type exclusive to Jonah unfolded, allowing her to sense everything within a two-kilometer radius through synesthesia.
Ethella
First, the 26th Training Ground.
Ethella's body transformed into light as she flew toward the mountain peak.
With a single teleportation, she covered one kilometer.
The Mage's Battle (1)
Canis and his group gathered the memory-blocked people at the starting point of the Uncrossable Bridge.
Arin's face was pale after completing her task.
Even though they were under Abyss Nova, mentally controlling hundreds of people to move was an exhausting ordeal.
Neide
Why here of all places? There's just a bridge and a cliff.
Iruki
No idea. Maybe they're waiting for someone?
